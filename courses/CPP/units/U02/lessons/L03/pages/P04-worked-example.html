Worked Example

💡Worked Example

Scenario: Maya is comparing two careers she likes: dental assistant and graphic designer.

Step 1: Maya looks up the demand and growth trend for both careers.

Step 2: Maya records the salary range information for each role.

Step 3: Maya compares the information in a clear way:

  • Which career appears to have stronger demand
  • Which career appears to be growing faster
  • How the salary information compares

Step 4: Maya combines this research with what she already knows about her interests and goals.

This example shows that salary and outlook data are helpful when students compare career options, but they should be used with other information too.
