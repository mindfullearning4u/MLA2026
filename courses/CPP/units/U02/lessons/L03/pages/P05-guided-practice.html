Guided Practice

Choose two careers you want to compare.

Write responses to all of the following:

  • Name both careers.
  • Describe the job outlook for each career in a short sentence.
  • Describe the salary information for each career in a short sentence.
  • Explain which career seems stronger based on the data and why.
  • Explain whether the career data changes your thinking about either option.

Use complete sentences. Focus on demand, growth trends, and earning potential.
