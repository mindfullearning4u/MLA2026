Guided Practice

Choose one career that interests you and focus only on the education and training pathway for that career.

Write responses to all of the following:

  • Name the career.
  • State whether the career may involve a degree, certification, technical training, or more than one pathway.
  • Describe what a student would need to do to begin preparing for that pathway.
  • Explain whether the pathway seems realistic or appealing to you and why.

Use complete sentences. Focus on pathway research rather than job duties.
