💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Elena is interested in automotive careers and wants to understand how people prepare for them.

Step 1: Elena chooses one role to research first: automotive technician.

Step 2: Elena identifies the pathway information to collect:

  • Whether a degree is required
  • Whether technical training is useful or required
  • Whether any certification is expected

Step 3: Elena records the findings clearly:

  • Degree: may not be the main requirement for entry
  • Technical training: important for learning hands-on repair skills
  • Certification: may strengthen job readiness and show specific knowledge

Step 4: Elena compares this pathway to other automotive careers before making a decision.

This example shows that pathway research helps students understand what preparation is needed before choosing a career direction.
