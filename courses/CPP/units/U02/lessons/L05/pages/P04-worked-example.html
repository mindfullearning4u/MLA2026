💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Taylor is deciding between two careers: respiratory therapist and video editor.

Step 1: Taylor reviews the job role and responsibilities of both options.

Step 2: Taylor reviews the pathway requirements for both careers.

Step 3: Taylor adds outlook and salary information.

Step 4: Taylor compares the evidence with personal interests, values, and work preferences.

Step 5: Taylor writes one clear decision statement and supports it with several reasons.

This example shows how the unit skills work together in one career decision scenario.
