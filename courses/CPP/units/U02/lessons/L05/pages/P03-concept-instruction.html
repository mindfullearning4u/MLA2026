Assessment Readiness

This lesson brings together the work from Unit 02. Students should now be able to research job information, identify education and training pathways, compare outlook and salary data, and evaluate career choices using evidence.

Before completing the checkpoint, review these questions:

  • What are the job roles and responsibilities of each option?
  • What kind of education or training pathway is connected to each option?
  • What does the outlook or salary information suggest?
  • Which option seems strongest based on the evidence you collected?

This readiness lesson is designed to help students organize thinking before the unit assessment. The goal is not to learn a new term. The goal is to use what has already been learned in a clear and supported way.
