Guided Practice

Use one career decision scenario of your own. Compare at least two careers you have researched in this unit.

Write responses to all of the following:

  • Name the two careers you are comparing.
  • Summarize the job role of each one.
  • Summarize the pathway requirement of each one.
  • Summarize outlook or salary information for each one.
  • State which career you would recommend for yourself right now and explain why.

Use complete sentences and support your answer with evidence from the unit.
