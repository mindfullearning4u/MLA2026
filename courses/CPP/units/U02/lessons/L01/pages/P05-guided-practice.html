Guided Practice

Choose one career that interests you. It may come from a cluster you explored earlier or from a job you already know about.

Write responses to all of the following:

  • Name the career you chose.
  • Describe the main job role in one sentence.
  • List at least three responsibilities connected to the career.
  • List at least two requirements for entering or preparing for the career.
  • Explain whether the career still seems worth exploring and why.

Use complete sentences. Keep your focus on clear research information, not guesses.
