🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Evaluating Career Choices
This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare two career options and justify an informed choice.

Important: Use two careers of your choice for this checkpoint.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The names of the two careers you selected.
  • At least one advantage of each career.
  • At least one challenge or limitation of each career.
  • A clear statement of which career seems stronger for you right now.
  • At least two pieces of evidence supporting your choice.

If your response does not include comparison and evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.
