Checkpoint Assignment

Required: Submit your completed Lesson 4 checkpoint.

Your Teacher of Record will review your work for clear comparison of options, use of evidence, and reasonable explanation of your decision.

Include all of the following in your submission:

  • The two careers you selected
  • One advantage of each career
  • One challenge or limitation of each career
  • Your current best choice
  • At least two pieces of evidence supporting that choice

Accepted submission formats: jpg, png, pdf

Important: Make sure your work is readable before you submit it.
