Worked Example

💡Worked Example

Scenario: Devin is comparing two careers: elementary teacher and web developer.

Step 1: Devin reviews earlier research on job duties, pathway requirements, and outlook information.

Step 2: Devin adds personal reflection:

  • Likes working with people
  • Enjoys solving problems
  • Values creativity and steady growth opportunities

Step 3: Devin compares the two careers side by side using the evidence.

Step 4: Devin writes one decision statement and explains it with reasons.

This example shows that evaluating career choices means using both research and self-knowledge to support a decision.
