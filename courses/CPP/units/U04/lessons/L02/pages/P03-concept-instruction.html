Concept Instruction

A resume is easier to understand when it is clearly organized. Good structure helps a reader quickly find important information.

Common resume sections may include:

  • Contact information
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Experience, activities, or achievements

Formatting matters because it affects readability. Clear headings, consistent spacing, and organized sections help make a resume look professional.

The purpose of resume structure is not decoration. It is to help important information stand out clearly.
