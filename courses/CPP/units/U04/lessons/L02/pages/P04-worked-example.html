💡 Worked Example

Scenario: A student wants to build a first resume for a volunteer program application.

Step 1: Start with contact information.

Step 2: Add an education section with school information.

Step 3: Add a skills section and list relevant strengths.

Step 4: Add activities, service, or experience that show responsibility.

Step 5: Use clear section headings and consistent formatting.

This example shows that even a first resume can be strong when the structure is organized and clear.
