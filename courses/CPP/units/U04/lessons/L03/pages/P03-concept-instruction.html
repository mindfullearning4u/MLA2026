Concept Instruction

Writing a resume means turning personal information, skills, and experiences into a clear professional document. Students must choose what information is relevant and place it in the right section.

When writing a resume, students should:

  • Use clear section headings
  • Choose information that supports the purpose of the resume
  • Keep wording concise and professional
  • Make the document easy to read

A resume does not need to include everything a student has ever done. It should include the information that best supports the current goal.

Resume writing improves when students draft, review, and revise.
