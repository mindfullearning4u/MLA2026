💡 Worked Example

Scenario: A student is creating a first resume for a school mentorship application.

Step 1: Write basic contact information and school information.

Step 2: Add a short skills section using clear, relevant wording.

Step 3: Add activities or service that show responsibility, teamwork, or leadership.

Step 4: Review the resume and remove information that does not support the application.

This example shows that resume writing is a process of selecting and organizing the strongest information.
