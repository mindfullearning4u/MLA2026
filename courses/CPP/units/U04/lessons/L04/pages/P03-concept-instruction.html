Concept Instruction

A cover letter and an application often work together. The cover letter explains interest and qualifications. The application collects the required information in a formal way.

Strong professional documents should be:

  • Clear and respectful
  • Focused on the purpose of the opportunity
  • Complete and accurate
  • Submitted in a professional format

Students should pay attention to tone, completeness, and accuracy. A strong application shows that the student can follow directions and communicate professionally.

Cover letters and applications are part of presenting yourself responsibly.
