💡 Worked Example

Scenario: A student is submitting materials for a student ambassador program.

Step 1: Review the resume for section organization and clarity.

Step 2: Review the cover letter or application writing for formal tone.

Step 3: Check that all required documents are complete.

Step 4: Revise any part that is unclear or incomplete before submission.

This example shows that strong document submission includes drafting, reviewing, and refining.
