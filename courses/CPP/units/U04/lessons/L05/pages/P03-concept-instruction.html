Assessment Readiness

This lesson brings together the work from Unit 04. Students should now be able to use formal communication, organize resume sections, draft resume content, and prepare application materials clearly.

Before completing the checkpoint, review these questions:

  • Does the resume include the needed sections?
  • Is the language clear and professional?
  • Does the application response match the purpose of the opportunity?
  • Are the documents complete and ready to submit?

This readiness lesson is designed to help students organize and refine professional documents before the unit assessment.
