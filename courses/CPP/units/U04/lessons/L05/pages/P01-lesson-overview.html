Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Resume and Application Submission

This lesson prepares students for the Unit 04 assessment by combining professional communication, resume structure, resume drafting, and application writing.

In this lesson, students organize professional documents and review them for completeness, clarity, and professionalism. This is an application lesson, not a new-concept lesson.

Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook and checkpoint work as evidence of readiness for the unit assessment.
