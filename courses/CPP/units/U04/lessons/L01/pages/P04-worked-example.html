💡 Worked Example

Scenario: A student needs to email a teacher about missing a meeting for a school leadership project.

Weak message: "hey i missed it my bad can we do it later?"

Step 1: Use a respectful greeting.

Step 2: Explain the situation clearly and briefly.

Step 3: Use a formal tone and polite request.

Stronger message: "Hello, I am sorry I missed the meeting today. I had a scheduling conflict. Would it be possible to discuss the next steps at another time? Thank you."

This example shows how tone and clarity can make communication more professional.
