Unit 03 | Lesson 4

Concept Instruction

Plans do not always go exactly as expected. Students may face barriers such as time limits, academic difficulty, changing interests, or other challenges. Strong planning includes flexibility.

Important ideas in this lesson:

  • Barrier: something that creates difficulty
  • Adjustment: a change made to keep the plan useful
  • Adaptability: the ability to respond to change without giving up

Adjusting a plan does not mean a student has failed. It means the student is using problem-solving to stay focused on the larger goal.

Flexible planning helps students continue making progress when situations change.
