Lesson Overview

This lesson prepares students for the Unit 03 assessment by combining SMART goals, timelines, academic planning, and plan adjustment strategies.

In this lesson, students use one planning scenario to organize a clear career plan, explain the steps, and show how the plan can adapt when needed. This is an application lesson, not a new-concept lesson.

Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook and checkpoint work as evidence of readiness for the unit assessment.
