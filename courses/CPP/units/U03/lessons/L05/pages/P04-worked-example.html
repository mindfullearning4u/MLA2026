💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Sofia wants to pursue a career in digital media and is building a career plan.

Step 1: Sofia writes one SMART goal connected to improving media and communication skills.

Step 2: Sofia adds a timeline with short-term steps for this semester and next year.

Step 3: Sofia includes academic planning choices that support the goal.

Step 4: Sofia identifies one possible barrier and explains how the plan could adjust if needed.

Step 5: Sofia checks that the full plan is organized, realistic, and connected to the larger goal.

This example shows how Unit 03 skills combine into one structured career plan.
