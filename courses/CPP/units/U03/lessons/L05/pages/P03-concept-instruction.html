Assessment Readiness

This lesson brings together the work from Unit 03. Students should now be able to write SMART goals, create timelines, connect academic choices to career goals, and adjust plans when barriers appear.

Before completing the checkpoint, review these questions:

  • What is the main goal in the plan?
  • What short-term steps support the goal?
  • How do school choices connect to the goal?
  • What barrier could appear, and how could the plan adjust?

This readiness lesson is designed to help students organize a complete plan before the unit assessment. The goal is to apply unit learning in one structured response.
