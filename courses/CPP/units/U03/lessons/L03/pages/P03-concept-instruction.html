Concept Instruction

Career planning is not only about future jobs. It also includes decisions students make in school right now. Academic planning helps students choose classes, activities, and preparation steps that support future opportunities.

Important academic planning ideas:

  • Course selection: choosing classes that build needed knowledge or skills
  • Preparation strategy: taking steps now to support later goals
  • Alignment: making sure school choices connect to a future direction

Academic planning does not require a perfect final answer. It helps students make useful decisions with the information they have now.

Strong academic planning keeps current school choices connected to long-term goals.
