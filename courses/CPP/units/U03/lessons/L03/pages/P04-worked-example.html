💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Alexis is interested in healthcare and wants to plan high school choices more carefully.

Step 1: Alexis identifies a possible career direction in healthcare.

Step 2: Alexis thinks about classes and preparation strategies that may help, such as science coursework, strong study habits, and relevant school activities.

Step 3: Alexis writes a simple academic plan that connects current choices to future preparation.

Step 4: Alexis reviews the plan to make sure the choices match the goal.

This example shows how academic planning turns a broad career idea into practical school choices.
