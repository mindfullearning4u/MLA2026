Unit 03 Lesson 1 - Setting SMART Goals

Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Specific: the goal clearly states what will be done.
  • Measurable: the goal can be checked or tracked.
  • Achievable: the goal is realistic.
  • Relevant: the goal connects to an important need or future plan.
  • Time-bound: the goal includes a deadline or time frame.
  • Key idea: SMART goals help students turn ideas into realistic action steps.
