🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Short-Term and Long-Term Planning
This checkpoint verifies your ability to connect short-term steps to a long-term goal using a timeline.

Important: Use one school or career goal for this checkpoint.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • One long-term goal.
  • At least three short-term steps.
  • A simple timeline showing the order of the steps.
  • One sentence explaining how the short-term steps support the long-term goal.

If your response does not clearly include both short-term and long-term planning, the checkpoint is not complete.
