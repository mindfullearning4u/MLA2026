💡Worked Example

Scenario: Marcus is interested in engineering and wants to plan ahead.

Short-term steps:

  • Improve performance in math this semester
  • Join a school engineering or robotics activity
  • Talk with a counselor about future course choices

Long-term goals:

  • Take advanced math and science courses in later grades
  • Research engineering majors or technical programs
  • Prepare for a pathway connected to engineering

Timeline idea: Marcus lists what should happen this semester, next school year, and later in high school.

This example shows how short-term and long-term planning work together.
