Lesson 4: Presenting a Career Portfolio

In this lesson, you will learn how to organize and deliver a presentation about your career portfolio.

A portfolio presentation should explain what is included, why it matters, and how the portfolio shows readiness. The goal is not only to show artifacts, but also to explain them clearly and professionally.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to plan a portfolio presentation with clear structure and purpose.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify the parts of a portfolio presentation
  • Explain how to organize a presentation logically
  • Connect artifacts to strengths and readiness
  • Prepare to deliver a clear portfolio presentation
