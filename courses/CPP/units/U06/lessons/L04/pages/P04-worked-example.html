💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Noah is preparing a short portfolio presentation.

Noah's presentation order:

  1. Introduce the portfolio purpose
  2. Show a leadership artifact and explain what it demonstrates
  3. Show a resume draft and explain improvement over time
  4. Close by explaining what the portfolio shows about readiness

Why this works:

  • The order is easy to follow.
  • Each artifact has a clear reason for being included.
  • The ending connects the full portfolio back to growth and readiness.
