💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Maya reviews her portfolio from the beginning of the course and compares it to her current portfolio draft.

What Maya notices:

  • Her newer artifacts are better labeled and organized.
  • Her reflections now explain what each item shows.
  • Her earlier work had less detail and weaker explanations.

Maya's reflection: "I have grown in organization and communication. My newer portfolio draft is clearer because I added categories, labels, and short explanations. I still want to improve how confidently I present my work aloud."

Why this works: Maya names a strength, gives evidence, and identifies a next step. That makes her reflection clear and useful.
