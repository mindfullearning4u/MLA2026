Lesson 1: Reflecting on Career Development

In this lesson, you will reflect on your career development by reviewing what you have learned, what you have created, and how your skills have grown.

Reflection helps you identify strengths, recognize progress, and decide what still needs improvement. It also helps you explain your growth clearly in school, career, and portfolio settings.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to evaluate your own development and describe how your work shows growth over time.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Define reflection in a career development context
  • Identify evidence of personal and academic growth
  • Explain strengths and areas for improvement
  • Connect reflection to portfolio quality and career readiness
