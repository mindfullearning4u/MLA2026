🧠 Concept Instruction

The Unit 6 assessment asks students to combine multiple career-readiness skills. This includes reflection, revision, presentation, and evaluation of growth. The goal is to demonstrate what the portfolio shows and how the student has developed.

To prepare well, a student should:

  • review the strongest portfolio artifacts
  • check organization and clarity
  • practice explaining what each artifact shows
  • prepare a short reflection on growth and next steps

This type of assessment is applied. Students are not only recalling information. They are using evidence from their own work and explaining what it means.

The presentation should answer questions such as:

  • What does this portfolio include?
  • What strengths does it show?
  • How has the student improved?
  • What are the student's next steps?

Strong preparation leads to a stronger final presentation and reflection.
