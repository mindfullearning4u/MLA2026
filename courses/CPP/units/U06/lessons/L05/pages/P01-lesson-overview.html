Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Portfolio Presentation and Reflection

In this lesson, you will prepare for the Unit 6 assessment by combining reflection, revision, presentation, and portfolio explanation.

This lesson is not about learning a new concept. It is about using what you already know to present your portfolio clearly and reflect on your growth.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to organize a portfolio presentation and explain how your work shows development and readiness.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Review growth across the course
  • Select portfolio evidence for presentation
  • Organize a short presentation plan
  • Reflect on strengths and next steps
