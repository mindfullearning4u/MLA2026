💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Sofia is preparing for her Unit 6 assessment.

Sofia's plan:

  • Choose three strong artifacts from her portfolio
  • Write one explanation for each artifact
  • Prepare a short reflection on how her work improved
  • Practice a clear beginning, middle, and ending

Sofia's reflection statement: "My portfolio shows that I improved in organization, communication, and professionalism. My strongest evidence includes revised documents and clearly labeled artifacts. My next step is to become more confident when presenting aloud."

Why this works: Sofia uses evidence, structure, and reflection together. That matches the goal of the unit assessment.
