💡 Worked Example

Scenario: Elena is presenting one portfolio artifact to her class.

Weak version: Elena reads directly from a paper in a rushed voice and does not explain why the artifact matters.

Improved version: Elena introduces the artifact, speaks clearly, explains what skill it shows, and ends by stating why it belongs in her portfolio.

Why the improved version is stronger:

  • The main idea is clear.
  • The delivery is easier to follow.
  • The audience understands the purpose of the artifact.
