Lesson 2: Revising and Improving Work

In this lesson, you will learn how to revise work by using feedback, checking quality, and making purposeful improvements.

Revision is an important career-readiness skill. Strong revision shows that you can respond to feedback, improve your work, and present better final products.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to use feedback to strengthen portfolio items and explain how revision improves quality.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain why revision matters
  • Use feedback to improve work quality
  • Identify weak areas in a draft
  • Describe changes that strengthen a portfolio
