Lesson 2: Revising and Improving Work

In this lesson, you will learn how to revise work by using feedback, checking quality, and making purposeful improvements.

Revision is an important career-readiness skill. Strong revision shows that you can respond to feedback, improve your work, and present better final products.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain key ideas related to Revising and Improving Work
  • Identify important details about Using feedback to refine portfolio
  • Compare career-related examples, options, or situations connected to this lesson
  • Apply the lesson ideas in a clear planning or reflection response
