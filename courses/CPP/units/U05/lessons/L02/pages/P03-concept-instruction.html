Concept Instruction

Not every school document belongs in a portfolio. Students should choose artifacts that show skills, effort, achievement, or growth.

Strong portfolio artifacts might include:

  • Projects or assignments that show skill
  • Certificates or awards
  • Reflections that explain learning or improvement
  • Service, leadership, or activity evidence

A strong artifact has a clear reason for being included. Students should be able to explain what the item shows and why it matters.

Portfolio quality improves when artifact choices are thoughtful instead of random.
