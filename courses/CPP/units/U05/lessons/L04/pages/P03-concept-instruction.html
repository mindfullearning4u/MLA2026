Concept Instruction

Portfolios can be created in different formats. Some students may use a digital portfolio. Others may use a physical binder or folder. The best format depends on the purpose, audience, and available tools.

Digital portfolios may help with:

  • Easy updating
  • Sharing online
  • Including multimedia or digital work

Physical portfolios may help with:

  • In-person review
  • Printed materials
  • Simple structure without technology

Students should think about the purpose of the portfolio before choosing the format.
