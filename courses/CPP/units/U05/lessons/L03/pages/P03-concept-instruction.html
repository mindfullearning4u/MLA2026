Concept Instruction

A strong portfolio is easier to use when it has clear organization. Students should group related items and make the portfolio easy for others to follow.

Organization choices may include:

  • Separating items into categories
  • Ordering items in a logical way
  • Using labels or headings
  • Making the layout easy to review

Good portfolio organization supports the purpose of the portfolio. It helps the reader focus on important evidence instead of getting lost in scattered materials.

Clear structure makes a portfolio look more professional and intentional.
