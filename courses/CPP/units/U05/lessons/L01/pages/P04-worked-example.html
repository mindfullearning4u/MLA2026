💡 Worked Example

Scenario: A student wants to apply for a leadership program and needs to show examples of skills and growth.

Step 1: The student identifies the purpose of the portfolio: to show leadership, communication, and responsibility.

Step 2: The student gathers possible evidence such as school projects, certificates, and activity records.

Step 3: The student organizes those items so they are easy to review.

This example shows that a portfolio is useful because it helps organize proof of growth and readiness.
