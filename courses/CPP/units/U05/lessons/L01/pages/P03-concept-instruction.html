Concept Instruction

A career portfolio is a collection of materials that helps students show who they are and what they can do. It gives structure to skills, achievements, reflections, and examples of growth.

A portfolio can help students:

  • Show evidence of learning and achievement
  • Organize important work in one place
  • Prepare for interviews, applications, and presentations

A strong portfolio is not just a folder of random papers. It should be organized and purposeful.

Portfolios matter because they help students communicate readiness with evidence.
