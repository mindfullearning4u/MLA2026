Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Portfolio Draft Submission

This lesson prepares students for the Unit 05 assessment by combining portfolio purpose, artifact selection, organization, and format choice.

In this lesson, students organize a portfolio draft, review the materials included, and explain why the structure and format make sense. This is an application lesson, not a new-concept lesson.

Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook and checkpoint work as evidence of readiness for the unit assessment.
