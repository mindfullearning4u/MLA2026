💡 Worked Example

Scenario: A student is preparing a portfolio draft for a school showcase.

Step 1: The student identifies the purpose of the portfolio.

Step 2: The student selects artifacts that show strong skills and growth.

Step 3: The student organizes the artifacts into categories.

Step 4: The student chooses a format and explains why it fits the situation.

This example shows how a portfolio draft brings together purpose, evidence, organization, and format choice.
