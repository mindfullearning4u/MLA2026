Assessment Readiness

This lesson brings together the work from Unit 05. Students should now be able to explain portfolio purpose, select useful artifacts, organize categories, and choose a format that fits the situation.

Before completing the checkpoint, review these questions:

  • What is the purpose of the portfolio?
  • What artifacts best support that purpose?
  • How should the portfolio be organized?
  • Which format makes the most sense and why?

This readiness lesson is designed to help students assemble and explain a complete portfolio draft.
