Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Values and Work Preferences

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify values and work preferences and connect them to possible career options.

You must choose ONE of the three guided practice profiles:
- A student values creativity, flexible schedules, and independent work.
- A student values teamwork, helping others, and a fast-paced environment.
- A student values job stability, predictable routines, and careful attention to detail.

Required evidence:
- The profile you selected.
- At least two values shown in the profile.
- At least two work preferences shown in the profile.
- One or two possible career areas connected to the profile.
- One sentence explaining why the career match makes sense.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly identify values, work preferences, and a career match, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
