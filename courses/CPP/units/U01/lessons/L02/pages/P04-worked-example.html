💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample profile and observe how values and work preferences influence career fit.

👤Student profile: Jordan wants a career that provides steady income, a predictable schedule, and opportunities to help people directly.

Values: stability, service to others, and responsibility.

Work preferences: structured environment, regular hours, and working with people.

💼Possible career connections: healthcare support, education, public service, or office administration.

🧠Reasoning: Jordan's values and work preferences point toward careers with structure, steady routines, and opportunities to support others.

✅Why this works:

  • It identifies what matters most to the student.
  • It connects workplace preferences to realistic career areas.
  • It shows that career fit depends on priorities, not only interests.
