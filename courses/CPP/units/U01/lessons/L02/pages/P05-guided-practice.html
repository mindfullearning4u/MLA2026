Guided Practice

Practice analyzing values and work preferences and connecting them to possible career choices.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE profile:

  • A student values creativity, flexible schedules, and independent work.
  • A student values teamwork, helping others, and a fast-paced environment.
  • A student values job stability, predictable routines, and careful attention to detail.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the profile you selected.
  2. List at least two values shown in the profile.
  3. List at least two work preferences shown in the profile.
  4. Name one or two possible career areas that could match the profile.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the career match makes sense.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on what the student wants in a workplace and lifestyle, not only on what sounds interesting.
