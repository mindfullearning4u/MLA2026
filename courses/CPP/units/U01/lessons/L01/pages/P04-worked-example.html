Worked Example

💡Read the sample self-assessment and observe how interests and strengths can connect to career ideas.

👤Student profile: Maya enjoys organizing events for school clubs, speaking in front of groups, and helping classmates solve problems.

✨Interests: leadership, communication, planning, and helping others.

💪Strengths: speaking clearly, staying organized, and working well with groups.

💼Possible career connections: event planning, teaching, counseling, management, or public relations.

🧠Reasoning: Maya's interests and strengths both point toward careers that involve communication, coordination, and working with people.

✅Why this works:

  • It identifies both interests and strengths clearly.
  • It uses real examples of student behavior instead of vague labels.
  • It connects personal traits to possible career directions without treating one option as final.
