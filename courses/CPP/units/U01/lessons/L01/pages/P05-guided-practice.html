Guided Practice

Practice identifying interests and strengths and connecting them to possible career directions.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE option to analyze:

  • A student enjoys drawing, designing posters, and using digital art tools.
  • A student enjoys solving math problems, building models, and figuring out how machines work.
  • A student enjoys listening to others, giving advice, and helping peers work through problems.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the option you selected.
  2. List at least two interests shown in the option.
  3. List at least two strengths shown in the option.
  4. Name one or two possible career areas that could connect to the option.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the career connection makes sense.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on patterns in what the student enjoys and does well. Do not choose a career randomly.
