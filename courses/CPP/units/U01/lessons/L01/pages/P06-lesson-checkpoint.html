🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Identifying Interests and Strengths
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify personal interests and strengths and connect them to possible career options.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three guided practice options:

  • A student enjoys drawing, designing posters, and using digital art tools.
  • A student enjoys solving math problems, building models, and figuring out how machines work.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):
