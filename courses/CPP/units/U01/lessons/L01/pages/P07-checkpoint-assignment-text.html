Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Identifying Interests and Strengths

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify personal interests and strengths and connect them to possible career options.

You must choose ONE of the three guided practice options:
- A student enjoys drawing, designing posters, and using digital art tools.
- A student enjoys solving math problems, building models, and figuring out how machines work.
- A student enjoys listening to others, giving advice, and helping peers work through problems.

Required evidence:
- The option you selected.
- At least two interests shown in the option.
- At least two strengths shown in the option.
- One or two possible career areas connected to the option.
- One sentence explaining why the career connection makes sense.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly identify interests, strengths, and a career connection, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
