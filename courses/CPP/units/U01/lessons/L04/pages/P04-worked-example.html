💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample match and observe how personal traits connect to a possible career.

👤Student profile: Sam enjoys solving problems, working carefully with details, and prefers structured routines with clear expectations.

Possible career match: Accounting, data management, or quality control

Evidence: Sam likes careful work, problem-solving, and organized environments.

Why the match works: These careers often require attention to detail, accuracy, and steady routines that align with Sam's preferences and strengths.

✅Why this works:

  • It uses multiple personal traits, not just one.
  • It connects those traits to realistic work demands.
  • It explains the match clearly with evidence.
