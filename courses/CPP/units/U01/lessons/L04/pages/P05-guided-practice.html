Guided Practice

Practice matching student traits to possible career options.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE profile:

  • A student enjoys creative projects, visual design, and working independently.
  • A student enjoys teamwork, leadership, and speaking confidently in front of groups.
  • A student enjoys careful organization, routines, and working accurately with information.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the profile you selected.
  2. List two or three personal traits shown in the profile.
  3. Name one possible career option that could fit the profile.
  4. Explain how the personal traits connect to that career.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why evidence matters when matching self to careers.

⚠️ Reminder: A strong match should be based on clear evidence from the profile, not a random guess.
