🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Matching Self to Careers
This checkpoint verifies your ability to match personal traits to a possible career and explain the connection with evidence.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three guided practice profiles:

  • A student enjoys creative projects, visual design, and working independently.
  • A student enjoys teamwork, leadership, and speaking confidently in front of groups.
  • A student enjoys careful organization, routines, and working accurately with information.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The profile you selected.
  • Two or three personal traits shown in the profile.
  • One possible career option connected to the profile.
  • An explanation of how the traits connect to that career.
  • One sentence explaining why evidence matters when matching self to careers.

If the response does not clearly identify traits, a career option, and supporting evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.
