Guided Practice

Apply all Unit 01 concepts to justify a possible career match.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student enjoys designing projects, prefers independent work, and values creativity and flexible schedules.
  • A student enjoys solving problems, likes routines, and values stability, accuracy, and clear expectations.
  • A student enjoys helping people, working in teams, and values service, communication, and active work environments.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Explain what interests and strengths are shown.
  3. Explain what values or work preferences are shown.
  4. Identify one possible career cluster or pathway.
  5. Name one possible career option.
  6. Write one complete paragraph justifying why the match makes sense.

⚠️ Reminder: You must connect multiple Unit 01 concepts together. Do not list them separately without explaining the relationship.
