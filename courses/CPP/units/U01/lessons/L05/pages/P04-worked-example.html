💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample reflection and observe how multiple Unit 01 ideas are used together.

Scenario: A student enjoys helping others, speaking clearly, staying organized, and prefers structured work with regular interaction with people.

Interests and strengths: helping, communication, and organization.

Values and work preferences: service to others, structure, and working with people.

Career cluster: Education and Training or Health Science.

Possible match: Teaching assistant, school support role, or healthcare support role.

Conclusion: The student's interests, strengths, values, and preferences all support careers that involve direct service, communication, and organized work.

✅Why this works:

  • It connects several Unit 01 concepts in one explanation.
  • It uses evidence instead of making a random career choice.
  • It shows how self-assessment supports career exploration.
