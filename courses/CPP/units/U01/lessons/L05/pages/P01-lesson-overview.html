Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Career Exploration Reflection

This lesson prepares you for the Unit 01 Assessment. You will apply what you learned about interests, strengths, values, work preferences, career clusters, and career matching to justify possible career options.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Review the self-assessment evidence from this unit.
  • Analyze which career options are the strongest matches.
  • Compare career matches using interests, strengths, values, and preferences.
  • Justify a final career match with clear reflection and evidence.
