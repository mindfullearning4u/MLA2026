Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Career Exploration Reflection

This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply all Unit 01 concepts to justify a career match.

You must choose ONE of the three guided practice scenarios:
- A student enjoys designing projects, prefers independent work, and values creativity and flexible schedules.
- A student enjoys solving problems, likes routines, and values stability, accuracy, and clear expectations.
- A student enjoys helping people, working in teams, and values service, communication, and active work environments.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected.
- An explanation of the interests and strengths shown.
- An explanation of the values or work preferences shown.
- One possible career cluster or pathway.
- One possible career option.
- One complete paragraph justifying why the match makes sense.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly connect multiple Unit 01 concepts to a justified career match, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
