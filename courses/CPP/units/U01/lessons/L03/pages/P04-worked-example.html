💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample profile and observe how interests can connect to a career cluster and pathway.

👤Student profile: Alex enjoys coding, solving technical problems, and learning how apps and devices work.

Possible career cluster: Information Technology

Possible pathway: Software development or technical support

🧠Reasoning: Alex's interests connect to technology-focused work that involves problem-solving, systems, and digital tools.

What this shows: A student can begin with interests, identify a broad cluster, and then narrow to one or more pathways inside that cluster.

✅Why this works:

  • It starts with personal interests.
  • It identifies both a broad cluster and a more specific pathway.
  • It shows how career exploration becomes more organized and focused.
