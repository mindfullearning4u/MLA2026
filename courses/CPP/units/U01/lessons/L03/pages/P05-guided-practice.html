Guided Practice

Practice connecting student interests to career clusters and pathways.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE profile:

  • A student enjoys helping younger students learn and explaining ideas clearly.
  • A student enjoys fixing machines, using tools, and building things.
  • A student enjoys designing graphics, creating media, and expressing ideas visually.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the profile you selected.
  2. Identify one possible career cluster connected to the profile.
  3. Identify one possible pathway or type of job within that cluster.
  4. Explain which interests from the profile support that match.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why career clusters are useful for exploration.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on a broad career category first, then narrow to a pathway or job type.
