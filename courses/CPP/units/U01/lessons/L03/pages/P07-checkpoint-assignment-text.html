Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Career Clusters and Pathways

This checkpoint verifies your ability to connect a student profile to a career cluster and pathway.

You must choose ONE of the three guided practice profiles:
- A student enjoys helping younger students learn and explaining ideas clearly.
- A student enjoys fixing machines, using tools, and building things.
- A student enjoys designing graphics, creating media, and expressing ideas visually.

Required evidence:
- The profile you selected.
- One possible career cluster connected to the profile.
- One possible pathway or type of job within that cluster.
- An explanation of which interests support the match.
- One sentence explaining why career clusters are useful for exploration.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly identify a cluster, pathway, and connection to the profile, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
