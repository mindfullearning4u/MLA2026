UNIT 01 | LESSON 4

Guided Practice

Practice inferring word meaning from context. Each response must include both the meaning and the clue that supports it.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Item 1

Sentence: The audience was restless, so the speaker shortened the introduction and moved quickly to the main point.

Word: restless

Do this: Infer the meaning and name the clue that supports your answer.

Item 2

Sentence: After hours without rain, the soil looked brittle and cracked beneath the sun.

Word: brittle

Do this: Write the best meaning and explain how the context helps you know.

Item 3

Sentence: The coach gave a brief but emphatic reminder that practice would begin exactly at 3:15.

Word: emphatic

Do this: Infer the meaning and justify it with a clue from the sentence.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Respond to all three items.
  2. For each word, write a meaning that fits the full sentence.
  3. Point to a specific context clue that supports your inference.

⚠️Reminder: Do not guess a meaning without using textual evidence from the sentence.
