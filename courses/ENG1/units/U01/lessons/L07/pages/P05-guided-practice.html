UNIT 01 | LESSON 7

Guided Practice

Practice monitoring comprehension by identifying a problem, choosing a strategy, and explaining why the strategy fits.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Item 1

Passage: The article says the project was efficient, but the next sentence describes repeated delays and confusion.

Do this: Write what causes the confusion and choose one fix-up strategy.

Item 2

Passage: The writer uses the word fragile while describing a bridge that shakes under heavy traffic.

Do this: Use context clues to infer the word's meaning, then explain why that strategy helps.

Item 3

Passage: A paragraph includes many details about recycling bins, collection days, and student volunteers, but you are no longer sure what the paragraph is mainly saying.

Do this: Restate the central idea and explain why that fix-up strategy would improve comprehension.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Respond to all three items.
  2. For each item, identify the confusion clearly before naming a strategy.
  3. Write one to two sentences explaining why the strategy fits that specific problem.

⚠️Reminder: Monitoring comprehension means matching the strategy to the problem. Do not give the same answer to every item.
