🎯Lesson Checkpoint - Monitoring Comprehension
This checkpoint verifies your ability to notice confusion, apply Unit 01 reading skills, and explain a fix-up strategy.

Read this checkpoint passage: The article claims that the new cafeteria system gives students more independence. However, the next lines explain that students now have fewer lunch choices and must follow a tighter schedule. A reader may need to stop and decide how these ideas fit together.

Standard Expectation

  • Identify the exact point of confusion in the passage.
  • Use two to three Unit 01 concepts to analyze the problem, apply a fix-up strategy, and explain how that strategy improves comprehension.
  • Your explanation should include evidence from the passage.

Accelerated Expectation

  • Choose one specific fix-up strategy for the confusion in the passage.
  • Apply it and justify your choice in a concise written response using passage evidence.

The checkpoint is not met if the response names a strategy without identifying the confusion or without explaining why the strategy fits the problem.
