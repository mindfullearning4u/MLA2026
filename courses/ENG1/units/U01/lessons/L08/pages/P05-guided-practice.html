UNIT 01 | LESSON 8

Guided Practice

This practice requires more independent reading decisions. Use the Unit 01 routine to determine which skills matter most for each situation.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Item 1

Reading situation: You understand most of a passage, but one unfamiliar word affects the main point.

Do this: Decide which Unit 01 skills you would use first and explain why.

Item 2

Reading situation: A paragraph includes several details, but you are not sure which ones actually support the central idea.

Do this: Explain how you would sort useful details from weaker ones.

Item 3

Reading situation: A text compares two solutions to the same problem, and you need to explain the text objectively afterward.

Do this: Describe the reading routine you would use from start to finish.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Respond to all three items.
  2. Name the Unit 01 skills you would use and explain your reasoning.
  3. Do not rely on a fixed script; make choices based on the reading situation.

⚠️Reminder: This lesson is about integration. Strong responses explain why a reader would choose certain skills in a specific situation.
