🎯Lesson Checkpoint - Integrated Reading Routine
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply multiple Unit 01 reading skills independently in one realistic reading situation.

Reading scenario: You are reading a short school newsletter article about a new after-school study lab. The article explains why the lab was created, describes how students use it, includes the word structured, compares study time before and after the lab opened, and ends by emphasizing that regular support improves student confidence. Your task is to show how you would read this article skillfully from beginning to end.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Explain the reading routine you would use to understand the article.
  • Apply at least three to six Unit 01 concepts in your response.
  • Describe how you would identify the central idea, choose supporting details, handle vocabulary in context, notice structure, monitor comprehension, and summarize objectively as needed.
  • Justify your reading decisions with clear reasoning.

The checkpoint is not met if the response lists skills without explaining how and why they would be used in this specific scenario.
