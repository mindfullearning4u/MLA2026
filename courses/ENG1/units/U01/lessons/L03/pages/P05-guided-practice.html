UNIT 01 | LESSON 3

Guided Practice

Practice judging and creating supporting details. Each response should show how the detail connects to the central idea.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Item 1

Central idea: Reading routines improve comprehension.

Details: (a) Readers pause to annotate important lines. (b) The classroom walls are blue.

Do this: Choose the stronger supporting detail and explain why.

Item 2

Central idea: Exercise can improve focus in school.

Details: (a) Some students stretch before class. (b) A school mascot wears green and gold.

Do this: Write which detail is relevant and how it supports the central idea.

Item 3

Passage idea: Community service teaches responsibility.

Do this: Write one original supporting detail that would strongly support that idea.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Respond to all three items.
  2. For Items 1 and 2, explain the connection between the detail and the central idea.
  3. For Item 3, write one specific detail, not a broad topic.

⚠️Reminder: A detail is only strong if it clearly helps prove or develop the central idea.
