Guided Practice

Practice identifying text structure and explaining how the organization shapes meaning.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Item 1

Passage: First the class gathered ideas, then students organized those ideas into categories, and finally they wrote their drafts.

Do this: Identify the structure and explain how the organization helps the reader.

Item 2

Passage: Unlike paper maps, digital maps can update directions immediately. Both tools help travelers, but one changes more quickly when conditions shift.

Do this: Identify the structure and name the comparison that proves it.

Item 3

Passage: The sidewalk flooded after the storm because leaves blocked the drain.

Do this: Identify the structure and explain how the cause and result shape meaning.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Respond to all three items.
  2. For each item, identify the structure by name.
  3. Use one sentence to explain how that structure helps the reader understand the passage.

⚠️Reminder: Do not stop after naming the structure. Explain how the organization shapes meaning.
