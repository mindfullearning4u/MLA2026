Lesson 2 Checkpoint – Central Idea Notebook Evidence
This checkpoint verifies your ability to correctly identify the central idea of a text.

You must submit evidence of your notebook work from Lesson 2.

Required evidence:
- Topic written in 1–2 words
- Central idea written as one complete sentence
- Two supporting phrases copied from different parts of the text
- One sentence explaining why your central idea is not just a topic

Accepted formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If your response lists only a topic, includes personal opinion, or cannot be supported by the text, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
