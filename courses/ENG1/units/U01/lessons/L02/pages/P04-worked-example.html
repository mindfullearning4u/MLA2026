Text:
Jordan’s team was in the championship game. He played aggressively, trying to score every point himself. His teammates grew frustrated and stopped passing to him. Near the end of the game, Jordan twisted his ankle and had to sit out. Watching from the sidelines, he saw his teammates work together. They passed, they supported each other, they communicated. And they won. Jordan realized that victory felt more meaningful when shared. He approached his teammates afterward and thanked them.

Model:

  • Topic (incorrect): teamwork
  • Central idea (correct): The text shows that success becomes more meaningful when it is shared through teamwork and communication.

This central idea applies to the entire text and is supported by multiple details.
