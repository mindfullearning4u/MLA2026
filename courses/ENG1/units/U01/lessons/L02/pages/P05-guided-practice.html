Practice Text:
Plastic bottles, bags, and food packaging end up in landfills and oceans every day. Many of these items are single-use and remain in the environment for hundreds of years. Wildlife are harmed when they mistake plastic for food or become entangled in it. Communities that reduce plastic use report cleaner environments and improved water quality.
