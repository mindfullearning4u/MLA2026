UNIT 01 | LESSON 2

Guided Practice

Practice identifying central idea. Read each short passage and write the best central idea as one complete sentence.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Item 1

Passage: Morning routines help students arrive ready to learn. Packing materials the night before, checking the schedule, and arriving on time reduce confusion at the start of the day.

Do this: Write the topic, then write one central idea sentence.

Item 2

Passage: Community gardens give neighborhoods more than fresh food. They create shared spaces, teach practical skills, and encourage people to work together on a common goal.

Do this: Write the central idea and list two details that support it.

Item 3

Passage: Some students prefer to study with music, while others need silence. The stronger choice depends on which environment helps the student focus and remember information.

Do this: Write the central idea in one sentence without copying a full line from the passage.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Respond to all three items.
  2. Write the central idea as a complete statement, not a topic word.
  3. For Item 2, include two supporting details from the passage.

⚠️Reminder: A central idea must fit the whole passage. Do not choose one detail and treat it as the main point.
