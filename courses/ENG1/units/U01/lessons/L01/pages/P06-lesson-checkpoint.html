🎯Lesson Checkpoint - Active Reading in MLA
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply active reading by annotating a passage, asking a useful question, and explaining your thinking.

Read this checkpoint passage: Effective readers do not wait until the end of a text to think about meaning. They stop, mark what matters, and question ideas as they read. These habits help them notice confusion early and build a clearer understanding of the text.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Write one annotation from the passage and explain why you marked that part.
  • Write one question you would ask while reading the passage.
  • Write two to three sentences explaining how your annotation and question show active reading instead of passive scanning.

The checkpoint is not met if the response only copies lines from the passage without showing a reason for the annotation or the question.
