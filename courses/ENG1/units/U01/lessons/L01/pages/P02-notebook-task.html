Write this heading in your notebook:

Unit 01 Lesson 1 - Active Reading in MLA

Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Active reading: reading on purpose by interacting with the text while you read.
  • Annotate: mark important ideas, patterns, or reactions in the text.
  • Question: pause to ask what is clear, unclear, important, or surprising.
  • Track thinking: leave a written record of what you notice and how your thinking changes.
  • Key idea: active reading creates evidence of comprehension; passive scanning does not.

Write this required test sentence in your notebook:

  • If I can annotate, ask questions, and track my thinking while I read, I understand this lesson.
