Lesson 2: Core Constitutional Principles

This lesson teaches one concept: the Constitution is built on four core principles that organize and limit government power. These principles are popular sovereignty, limited government, separation of powers, and federalism.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain key ideas related to Core Constitutional Principles
  • Identify important details about Popular sovereignty, limited government, separation of powers, and federalism
  • Compare civic situations, government actions, or constitutional ideas connected to this lesson
  • Apply the lesson ideas in a clear, supported civic response
