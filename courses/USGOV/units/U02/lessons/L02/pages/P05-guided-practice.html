UNIT 02 | LESSON 2

Guided Practice

📒Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: Citizens vote in an election to choose representatives who will make laws.

Scenario B: Congress writes laws, the president enforces laws, and courts interpret laws.

Scenario C: A state runs its own public schools, but the national government handles national defense.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you chose.
  2. Write the name of the constitutional principle shown in that scenario.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining how the scenario shows that principle.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining how that principle helps limit or organize government power.

⚠️Final Reminder: Your response must name the correct principle and explain the connection clearly. Do not only copy words from the scenario.
