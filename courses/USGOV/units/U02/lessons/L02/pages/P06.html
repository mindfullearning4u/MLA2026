🎯 Lesson Checkpoint

This checkpoint verifies that you can identify a constitutional principle and explain how it limits or organizes government power.

Important: Use one of the guided practice scenarios or a similar government example.

Write the following:

  • 1 paragraph of 5–6 complete sentences
  • Name 1 constitutional principle from this lesson
  • Explain what the principle means
  • Explain how the principle works in government
  • Explain how the principle limits or organizes power
