Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Constitutional Principles Analysis

This lesson prepares you for the Unit Assessment by requiring you to apply what you have learned in Unit 02 to real-world government scenarios.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Review the constitutional principles from this unit.
  • Analyze the scenario using federalism and core constitutional ideas.
  • Compare possible responses through the lens of constitutional principles.
  • Apply and justify the strongest constitutional analysis.
