Concept: Constitutional Analysis

In this lesson, you will demonstrate your ability to apply constitutional ideas to real situations. You must identify the issue, apply the correct principle, and explain your reasoning clearly.

A strong response does three things: it identifies the constitutional issue, applies the correct concept from Unit 02, and explains why that concept fits the situation.
