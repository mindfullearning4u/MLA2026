Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all steps.

Scenario A: A government action limits a citizen’s rights in a way that may conflict with constitutional protections.

Scenario B: A state law conflicts with a federal law on the same issue.

Scenario C: Leaders want to change a major constitutional rule that applies across all states.

Steps:

  1. Identify the main issue in the scenario.
  2. Name the relevant constitutional concept (federalism, amendment process, or constitutional principle).
  3. Write 2–3 complete sentences explaining the problem.
  4. Write 1–2 complete sentences explaining the correct outcome and why.

Final Reminder: Your answer must clearly connect the scenario to a constitutional concept and explain your reasoning.
