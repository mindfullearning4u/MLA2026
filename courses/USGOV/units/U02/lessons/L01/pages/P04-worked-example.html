💡Worked Example

Scenario:
After the Revolutionary War, the United States owed money to other countries. Congress asked states to contribute money, but several states refused to pay.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
The government can force states to pay the money.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
Under the Articles of Confederation, Congress could not force states to pay because it did not have the power to tax.

✅Why this works:

  • Identifies the weakness: no power to tax
  • Connects the weakness to a real problem
  • Explains why the government could not act
