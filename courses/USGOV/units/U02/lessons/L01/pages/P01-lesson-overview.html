Lesson 1: Origins of the U.S. Constitution

This lesson teaches one concept: why the United States replaced the Articles of Confederation with the U.S. Constitution.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain key ideas related to Origins of the U.S. Constitution
  • Identify important details about Historical context and the weaknesses of the Articles of Confederation
  • Compare civic situations, government actions, or constitutional ideas connected to this lesson
  • Apply the lesson ideas in a clear, supported civic response
