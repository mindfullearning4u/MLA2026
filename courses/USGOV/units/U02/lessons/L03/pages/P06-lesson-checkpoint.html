🎯 Lesson Checkpoint

This checkpoint verifies that you can identify an example of federalism and explain how power is divided between national and state governments.

Important: Use one of the guided practice scenarios or a similar government example.

Write the following:

  • 1 paragraph of 5–6 complete sentences
  • Name the type of power: delegated, reserved, or concurrent
  • Explain why that type of power fits the example
  • Explain how the example shows federalism
  • Explain how dividing power helps balance government authority
