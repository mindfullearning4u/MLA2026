Respond:

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: Congress creates laws about immigration and the national government manages foreign treaties.

Scenario B: A state creates graduation requirements for its public high schools and issues driver licenses.

Scenario C: Both the national government and a state government collect taxes to pay for public services.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Write the type of power shown: delegated, reserved, or concurrent.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining why the scenario fits that type of power.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining how the scenario shows federalism in action.
