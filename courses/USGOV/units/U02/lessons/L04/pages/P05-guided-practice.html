Respond:

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: A state wants to change its local school attendance rule for one district.

Scenario B: National leaders want to add a new constitutional protection that would apply in every state.

Scenario C: Congress wants to change a federal tax rule, but the Constitution itself would stay the same.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Write whether the situation requires an amendment or a regular law.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining why your choice is correct.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining why the amendment process is difficult on purpose.

⚠️Final Reminder: Your response must explain whether the Constitution itself is being changed. If the Constitution is being changed, an amendment is required.
