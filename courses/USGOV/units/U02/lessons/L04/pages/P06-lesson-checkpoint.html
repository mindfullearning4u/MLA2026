🎯 Lesson Checkpoint

This checkpoint verifies that you can explain why amendments exist and why the amendment process is intentionally difficult.

Important: Use one of the guided practice scenarios or a similar example.

Write the following:

  • 1 paragraph of 5–6 complete sentences
  • Define an amendment
  • Explain why amendments are needed
  • Explain why the process is difficult
  • Include one example of a situation that would require an amendment or a regular law
