UNIT 01 | LESSON 5

Guided Practice

Practice applying Unit 01 concepts to a scenario. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A government makes a decision without citizen input.
  • Citizens disagree about whether a law is fair.
  • A group of people is not allowed to participate equally in a decision.

📒Notebook Accountability (Required)

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the main issue (government power, authority, rights, or participation).
  3. Explain why the situation may be unfair, unclear, or problematic.
  4. Describe the most responsible civic response.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why responsible civic action matters.

⚠️Reminder: The assessment will require you to apply multiple concepts at the same time.
