Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Foundations of Government Scenario

This lesson is the Unit 01 assessment preparation. You will apply everything learned about government foundations, types of government, power and authority, and citizenship.

You will analyze scenarios and demonstrate your ability to identify government structures, explain authority, and apply civic responsibility.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Review the foundations of government concepts from this unit.
  • Analyze the civic situation using purpose of government, authority, and citizenship ideas.
  • Compare possible government or citizen responses using evidence.
  • Justify the most responsible civic conclusion with clear reasoning.
