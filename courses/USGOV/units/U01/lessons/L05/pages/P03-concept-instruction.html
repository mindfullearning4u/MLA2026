Concept: Unit Assessment Overview

This assessment measures your understanding of all concepts from Unit 01. You will apply your knowledge to real-world scenarios involving government and civic behavior.

The assessment includes:

  • Questions about the purpose and functions of government
  • Identification of different types of government
  • Analysis of power, authority, and legitimacy
  • Evaluation of citizenship and civic responsibility

Your goal is to demonstrate mastery by identifying accurate concepts and choosing responsible, informed actions in each situation.

💭 Think: How can you use multiple concepts together to fully explain a situation?

⚠️ Common mistake: Answering with only one concept when the situation requires multiple ideas (government type, authority, and citizenship).
