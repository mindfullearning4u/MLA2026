Worked Example

👀 Review this example of a scenario-based assessment question.

Scenario: A government limits public protests, and citizens question whether the rule is fair.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking: Treating the situation as if it does not involve rights, authority, or legitimacy.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown: The situation involves limits on rights, questions about authority, and concerns about legitimacy.

Why this is correct:

  • It recognizes the role of government in setting rules.
  • It identifies that authority must be accepted to be legitimate.
  • It considers citizen rights and civic response.
