Concept Instruction

Governments are created because people need structure, safety, and organization in society. Without systems in place, it would be difficult to maintain stability or resolve conflicts.

One major reason governments form is to maintain order. Rules and laws help prevent chaos and create predictable systems for behavior.

Governments also provide protection. This includes protecting citizens from harm and ensuring safety within communities.

Another reason is cooperation. Governments allow people to work together to achieve goals that would be difficult individually, such as building infrastructure or organizing services.

Finally, governments promote the common good by making decisions that benefit society as a whole.

🧠Think: Why might people agree to follow rules created by a government?

⚠️Common mistake: Thinking governments only exist to control people. Governments also exist to support and organize society.
