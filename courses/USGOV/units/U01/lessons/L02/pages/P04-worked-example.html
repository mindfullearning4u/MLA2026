Worked Example

Scenario: A community creates rules about public safety and organizes emergency services.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking: These rules are unnecessary and limit freedom.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown: These systems exist to maintain order, protect people, and support cooperation within the community.

Why this works:

  • Rules prevent disorder and unsafe situations.
  • Protection systems keep people safe.
  • Organized services help communities function effectively.
