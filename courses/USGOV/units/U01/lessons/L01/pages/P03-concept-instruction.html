Concept Instruction

Government is the system or organization that creates and enforces laws within a society. It exists to manage people, resources, and decisions in an organized way.

Governments play a key role in maintaining order by setting rules, protecting people, and resolving conflicts. They also provide services such as schools, roads, and public safety systems.

Without government, societies would struggle to maintain stability, cooperation, and fairness.

🧠Think: What would happen if there were no rules or systems to organize society?

⚠️Common mistake: Thinking government only creates laws. Governments also provide services and make decisions that affect daily life.
