💡Worked Example

Scenario: A school principal enforces school rules, and students follow them.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking: Students follow rules only because they are forced to.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown: The principal has authority because students recognize the role, and legitimacy because rules are accepted as fair.

✅Why this works:

  • The principal has power to enforce rules.
  • Authority comes from the recognized leadership role.
  • Legitimacy exists when students believe rules are fair and necessary.
