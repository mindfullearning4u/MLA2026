Worked Example

Scenario:
A school suspends one student for breaking a rule but does not tell the student what rule was broken and does not allow the student to explain what happened.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
This is only about school discipline, so constitutional fairness does not matter.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This situation raises a due process issue because the student was punished without fair procedure. Fair treatment includes being told the reason for the action and having a chance to respond before a decision is finalized.

Why this works:

  • It identifies the correct issue: fair procedure.
  • It explains that punishment should follow rules and process.
  • It shows that government-related institutions must act fairly.
  • It distinguishes due process from equal protection.
