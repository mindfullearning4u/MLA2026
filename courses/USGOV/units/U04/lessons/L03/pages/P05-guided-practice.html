Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: A city fines a person without giving notice of the charge or a chance to respond.

Scenario B: Two students break the same rule, but only one is punished for no clear reason.

Scenario C: A government office applies a rule differently to similar people without a valid legal reason.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify whether the issue is due process or equal protection.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining what happened in the scenario.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining why the situation is unfair under the law.

Final Reminder: Focus on the kind of unfairness shown. Ask yourself: Is the problem unfair procedure or unequal treatment?
