Lesson Checkpoint

This checkpoint verifies that you can explain the difference between due process and equal protection and apply the correct idea to a situation.

Important: Use one of the guided practice scenarios or a similar legal example.

Write the following:

  • 1 paragraph of 5-6 complete sentences
  • Use the term due process or equal protection correctly
  • Explain what happened in the situation
  • Explain why the situation is unfair under the law
  • Explain how fair treatment should work instead
