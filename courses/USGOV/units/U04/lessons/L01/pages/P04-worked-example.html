Worked Example

Scenario:
A student writes an article criticizing a government policy and shares it publicly.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
The student can be punished because they disagreed with the government.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This situation is protected by the First Amendment, which guarantees freedom of speech. The government cannot punish individuals simply for expressing opinions.

Why this works:

  • Identifies the correct right (freedom of speech)
  • Explains protection from government punishment
  • Connects directly to the Bill of Rights
