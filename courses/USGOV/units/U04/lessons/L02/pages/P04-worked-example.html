Worked Example

Scenario:
A student is not allowed to join a school club because of their race.

Incorrect Thinking:
This is a civil liberty issue because it involves a student.

Correct Explanation:
This is a civil rights issue because it involves discrimination. Civil rights protect individuals from unequal treatment based on characteristics such as race.

Why this works:

  • Identifies discrimination
  • Connects to equality under the law
  • Clearly distinguishes rights from liberties
