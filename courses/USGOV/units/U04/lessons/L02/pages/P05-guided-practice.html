Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario:

A: A person is free to express political opinions.

B: A person is denied a job because of their gender.

C: A citizen practices their religion without interference.

Steps:

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Identify whether it is a liberty or a right
  3. Explain the situation in 2 sentences
  4. Explain why it fits that category
