Worked Example

Scenario:
A school allows students to express opinions but stops a student from making threatening statements toward others.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
The school is violating freedom of speech because all speech must be allowed.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This situation involves a limit on rights. While freedom of speech is protected, it can be limited to prevent harm and protect the safety of others. The school's action is justified because it protects public safety.

Why this works:

  • Identifies the correct right (freedom of speech)
  • Recognizes that rights can be limited
  • Explains the reason for the limit (safety)
  • Justifies the outcome clearly
