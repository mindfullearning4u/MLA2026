Guided Practice

Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: A person is denied entry to a public place because of their race.

Scenario B: A person is punished without being given a chance to explain their actions.

Scenario C: A protest is required to follow safety rules to protect the public.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the type of right involved.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining the situation.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining whether the action is fair or unfair.

Final Reminder: Clearly explain your reasoning. Identify the right and justify your conclusion.
