Worked Example

Scenario:
A person tries to organize a large protest in a crowded area without following safety rules or obtaining required permits.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
The government cannot place any limits because people have freedom of speech.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This situation shows that rights can be limited. While people have freedom of speech, the government can require rules such as permits to protect public safety and order.

Why this works:

  • Recognizes the right involved (freedom of speech)
  • Explains why limits may be necessary
  • Connects limits to safety and order
  • Shows balance between rights and responsibility
