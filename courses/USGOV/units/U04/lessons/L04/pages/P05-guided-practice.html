Guided Practice

Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: A person is stopped from shouting threats in a crowded place.

Scenario B: A protest is required to follow safety rules and obtain permits.

Scenario C: A school sets rules for student speech during class time.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the right involved.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining the situation.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining why a limit may be allowed.

Final Reminder: Focus on the balance between individual rights and the needs of society.
