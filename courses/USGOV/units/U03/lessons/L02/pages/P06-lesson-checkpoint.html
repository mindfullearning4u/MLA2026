🎯 Lesson Checkpoint

This checkpoint verifies that you can identify the executive branch and explain its role in enforcing laws and providing leadership.

Important: Use one of the guided practice scenarios or a similar government example.

Write the following:

  • 1 paragraph of 5–6 complete sentences
  • Name the executive branch
  • Explain that the executive branch enforces laws
  • Include the role of the president
  • Explain how leadership is part of the executive branch
