Scenario:
Congress passes a national law about transportation safety. After the law is passed, the president and executive agencies are responsible for putting the law into action and making sure it is enforced.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
This is mainly the job of the legislative branch because Congress passed the law, so Congress also enforces it.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This scenario shows the executive branch in action. Congress creates the law, but the president and executive branch carry it out and enforce it. This is one of the main constitutional roles of the executive branch.

✅Why this works:

  • It correctly identifies enforcement as an executive branch responsibility.
  • It distinguishes lawmaking from law enforcement.

  • It explains how the executive branch functions within the constitutional system.
