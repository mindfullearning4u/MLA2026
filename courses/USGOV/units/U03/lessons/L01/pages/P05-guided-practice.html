Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: Members of the House of Representatives and the Senate debate a proposed law about transportation safety.

Scenario B: Citizens vote for candidates who will represent them in Congress and help create national laws.

Scenario C: Lawmakers in Congress review a proposed bill, discuss it, and prepare to vote on it.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Name the branch of government shown in the scenario.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining how the scenario shows the legislative branch.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining how representation connects citizens to Congress.

Final Reminder: Your response must clearly connect the scenario to Congress, lawmaking, and representation.
