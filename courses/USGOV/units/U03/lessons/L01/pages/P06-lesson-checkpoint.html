🎯 Lesson Checkpoint

This checkpoint verifies that you can identify the legislative branch and explain its role in lawmaking and representation.

Important: Use one of the guided practice scenarios or a similar government example.

Write the following:

  • 1 paragraph of 5–6 complete sentences
  • Name the legislative branch
  • Explain that Congress makes laws
  • Include the House of Representatives and the Senate
  • Explain how representation connects citizens to Congress
