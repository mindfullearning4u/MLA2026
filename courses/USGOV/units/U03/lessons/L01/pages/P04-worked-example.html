Worked Example

Scenario:
A citizen wants a new national safety rule to become law. The proposal is introduced in Congress, discussed by elected lawmakers, and then voted on in both the House of Representatives and the Senate.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
The judicial branch is responsible because judges decide whether rules are important.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This scenario shows the legislative branch in action. Congress is responsible because it makes laws. The House of Representatives and the Senate both take part in discussing and voting on proposed legislation.

Why this works:

  • It identifies Congress as the lawmaking branch.
  • It correctly names the House and Senate as the two parts of Congress.
  • It connects elected representatives to the lawmaking process.
  • It distinguishes lawmaking from the roles of the executive and judicial branches.
