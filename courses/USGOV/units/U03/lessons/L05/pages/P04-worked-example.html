Worked Example

Scenario:
A national law is challenged in court. Judges review the law and decide whether it follows the Constitution.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
This is the legislative branch because the law was created by Congress.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This scenario shows the judicial branch. Courts interpret laws and decide whether they follow the Constitution. This process is known as judicial review.

Why this works:

  • It identifies the correct action: interpreting a law.
  • It connects that action to the judicial branch.
  • It explains judicial review clearly.
  • It distinguishes interpretation from lawmaking and enforcement.
