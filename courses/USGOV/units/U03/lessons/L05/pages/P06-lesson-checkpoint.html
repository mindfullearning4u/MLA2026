🎯 Lesson Checkpoint

This checkpoint verifies that you can correctly identify which branch of government is responsible in a given scenario and explain your reasoning.

Important: Use one of the guided practice scenarios or a similar government situation.

Write the following:

  • 1 paragraph of 5–6 complete sentences
  • Name the correct branch
  • Explain the action taking place
  • Explain why that branch has authority
  • Use accurate vocabulary (legislative, executive, judicial)
