Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: Congress debates and votes on a new national law.

Scenario B: The president signs a law and ensures it is enforced across the country.

Scenario C: A court reviews a law to determine if it follows the Constitution.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Name the correct branch of government.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining the action in the scenario.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining why that branch has authority.

Final Reminder: Your response must clearly match the action in the scenario to the correct branch.
