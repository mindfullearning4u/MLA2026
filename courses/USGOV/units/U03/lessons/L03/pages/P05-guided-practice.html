Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: The Supreme Court reviews a law to determine if it violates the Constitution.

Scenario B: A court decides how a law applies in a specific case between two individuals.

Scenario C: Judges interpret the meaning of a law to resolve a legal dispute.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Name the branch of government shown in the scenario.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining how the scenario shows the judicial branch.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining how judicial review or interpretation is used.

Final Reminder: Your response must clearly connect the scenario to interpretation of laws or judicial review.
