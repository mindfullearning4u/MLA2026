Guided Practice

📒Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario. Then complete all 4 steps.

Scenario A: A court reviews a law passed by Congress and determines it does not follow the Constitution.

Scenario B: The president enforces a law but must follow rules set by Congress.

Scenario C: Congress creates a law, and the president reviews it before it becomes official.

Steps:

  1. Write the letter of the scenario you selected.
  2. Name the branches involved in the scenario.
  3. Write 2 complete sentences explaining how one branch limits another.
  4. Write 1 complete sentence explaining why this is an example of checks and balances.

⚠️Final Reminder: Your response must clearly explain how one branch limits another.
