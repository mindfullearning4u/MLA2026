Guided Practice

Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario and complete all steps.

Scenario A: A person votes and also joins a group that supports a specific issue.

Scenario B: A group uses media to influence public opinion about a policy.

Scenario C: Citizens volunteer and communicate with leaders to improve their community.

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Identify the type(s) of participation
  3. Explain what is happening (2 sentences)
  4. Explain how it influences government or society (2 sentences)

Final Reminder: Focus on identifying actions and explaining their impact clearly.
