Worked Example

Scenario:
A group of citizens organizes a campaign to raise awareness about a local issue, encourages people to vote, and communicates with elected officials about policy changes.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
This situation only shows voting and has no other impact.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This scenario shows multiple forms of civic participation. The group influences public opinion, encourages voting, and communicates with government officials. These actions help shape both elections and policy decisions.

Why this works:

  • Identifies multiple types of participation
  • Explains how each action influences government
  • Connects participation to outcomes
  • Applies the concept clearly
