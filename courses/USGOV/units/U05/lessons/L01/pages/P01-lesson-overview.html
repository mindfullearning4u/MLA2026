Lesson 1: Voting and Elections

Voting is one of the most direct ways citizens participate in government. Elections allow people to choose leaders and influence decisions that affect their communities and the country.

In this lesson, you will learn how elections work and why voting is an essential responsibility in a democratic system.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain key ideas related to Voting and Elections
  • Identify important details about Election processes and the importance of civic participation
  • Compare civic situations, government actions, or constitutional ideas connected to this lesson
  • Apply the lesson ideas in a clear, supported civic response
