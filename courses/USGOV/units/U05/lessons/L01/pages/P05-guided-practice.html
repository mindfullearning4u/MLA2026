Guided Practice

Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario and complete all steps.

Scenario A: A large number of citizens vote in a national election.

Scenario B: Very few people participate in a local election.

Scenario C: A community encourages people to register and vote.

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Explain what is happening (2 sentences)
  3. Explain how participation affects government (2 sentences)
  4. Explain why voting is important (1 sentence)

Final Reminder: Focus on how participation affects representation and decision-making.
