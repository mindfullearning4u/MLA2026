Worked Example

Scenario:
A group of citizens volunteers to clean a local park and also contacts city officials to request improvements to public spaces.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
This is not civic participation because it does not involve voting.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This is an example of civic participation beyond voting. The group is improving the community through service and influencing government by communicating with officials.

Why this works:

  • Identifies multiple forms of participation
  • Connects actions to community impact
  • Shows how citizens influence government
  • Applies the concept to a real situation
