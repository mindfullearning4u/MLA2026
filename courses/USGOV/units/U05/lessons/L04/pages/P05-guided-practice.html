Guided Practice

Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario and complete all steps.

Scenario A: A person volunteers in their community to help improve local conditions.

Scenario B: A group organizes support for a policy change.

Scenario C: A citizen contacts an elected official to express concerns.

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Explain what is happening (2 sentences)
  3. Explain how this is civic participation (2 sentences)
  4. Explain how it influences government or community (1 sentence)

Final Reminder: Focus on how actions beyond voting contribute to change.
