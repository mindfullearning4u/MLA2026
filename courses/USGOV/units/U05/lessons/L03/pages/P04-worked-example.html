Worked Example

Scenario:
Two news sources report on the same election event. One focuses on economic issues, while the other focuses on social issues.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
Both reports will cause people to form the exact same opinion.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
This situation shows how media can shape public opinion. Different presentations of information can lead people to focus on different aspects of an issue and form different opinions.

Why this works:

  • Recognizes that media influences perception
  • Explains how presentation affects understanding
  • Connects information to opinion formation
  • Applies the concept to a real-world situation
