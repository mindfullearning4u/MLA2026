Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario and complete all steps.

Scenario A: A student verifies information before sharing a post about a public issue.

Scenario B: A citizen participates in an online discussion and communicates respectfully with people who disagree.

Scenario C: A community member learns about a global issue and supports a local effort connected to that problem.

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Identify the responsibility shown
  3. Explain what is happening in 2 complete sentences
  4. Explain why this is an example of responsible citizenship in 1 complete sentence

Final Reminder: Focus on how citizenship responsibilities apply in modern society, including digital spaces.
