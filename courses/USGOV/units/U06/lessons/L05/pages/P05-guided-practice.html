Guided Practice

Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario and complete all steps.

Scenario A: A policy is created to improve public transportation access.

Scenario B: Government responds to a public health issue with new regulations.

Scenario C: A program is designed to support economic growth.

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Identify the issue
  3. Explain the government response (2 sentences)
  4. Evaluate effectiveness and fairness (2 sentences)

Final Reminder: Focus on analysis and evaluation, not just description.
