Concept: Applying Public Policy and Civic Analysis

This assessment requires you to apply your understanding of public policy and civic responsibility. You will examine a situation and explain how government action addresses a problem.

Begin by identifying the issue. Clearly describe the problem or challenge that requires government attention. This could involve economic, social, or environmental concerns.

Next, explain the government response. Identify how policies, laws, or programs are used to address the issue.

Then evaluate the outcome. Consider whether the policy is effective, whether it is fair, and what consequences result from the action.

Finally, explain the role of citizens. Civic participation and responsible citizenship help influence and evaluate government decisions.

💭 Think: How can citizens influence whether a policy continues or changes?

⚠️ Common mistake: Do not only describe the situation. You must analyze and evaluate the government action.
