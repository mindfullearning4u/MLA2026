💡Worked Example

Scenario:
A government identifies rising unemployment as a major issue. It creates job training programs and provides funding to support businesses. Later, it evaluates whether employment rates improve.

Not Responsible / Incorrect Thinking:
The government only responds by identifying the problem.

Correct / Responsible Breakdown:
The government identifies the issue, creates policies such as job training programs, implements those programs, and evaluates their effectiveness.

✅Why this works:

  • Shows full policy response process
  • Connects issue to solution
  • Explains implementation clearly
  • Includes evaluation of results
