Choose ONE scenario and complete all steps.

Scenario A: Government identifies a need for improved school safety.

Scenario B: A policy is created to improve public transportation.

Scenario C: A program is reviewed to determine if it is effective.

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Identify which step of the policy process is shown
