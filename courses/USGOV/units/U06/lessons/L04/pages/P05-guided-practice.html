Guided Practice

📓 Notebook Accountability (Required)

Choose ONE scenario and complete all steps.

Scenario A: A policy improves school safety but increases costs for families.

Scenario B: A program reduces unemployment but benefits some groups more than others.

Scenario C: A law improves environmental conditions but limits certain business activities.

  1. Write the scenario letter
  2. Identify one positive outcome
  3. Identify one concern or consequence
  4. Explain whether the policy is effective, fair, or both (2 sentences)

Final Reminder: Always evaluate policies using multiple criteria, not just one outcome.
