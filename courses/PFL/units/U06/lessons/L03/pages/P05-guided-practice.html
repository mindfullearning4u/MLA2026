Guided Practice

Scenario: Compare these housing choices:

  • Choice A: Housing cost of $650 per month
  • Choice B: Housing cost of $920 per month with fewer dollars available for savings

Respond:

  • Calculate the monthly difference between the two choices.
  • Explain one trade-off created by the higher-cost choice.
  • Describe how the decision could affect other financial priorities.
  • State which choice is stronger for a tighter budget.
  • Justify your answer clearly.
