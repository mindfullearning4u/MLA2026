Lesson Checkpoint | Major Financial Decisions
This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare major financial choices and justify the stronger option using cost and consequence evidence.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Include one correct cost comparison or calculation.
  • Identify at least one trade-off involved.
  • Explain how the choice affects other financial priorities.
  • State which option better supports the situation.
  • Provide a justified final conclusion.
