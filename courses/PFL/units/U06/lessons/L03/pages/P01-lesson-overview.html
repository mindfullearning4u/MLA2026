Lesson 3: Major Financial Decisions

In this lesson, you will examine major financial decisions involving housing, transportation, and lifestyle choices. These decisions often involve trade-offs because large costs can affect many other parts of a financial plan.

You will compare major life choices, analyze likely consequences, and explain why careful evaluation matters before committing to expensive or long-term financial decisions.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify examples of major financial decisions
  • Compare costs and consequences of different choices
  • Explain how lifestyle choices can affect a financial plan
  • Justify a decision using evidence and trade-offs
