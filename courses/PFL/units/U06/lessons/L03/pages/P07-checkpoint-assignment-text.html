Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Major Financial Decisions

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare major financial choices and explain the consequences of each option.

Required evidence:
- One correct cost comparison or calculation
- Identification of at least one trade-off
- Explanation of how the decision affects other priorities
- Statement of the stronger option for the situation
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly compare the choices using cost and consequence evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
