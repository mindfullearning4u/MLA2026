Guided Practice

Scenario: Compare these two planning choices:

  • Choice A: Save $75 each month for future transportation costs.
  • Choice B: Spend the $75 each month on short-term entertainment.

Respond:

  • State which choice better supports future planning.
  • Calculate how much would be saved after 8 months.
  • Explain what future need the saving choice could support.
  • Describe one likely consequence of not planning ahead.
  • Justify your answer clearly.
