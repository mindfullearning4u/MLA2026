Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Financial Planning for Life Stages

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare financial planning choices across life stages and justify the stronger decision.

Required evidence:
- Identification of the stronger planning choice
- One correct numerical calculation
- Explanation of a future goal or expense
- Description of a likely consequence
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly compare the two options using future planning evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
