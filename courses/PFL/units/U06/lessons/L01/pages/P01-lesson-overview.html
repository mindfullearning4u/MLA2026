Lesson 1: Financial Planning for Life Stages

In this lesson, you will examine how financial planning changes across life stages. Planning for education, career, and future expenses requires comparing options, setting priorities, and preparing for long-term responsibilities.

You will study how goals and expected costs connect to life-stage planning, compare financial priorities at different points in life, and explain why planning ahead can reduce future financial pressure.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain why financial planning changes across life stages
  • Identify common future expenses related to education, career, and adulthood
  • Compare short-term and long-term financial priorities
  • Justify planning choices using future needs and consequences
