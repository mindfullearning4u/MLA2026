Lesson Checkpoint | Financial Planning for Life Stages
This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare life-stage financial choices and justify the stronger planning decision.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Identify which option better supports future planning.
  • Include one correct savings or cost calculation.
  • Explain how the decision connects to a future expense or goal.
  • Describe one likely consequence of the weaker choice.
  • Provide a justified final conclusion.
