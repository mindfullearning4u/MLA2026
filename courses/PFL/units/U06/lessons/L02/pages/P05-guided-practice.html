Guided Practice

Scenario: Compare these two situations:

  • Choice A: Pay $60 each month for property insurance.
  • Choice B: Pay no premium, but risk covering a $950 loss alone.

Respond:

  • Calculate the annual premium cost for Choice A.
  • Compare the premium total to the possible loss.
  • Explain what risk the insurance is meant to reduce.
  • State which choice better supports protection planning.
  • Justify your answer clearly.
