Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Insurance and Risk Protection

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare insurance choices and explain how protection reduces financial risk.

Required evidence:
- One correct premium or cost calculation
- Identification of the financial risk involved
- Comparison of protected and unprotected outcomes
- Explanation of how insurance supports stability
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly explain the protection comparison using evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
