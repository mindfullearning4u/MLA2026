💡Worked Example | Comparing Protected and Unprotected Risk

Scenario: One driver has auto insurance with a $75 monthly premium. Another driver has no insurance and later faces a $1,200 repair bill after an accident.

Step 1: Calculate the premium cost over 12 months.
$75 x 12 = $900

Step 2: Compare the two financial outcomes.
The insured driver pays $900 in premiums. The uninsured driver faces a sudden $1,200 cost.

Step 3: Identify the protection benefit.
Insurance helps reduce the financial shock of an unexpected loss.

Best-supported conclusion: The insured option better supports financial protection because it prepares for risk and reduces the chance of a large unexpected cost.
