Lesson 2: Insurance and Risk Protection

In this lesson, you will examine how insurance helps protect people from financial risk. Insurance decisions require comparing coverage, costs, and possible consequences when unexpected events happen.

You will study basic insurance types, analyze what risks they are designed to reduce, and explain why protection choices matter in long-term financial planning.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify basic types of insurance
  • Explain how insurance reduces financial risk
  • Compare different protection needs in common situations
  • Justify why insurance can support financial stability
