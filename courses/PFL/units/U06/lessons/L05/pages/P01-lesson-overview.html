Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Financial Plan and Reflection

In this lesson, you will prepare for the Unit 6 assessment by bringing together planning, insurance, major financial choices, and financial responsibility. This lesson focuses on applying what you have learned to a realistic personal finance situation.

You will review how to justify decisions, compare consequences, and build a short financial plan that reflects responsible thinking.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Connect Unit 6 concepts to one financial planning situation
  • Compare choices using cost, risk, and responsibility
  • Explain why some decisions better support long-term stability
  • Prepare a clear response for the unit assessment
