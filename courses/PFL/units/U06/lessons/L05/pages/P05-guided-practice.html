Guided Practice

Scenario: A person must plan for insurance, transportation, and a future expense. They can either:

  • Choice A: Save $100 each month and keep a lower-cost transportation plan.
  • Choice B: Spend the $100 and choose a higher-cost transportation plan immediately.

Respond:

  • Calculate the savings total after 6 months for Choice A.
  • Explain one advantage of planning ahead.
  • Describe one risk created by Choice B.
  • State which option better supports long-term financial responsibility.
  • Justify your answer clearly.
