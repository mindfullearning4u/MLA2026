Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Unit Assessment: Financial Plan and Reflection

This checkpoint verifies your readiness to complete the Unit 6 assessment by applying financial planning, risk protection, and responsibility concepts.

Required evidence:
- One correct numerical calculation
- Comparison of at least two financial choices
- Explanation of one consequence or risk
- Statement of the stronger long-term option
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly apply Unit 6 concepts using evidence and reasoning, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
