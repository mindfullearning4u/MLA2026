Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Financial Responsibility and Accountability

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare responsible and irresponsible financial choices and explain the consequences of each.

Required evidence:
- One correct cost calculation
- Identification of at least one consequence
- Explanation of why accountability matters
- Statement of the stronger responsible choice
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly explain the responsible choice using evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
