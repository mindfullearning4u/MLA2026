Worked Example | Comparing Responsible and Irresponsible Choices

Scenario: One person pays a $45 bill on time each month. Another ignores the same bill and later owes a $25 late fee.

Step 1: Identify the original obligation.
Both people were responsible for the same $45 bill.

Step 2: Calculate the new total for the late payment.
$45 + $25 = $70

Step 3: Compare the outcomes.
The on-time payment avoids extra cost. The late payment creates a higher total and a preventable consequence.

Best-supported conclusion: The responsible choice is stronger because it meets the obligation, avoids extra cost, and supports financial accountability.
