Lesson Checkpoint | Financial Responsibility and Accountability
This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare responsible and irresponsible financial choices and justify the stronger decision.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Include one correct cost calculation.
  • Identify at least one consequence of the weaker choice.
  • Explain why accountability matters in the scenario.
  • State which option is financially responsible.
  • Provide a justified final conclusion.
