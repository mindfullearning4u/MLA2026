Guided Practice

Scenario: Compare these two actions:

  • Choice A: Pay a $60 obligation on time.
  • Choice B: Pay late and add a $15 fee.

Respond:

  • Calculate the total cost of the late payment.
  • Explain one consequence of failing to act responsibly.
  • Describe why accountability matters in this example.
  • State which choice is stronger.
  • Justify your answer clearly.
