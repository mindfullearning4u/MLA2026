🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Unit Assessment: Financial Decision Scenario
This checkpoint verifies your ability to analyze a financial situation and justify one decision using Unit 1 concepts.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Identify the need, want, and goal in the scenario.
  • State the opportunity cost.
  • Explain one short-term and one long-term consequence.
  • Choose the strongest financial decision.
  • Justify the answer using evidence from the scenario.
