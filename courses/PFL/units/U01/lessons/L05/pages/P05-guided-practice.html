Guided Practice

Scenario: A student has $95 and must make a decision this month.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

  • Required class fee: $35
  • Optional concert ticket: $40
  • Savings goal for a phone repair: $100 total, with $20 already saved

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

Respond:

  • Identify the need, want, and goal in the scenario.
  • State the opportunity cost of buying the concert ticket.
  • Explain one short-term and one long-term consequence.
  • Choose the stronger financial decision.
  • Justify the decision using Unit 1 reasoning.

⚠️Reminder: Show your reasoning clearly and use the scenario details in your response.
