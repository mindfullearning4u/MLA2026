Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Financial Decision Scenario

In this lesson, you will prepare for the Unit 1 assessment by applying all Unit 1 concepts to a financial scenario.

This lesson is not focused on new instruction. Instead, you will combine trade-offs, priorities, goals, and decision strategies to justify a financial choice using reasoning.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Analyze a financial scenario using Unit 1 concepts
  • Compare options using priorities and consequences
  • Connect financial choices to goals
  • Justify a final decision with evidence
