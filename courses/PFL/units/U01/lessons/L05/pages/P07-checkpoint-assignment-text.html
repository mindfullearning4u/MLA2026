Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Unit Assessment: Financial Decision Scenario

This checkpoint verifies your ability to analyze a financial situation and justify a decision using Unit 1 concepts.

Required evidence:
- Identification of the need, want, and goal in the scenario
- The opportunity cost of the decision
- One short-term consequence
- One long-term consequence
- One justified final decision using evidence

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly analyze the scenario and justify a decision, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
