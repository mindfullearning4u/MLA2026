Concept | Financial Decision Scenario

In this lesson, you are not learning a new concept. Instead, you are combining all concepts from Unit 01 to evaluate a financial decision.

A strong Unit 1 response should answer these questions:

  • What are the options?
  • What is the opportunity cost?
  • Which expenses are needs and which are wants?
  • What short-term and long-term consequences matter most?
  • Does the decision support a financial goal or weaken it?

Strong reasoning connects all of these ideas into one clear explanation. The best-supported decision is not always the easiest or most exciting choice. It is the choice that best matches priorities, goals, and long-term financial health.

⚠️ Common mistake: listing financial facts without using them to justify one final decision.
