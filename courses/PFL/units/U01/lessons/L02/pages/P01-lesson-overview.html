Lesson 2: Needs, Wants, and Financial Priorities

In this lesson, you will distinguish between needs, wants, and financial priorities. Strong financial choices depend on knowing what should come first.

You will compare spending options, evaluate which expenses matter most, and justify decisions based on value and financial responsibility.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Define needs, wants, and priorities
  • Compare spending choices using evidence
  • Explain why priorities affect financial outcomes
  • Justify a spending decision based on value
