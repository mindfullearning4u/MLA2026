🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Needs, Wants, and Financial Priorities
This checkpoint verifies your ability to classify expenses and justify a priority-based spending decision.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Identify at least two needs and two wants from the scenario.
  • Rank the expenses in order of priority.
  • Explain one likely consequence of choosing wants before needs.
  • State the strongest financial decision.
  • Justify your answer clearly.
