🎯 Guided Practice

Scenario: A student has $70 and must decide how to use it this week.

  • School activity fee: $30
  • Replacement charger: $20
  • Movie trip: $15
  • Fast food: $12

Respond:

  • Identify which expenses are needs and which are wants.
  • Rank the expenses in priority order.
  • Explain one consequence of poor prioritization.
  • State which items should be paid first.
  • Justify your final spending plan.
