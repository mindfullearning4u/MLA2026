Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Needs, Wants, and Financial Priorities

This checkpoint verifies your ability to sort expenses into needs and wants and justify a priority-based financial decision.

Required evidence:
- At least two needs from the scenario
- At least two wants from the scenario
- A ranked list of priorities
- One consequence of poor prioritization
- One justified final decision

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly classify expenses and justify priorities, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
