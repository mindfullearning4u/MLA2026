Lesson 1: Financial Choices and Consequences

In this lesson, you will study how financial choices lead to consequences. Every spending, saving, or goal-setting decision has a trade-off.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • You will examine opportunity cost, compare options, and explain how one choice can affect both short-term and long-term financial outcomes.
