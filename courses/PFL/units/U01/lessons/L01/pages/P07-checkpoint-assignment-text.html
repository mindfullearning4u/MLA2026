Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Financial Choices and Consequences

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare two financial options and justify a decision using opportunity cost and consequences.

Required evidence:
- The two financial options in the scenario
- The opportunity cost of the decision
- One short-term consequence
- One long-term consequence
- One justified final decision

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly compare options and justify a decision, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
