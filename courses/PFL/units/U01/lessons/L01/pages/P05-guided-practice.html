🎯 Guided Practice

Option 1: Jordan has $25 and can either buy a game add-on or save the money for a school field trip fee next week.

Option 2: Mia has $80 and can either buy new shoes now or keep the money for gas costs she expects this month.

Choose ONE option and respond:

  • Identify the two choices.
  • State the opportunity cost of each choice.
  • Identify one short-term consequence.
  • Identify one long-term consequence.
  • Justify which option is the stronger financial decision.
