💡Worked Example | Building a Measurable Goal

Scenario: Sam wants $240 for a summer class fee in 6 months.

Step 1: Identify the total amount.
Sam needs $240.

Step 2: Identify the timeline.
Sam has 6 months.

Step 3: Calculate the monthly target.
$240 divided by 6 months = $40 per month.

Step 4: Evaluate decisions.
If Sam spends $25 each month on optional purchases, that leaves less money available for the goal.

Best-supported goal statement: "I will save $240 in 6 months by setting aside $40 each month for my summer class fee."
