Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Short-Term and Long-Term Financial Goals

This checkpoint verifies your ability to create and justify a measurable financial goal.

Required evidence:
- Identification of whether the goal is short-term or long-term
- The monthly savings calculation
- One supporting decision
- One decision that would hurt the goal
- One complete measurable goal statement

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly calculate and justify the goal, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
