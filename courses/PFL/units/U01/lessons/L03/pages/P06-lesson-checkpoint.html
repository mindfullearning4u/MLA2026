🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Short-Term and Long-Term Financial Goals
This checkpoint verifies your ability to classify a financial goal, calculate a target amount, and justify decisions that support the goal.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Identify whether the goal is short-term or long-term.
  • Show the monthly savings calculation.
  • Write one decision that supports the goal.
  • Write one decision that works against the goal.
  • Write one complete measurable goal statement.
