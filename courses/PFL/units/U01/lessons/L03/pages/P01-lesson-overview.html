Lesson 3: Short-Term and Long-Term Financial Goals

In this lesson, you will set short-term and long-term financial goals. Good goals help people make decisions with purpose instead of spending without a plan.

You will compare goal timelines, identify measurable targets, and explain how goals affect daily financial choices.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Define short-term and long-term financial goals
  • Explain how goals shape decisions
  • Compare realistic and unrealistic goals
  • Write a measurable financial goal
