Lesson 4: Decision-Making Strategies in Personal Finance

In this lesson, you will use decision-making strategies to compare financial options. Strong choices are based on evidence, not impulse.

You will evaluate options, weigh consequences, and justify decisions using cost, value, timing, and future impact.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain key ideas related to Decision-Making Strategies in Personal Finance
  • Identify important details about Evaluating options and making informed financial decisions
  • Compare financial choices, trade-offs, or outcomes connected to this lesson
  • Apply the lesson ideas in a clear, supported financial decision
