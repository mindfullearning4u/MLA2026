🎯 Guided Practice

Scenario: A student must choose between two transportation options for the next month.

  • Option A: ride-share trips costing about $75 total
  • Option B: a bus pass costing $48 total

Respond:

  • Identify the financial decision being made.
  • Compare the total cost of both options.
  • Explain one advantage of each option.
  • State one likely consequence of each option.
  • Justify which option is the stronger financial decision.
