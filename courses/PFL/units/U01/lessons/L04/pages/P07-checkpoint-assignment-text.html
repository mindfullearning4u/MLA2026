Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Decision-Making Strategies in Personal Finance

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare financial options and justify a decision using evidence and consequences.

Required evidence:
- Identification of the financial decision
- Comparison of both options
- One advantage and one consequence for each option
- One justified final decision

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly compare options and justify the decision, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
