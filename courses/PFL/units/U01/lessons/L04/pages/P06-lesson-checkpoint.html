🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Decision-Making Strategies in Personal Finance
This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare financial options and justify a decision using evidence.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Identify the decision and the two options.
  • Compare the cost of both options.
  • Explain one advantage and one consequence for each option.
  • Choose the stronger financial decision.
  • Justify the choice with evidence.
