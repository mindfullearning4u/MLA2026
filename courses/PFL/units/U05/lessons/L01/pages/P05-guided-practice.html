Guided Practice

Scenario: Compare these two choices:

  • Choice A: $600 remains at $600
  • Choice B: $600 grows to $675 over time

Respond:

  • State which choice shows financial growth.
  • Calculate the amount of growth.
  • Explain why time matters in this comparison.
  • State which choice is stronger for long-term growth.
  • Justify your answer clearly.
