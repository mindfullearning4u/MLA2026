Lesson 1: Basics of Investing and Financial Growth

In this lesson, you will examine why investing matters and how money can grow over time. Investing involves decision-making because growth opportunities must be compared to financial goals and time horizons.

You will study basic investment growth, compare saving and investing outcomes, and explain why long-term thinking matters in financial growth decisions.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain why investing matters
  • Describe how investing can support financial growth
  • Compare short-term and long-term growth choices
  • Justify why time matters in investment decisions
