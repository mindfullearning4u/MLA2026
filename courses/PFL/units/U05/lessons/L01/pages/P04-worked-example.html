💡 Worked Example | Comparing Growth Over Time

Scenario: One student keeps $500 without growth. Another invests $500 and the value grows to $560 over time.

Step 1: Compare starting amounts.
Both students begin with $500.

Step 2: Compare later values.
One total stays at $500, while the other grows to $560.

Step 3: Calculate the growth difference.
$560 - $500 = $60 growth

Best-supported conclusion: The investing option shows stronger financial growth because the money increased by $60 over time.
