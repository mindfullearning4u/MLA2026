Lesson 2: Types of Investments

In this lesson, you will compare common investment types, including stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and retirement accounts. Different investment tools support different goals, growth patterns, and levels of flexibility.

You will evaluate investment types, compare their purposes, and explain which option best fits a stated financial goal.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify major types of investments
  • Explain the purpose of different investment options
  • Compare investment choices based on financial goals
  • Justify which type of investment best fits a scenario
