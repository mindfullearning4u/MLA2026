Concept | Types of Investments

There is more than one way to invest money. Different investment types exist because investors may have different goals, timelines, and preferences for how money is managed.

Examples in this lesson include:

  • stocks
  • bonds
  • mutual funds
  • retirement accounts

Strong investment decisions compare the purpose of the goal to the type of investment being considered. The strongest option is not simply the one that sounds most exciting. It is the one that best fits the actual financial plan.

⚠️ Common mistake: choosing an investment type without first checking whether it matches the intended financial goal and timeline.
