💡 Worked Example | Matching an Investment Type to a Goal

Scenario: One person wants to invest for retirement over many years. Another wants one investment made up of multiple assets rather than a single company.

Step 1: Match the long-term retirement goal.
A retirement account is the strongest match because it is designed for long-term future planning.

Step 2: Match the multiple-asset preference.
A mutual fund is the strongest match because it combines multiple assets into one investment.

Best-supported conclusion: Strong investment choices match the investment type to the actual purpose of the money.
