💡 Worked Example | Comparing Long-Term Growth

Scenario: One investment grows from $800 to $920. Another grows from $800 to $980 over a longer time period.

Step 1: Calculate the first growth amount.
$920 - $800 = $120 growth

Step 2: Calculate the second growth amount.
$980 - $800 = $180 growth

Step 3: Compare the outcomes.
The second option produced $60 more growth.

Best-supported conclusion: The longer growth period led to a stronger result because time allowed more value to build.
