Lesson 4: Compound Interest and Long-Term Growth

In this lesson, you will explore how money can grow more quickly over time through compound growth. The effect becomes stronger when growth builds on earlier growth across longer time periods.

You will compare growth amounts, calculate long-term change, and explain why time is one of the most powerful factors in investment growth.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain compound interest and long-term growth
  • Compare short-term and long-term growth outcomes
  • Use calculations to show growth over time
  • Justify why earlier investing can lead to stronger results
