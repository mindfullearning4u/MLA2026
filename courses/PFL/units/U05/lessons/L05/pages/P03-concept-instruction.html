Assessment Readiness | Investment Decision Scenario

This lesson is a unit assessment readiness lesson. You are expected to combine the full unit, not focus on one investment idea at a time.

To succeed, you should be able to:

  • explain why investing can support growth over time
  • match investment types to financial goals
  • compare risk and return using diversification reasoning
  • use long-term growth evidence in the decision
  • justify a final recommendation clearly

Strong responses compare multiple factors and explain why one investment choice is the stronger fit for the stated goal.

⚠️ Important: unsupported answers do not show mastery. Your recommendation must connect directly to the scenario details and financial evidence.
