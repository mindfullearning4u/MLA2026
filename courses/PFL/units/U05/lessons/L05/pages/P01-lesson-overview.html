Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Investment Decision Scenario

In this lesson, you will bring together the ideas from this unit to evaluate an investment scenario. You will compare options, consider risk and growth, and justify a recommendation using financial evidence.

This lesson prepares you for unit mastery by requiring you to apply investment growth, type comparison, diversification, and long-term reasoning in one scenario.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Analyze a full investment scenario
  • Compare investment choices using growth and risk reasoning
  • Use evidence to explain the stronger option
  • Justify a final investment recommendation clearly
