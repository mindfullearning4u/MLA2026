Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Unit Assessment: Investment Decision Scenario

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare investment options using growth, risk, diversification, and goal fit.

Required evidence:
- Identification of the stronger option for the stated goal
- Explanation of diversification and risk comparison
- At least one clear strength and one clear concern discussed
- One justified final recommendation

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly justify the recommendation using investment evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
