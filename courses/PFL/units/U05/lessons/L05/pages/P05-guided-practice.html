Scenario: Compare these two investment approaches:

  • Approach A: all money in one option with higher possible growth but higher dependence on one result
  • Approach B: money spread across multiple options with steadier long-term growth

Respond:

  • Identify which approach is more diversified.
  • Explain one risk in Approach A.
  • Explain one strength in Approach B.
  • State which approach is stronger for long-term stability.
  • Justify your recommendation clearly.
