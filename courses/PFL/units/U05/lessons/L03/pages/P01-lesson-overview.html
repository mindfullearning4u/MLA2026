Lesson 3: Risk, Return, and Diversification

In this lesson, you will explore how investment decisions balance risk and possible return. Investment choices are stronger when they consider both the chance of growth and the possibility of loss.

You will compare risk and return, explain why diversification matters, and justify stronger investment strategies using evidence.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain the relationship between risk and return
  • Describe the purpose of diversification
  • Compare investment choices using risk and reward reasoning
  • Justify a stronger diversification strategy
