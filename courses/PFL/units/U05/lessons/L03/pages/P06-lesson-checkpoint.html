🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Risk, Return, and Diversification
This checkpoint verifies your ability to explain how diversification affects investment risk.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Identify the more diversified investment choice.
  • Explain why diversification can reduce risk.
  • Describe one weakness of relying on a single investment.
  • State the stronger strategy for risk reduction.
  • Justify the conclusion clearly.
