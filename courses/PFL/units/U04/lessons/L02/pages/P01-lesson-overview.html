Lesson 2: Types of Credit and Loans

In this lesson, you will compare major borrowing options, including credit cards, personal loans, student loans, and mortgages. Different types of credit are designed for different purposes and carry different responsibilities.

You will evaluate borrowing options, compare their uses, and explain which type of credit is the strongest fit for a given financial need.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify common types of credit and loans
  • Explain the purpose of different borrowing options
  • Compare credit choices based on need and context
  • Justify which type of borrowing best fits a scenario
