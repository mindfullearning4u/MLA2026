💡 Worked Example | Matching a Loan Type to a Need

Scenario: One student needs borrowing for tuition. Another adult needs borrowing to purchase a home.

Step 1: Match the first purpose.
Tuition is best matched with a student loan because the borrowing is connected to education costs.

Step 2: Match the second purpose.
Buying a home is best matched with a mortgage because mortgages are designed for property purchases.

Best-supported conclusion: The strongest borrowing choice is the one that fits the actual financial need and long-term repayment purpose.
