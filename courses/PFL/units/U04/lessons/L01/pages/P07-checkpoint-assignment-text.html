Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Understanding Credit and Credit Scores

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare credit behaviors and explain their long-term effects.

Required evidence:
- Identification of the stronger credit behavior
- Explanation of why it is stronger
- One likely consequence of weaker credit behavior
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly connect credit behavior to future borrowing outcomes, the checkpoint is not complete.
