Lesson 1: Understanding Credit and Credit Scores

In this lesson, you will learn how credit works and why credit scores matter in borrowing decisions. Credit can create opportunities, but it also creates responsibility and long-term consequences.

You will examine how credit is used, identify factors that affect credit scores, and explain why strong credit behavior supports better financial outcomes.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain what credit is
  • Describe the purpose of a credit score
  • Identify behaviors that can strengthen or weaken credit
  • Justify why responsible credit use matters
