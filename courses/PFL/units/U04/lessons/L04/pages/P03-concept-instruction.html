Concept | Responsible Credit Use and Debt Management

Responsible credit use means borrowing only when the debt can be repaid through a realistic plan. Debt management is the process of making sure that repayment stays organized and controlled over time.

Warning signs of weak debt management include:

  • ignoring payment deadlines
  • borrowing again to cover older debt
  • making decisions without checking repayment ability
  • focusing only on immediate spending power

Strong debt decisions compare the benefit of borrowing now with the responsibility of repayment later. The strongest strategy is the one that protects financial stability over time.

⚠️ Common mistake: treating borrowed money as if repayment will be easy later without checking the actual plan.
