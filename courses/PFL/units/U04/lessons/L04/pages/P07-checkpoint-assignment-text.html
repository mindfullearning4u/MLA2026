Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Responsible Credit Use and Debt Management

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare debt management choices and explain the stronger repayment strategy.

Required evidence:
- Identification of the stronger strategy
- Explanation of why it is stronger
- At least one risk in the weaker strategy
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly explain the long-term consequences of the repayment choices, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
