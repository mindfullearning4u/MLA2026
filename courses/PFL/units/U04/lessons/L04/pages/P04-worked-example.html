💡 Worked Example | Comparing Two Repayment Behaviors

Scenario: Borrower A pays the required amount on time each month. Borrower B skips payments and adds new borrowing to cover older bills.

Step 1: Compare the behaviors.
Borrower A follows a repayment plan. Borrower B increases long-term risk by failing to manage the debt directly.

Step 2: Compare likely outcomes.
Borrower A is more likely to reduce debt over time. Borrower B is more likely to create a deeper debt problem.

Best-supported conclusion: Borrower A shows the stronger debt management strategy because the repayment plan is responsible and sustainable.
