Lesson 4: Responsible Credit Use and Debt Management

In this lesson, you will focus on avoiding debt traps and making repayment choices that protect long-term financial stability. Borrowing becomes risky when repayment is ignored or when debt grows faster than it can be managed.

You will compare repayment strategies, evaluate risky borrowing patterns, and explain how responsible credit use supports better outcomes over time.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain what responsible credit use means
  • Identify signs of weak debt management
  • Compare repayment choices and consequences
  • Justify a stronger debt management strategy
