Lesson 3: Interest, Fees, and Cost of Borrowing

In this lesson, you will examine how borrowing costs grow through interest and fees. A loan is never only about the amount borrowed because the total repayment can be much larger over time.

You will calculate borrowing costs, compare repayment totals, and explain how interest and fees affect financial decisions.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain how interest affects borrowing cost
  • Recognize how fees can increase total repayment
  • Calculate simple total borrowing amounts in a scenario
  • Justify which borrowing option is less costly
