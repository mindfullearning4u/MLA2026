💡 Worked Example | Comparing Total Borrowing Cost

Scenario: Option A borrows $500 with $40 in interest and a $10 fee. Option B borrows $500 with $25 in interest and no fee.

Step 1: Calculate total repayment for Option A.
$500 + $40 + $10 = $550

Step 2: Calculate total repayment for Option B.
$500 + $25 + $0 = $525

Step 3: Compare the totals.
Option B costs $25 less overall than Option A.

Best-supported conclusion: Option B is the stronger borrowing choice because the total repayment is lower.
