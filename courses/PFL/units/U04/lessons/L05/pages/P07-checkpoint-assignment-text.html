Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Unit Assessment: Credit and Loan Scenario

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare loan options using total repayment, risk, and responsible borrowing reasoning.

Required evidence:
- Total repayment calculation for both options
- Identification of the lower-cost option
- Correct difference in total cost
- One justified final recommendation

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly use the borrowing calculations to justify the recommendation, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
