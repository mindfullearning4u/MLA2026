🎯 Guided Practice

Scenario: A borrower needs $750. Compare these options:

  • Option A: $750 borrowed, $72 interest, $15 fee
  • Option B: $750 borrowed, $48 interest, $8 fee

Respond:

  • Calculate total repayment for both options.
  • State which option costs less overall.
  • Calculate the difference in total borrowing cost.
  • Explain one reason the lower-cost option is stronger.
  • Justify your final recommendation clearly.
