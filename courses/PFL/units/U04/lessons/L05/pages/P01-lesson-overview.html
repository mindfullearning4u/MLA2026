Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Credit and Loan Scenario

In this lesson, you will bring together the ideas from this unit to evaluate a credit and loan scenario. You will compare borrowing options, calculate costs, and justify a repayment-focused recommendation.

This lesson prepares you for unit mastery by requiring you to apply credit knowledge, loan comparison, total cost reasoning, and debt management in one scenario.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Analyze a full borrowing scenario
  • Compare credit and loan options using evidence
  • Use total repayment reasoning in a decision
  • Justify a final recommendation clearly
