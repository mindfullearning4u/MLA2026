Assessment Readiness | Credit and Loan Scenario

This lesson is a unit assessment readiness lesson. You are expected to combine the full unit, not focus on only one part of borrowing at a time.

To succeed, you should be able to:

  • explain how credit behavior affects future borrowing
  • match credit or loan types to financial needs
  • calculate total borrowing cost with interest and fees
  • identify stronger repayment behavior
  • justify a final recommendation using evidence

Strong responses compare options directly and explain why one choice is safer, lower-cost, or more responsible.

⚠️ Important: unsupported answers do not show mastery. Your recommendation must connect directly to the scenario details and numbers.
