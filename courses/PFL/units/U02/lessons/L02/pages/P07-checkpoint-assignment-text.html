Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Employment and Career Income Factors

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare career options using income factors and preparation requirements.

Required evidence:
- One advantage of each option
- Explanation of how education or training affects income comparison
- One justified immediate-income decision
- One justified long-term-income decision

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly compare the options with evidence, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
