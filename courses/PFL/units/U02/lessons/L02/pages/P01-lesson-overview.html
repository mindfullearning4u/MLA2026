Lesson 2: Employment and Career Income Factors

In this lesson, you will study how education, skills, and job choices can affect income potential.

You will compare employment options, evaluate career factors, and justify which path better supports earnings goals.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify career factors that influence income
  • Explain how skills and education affect earnings
  • Compare job options using evidence
  • Justify a career-income decision
