Lesson 4: Basics of Taxes and Financial Responsibility

In this lesson, you will study the purpose of taxes and basic financial responsibility related to paying them.

You will examine common tax types, compare how taxes affect earnings, and explain why understanding taxes is part of responsible financial decision-making.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain the purpose of taxes
  • Identify common tax types
  • Describe how taxes affect financial outcomes
  • Justify why tax awareness matters in personal finance
