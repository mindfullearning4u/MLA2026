💡 Worked Example | Full Income Scenario

Scenario: A worker earns $17 per hour for 18 hours in one week. Total deductions are $42.

Step 1: Calculate gross income.
$17 x 18 = $306 gross income

Step 2: Subtract deductions.
$306 - $42 = $264 net income

Step 3: Interpret the result.
The worker earned $306 before deductions but can only make spending decisions using the $264 take-home amount.

Best-supported conclusion: The correct financial analysis must use the net income, not just the gross pay, when evaluating the final outcome.
