Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Income and Tax Analysis

In this lesson, you will prepare for the Unit 2 assessment by combining income concepts, paycheck analysis, and tax reasoning.

This lesson is not focused on new instruction. Instead, you will compare income scenarios, interpret deductions, and justify conclusions using numerical evidence.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Compare income choices using earnings data
  • Interpret gross pay, deductions, and net pay
  • Explain how taxes affect financial outcomes
  • Justify a decision with calculations and reasoning
