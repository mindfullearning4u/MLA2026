Unit 02 | Lesson 5

Concept Instruction

In this lesson, you are not learning a new concept. Instead, you are combining all concepts from Unit 02 to analyze an income scenario.

Strong Unit 2 reasoning should answer these questions:

  • How is the income earned?
  • What factors affect the earnings amount?
  • What is the gross income?
  • What deductions or taxes reduce the total?
  • What is the final financial outcome after those reductions?

Strong analysis uses calculations, not guesses. The best-supported conclusion depends on the actual numbers and the reasoning behind them.

⚠️Common mistake: using gross income as if it were the amount available to spend.
