Lesson 1: Types of Income and Earnings

In this lesson, you will examine different ways people earn income. Income can come from wages, salaries, hourly work, or other sources.

You will compare income types, analyze how earnings are calculated, and explain how income choices affect financial outcomes.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify common types of income
  • Explain the difference between hourly wages and salary
  • Compare income examples using numbers
  • Justify which income option fits a scenario better
