🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Types of Income and Earnings
This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare income options and justify a decision using numerical evidence.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Show the weekly earnings calculation for both options.
  • Identify the option with the higher hourly rate.
  • Identify the option with the higher weekly total.
  • Choose the stronger option for the stated goal.
  • Justify the answer with evidence.
