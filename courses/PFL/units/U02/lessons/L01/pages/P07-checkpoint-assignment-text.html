Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Types of Income and Earnings

This checkpoint verifies your ability to compare income options using calculations and evidence.

Required evidence:
- Weekly earnings calculation for both options
- Identification of the higher hourly rate
- Identification of the higher weekly total
- One justified final decision

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly compare the income options using numbers, the checkpoint is not complete.
