💡 Worked Example | Comparing Two Earnings Options

Scenario: Eli is comparing two part-time job options.

  • Job A: $15 per hour for 12 hours each week
  • Job B: $13 per hour for 18 hours each week

Step 1: Calculate weekly earnings.

  • Job A: 15 x 12 = $180 per week
  • Job B: 13 x 18 = $234 per week

Step 2: Compare outcomes.
Job A has the higher hourly rate, but Job B leads to higher weekly earnings because it offers more hours.

Best-supported conclusion: If Eli's main goal is earning more money each week, Job B is the stronger income option.
