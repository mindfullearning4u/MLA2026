🎯 Guided Practice

Scenario: A student compares these two jobs:

  • Option A: $14 per hour for 10 hours each week
  • Option B: $12 per hour for 16 hours each week

Respond:

  • Calculate weekly earnings for both options.
  • Identify which job has the higher hourly rate.
  • Identify which job has the higher weekly total.
  • State which option is stronger if the goal is higher weekly income.
  • Justify your choice using numbers.
