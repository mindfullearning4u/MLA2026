💡 Worked Example | Gross Pay to Net Pay

Scenario: Jamie earns $16 per hour and works 20 hours this week. Taxes and other deductions total $48.

Step 1: Calculate gross income.
$16 x 20 = $320 gross income

Step 2: Subtract deductions.
$320 - $48 = $272 net income

Step 3: Interpret the result.
Jamie earned $320 before deductions but can only plan spending using the $272 take-home amount.
