Lesson 3: Understanding Paychecks and Deductions

In this lesson, you will analyze how paychecks are structured. Gross income is not the same as the amount a worker takes home.

You will compare gross and net income, interpret deductions, and explain how taxes and other reductions affect earnings.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Define gross income and net income
  • Identify common paycheck deductions
  • Calculate take-home pay in a basic scenario
  • Explain how deductions affect financial planning
