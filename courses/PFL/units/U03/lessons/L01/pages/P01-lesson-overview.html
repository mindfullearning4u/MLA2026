Lesson 1: Creating and Managing a Budget

In this lesson, you will learn how a budget helps a person organize money decisions. A budget tracks income, planned spending, and the difference between what comes in and what goes out.

You will compare financial choices, calculate totals, and explain how a budget supports stronger money management.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain the purpose of a budget
  • Track income and expenses in a simple budget
  • Calculate whether money is left over or not
  • Justify spending decisions using budget evidence
