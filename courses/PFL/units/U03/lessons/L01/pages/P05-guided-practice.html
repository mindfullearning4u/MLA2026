Scenario: Jordan has $120 to plan for one week. Expected expenses are:

  • $30 for transportation
  • $22 for meals
  • $28 for personal spending
  • $20 for savings

Respond:

  • Calculate the total planned expenses.
  • Calculate how much money is left after expenses.
  • State whether the budget is working.
  • Explain one reason the savings category matters.
  • Justify whether Jordan should keep or change the plan.
