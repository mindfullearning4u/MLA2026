Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Creating and Managing a Budget

This checkpoint verifies your ability to organize income and expenses into a simple budget and explain the financial result.

Required evidence:
- Total income shown clearly
- Total expenses shown clearly
- Correct calculation of the final budget result
- One justified conclusion about whether the plan works

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly use numbers to justify the budget decision, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
