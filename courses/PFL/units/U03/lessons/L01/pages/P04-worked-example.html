💡 Worked Example | Building a Simple Weekly Budget

Scenario: Maya earns $95 each week from part-time work. She plans to spend:

  • $25 on transportation
  • $18 on lunch
  • $20 on entertainment
  • $15 on savings

Step 1: Add planned expenses.
$25 + $18 + $20 + $15 = $78

Step 2: Compare income to expenses.
$95 - $78 = $17 left over

Step 3: Interpret the result.
Maya's budget works because her planned expenses are less than her weekly income.

Best-supported conclusion: Maya can follow this plan and still have $17 available for another goal, extra saving, or an unexpected cost.
