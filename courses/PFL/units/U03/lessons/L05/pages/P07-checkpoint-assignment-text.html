Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Unit Assessment: Budget Development Scenario

This checkpoint verifies your ability to organize a budget scenario, evaluate an added spending decision, and justify a final recommendation.

Required evidence:
- Correct total of planned budget categories
- Correct remaining-money calculations
- Explanation of at least one consequence
- One justified final recommendation

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly use the numbers in the scenario to justify the final decision, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
