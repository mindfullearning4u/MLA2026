Assessment Readiness | Budget Development Scenario

This lesson is a unit assessment readiness lesson. You are expected to combine the full unit, not focus on only one concept at a time.

To succeed, you should be able to:

  • organize a simple budget correctly
  • compare saving and spending choices
  • match account types to financial purposes
  • identify financial risks in weak spending decisions
  • justify a final recommendation with numerical evidence

Strong responses in this lesson explain not only what choice is made, but why that choice is stronger than the alternatives.

⚠️ Important: unsupported answers do not show mastery. Your reasoning must connect directly to the numbers and scenario details.
