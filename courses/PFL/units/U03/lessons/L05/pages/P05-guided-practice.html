🎯 Guided Practice

Scenario: Kim has $180 available this week. Planned categories are:

  • $40 transportation
  • $30 meals
  • $25 savings
  • $22 school expenses

Kim is also considering a $35 unplanned purchase.

Respond:

  • Calculate the total of the planned categories.
  • Calculate how much money remains before the unplanned purchase.
  • Calculate how much money remains after the unplanned purchase.
  • Explain one financial risk created by the purchase.
  • Justify whether Kim should keep or delay the purchase.
