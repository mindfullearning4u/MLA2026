Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Budget Development Scenario

In this lesson, you will bring together the ideas from this unit to analyze a budget scenario. You will use financial evidence to organize a plan, compare options, and justify decisions.

This lesson prepares you for unit mastery by requiring budgeting, saving, account use, and spending control in one applied task.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Analyze a full budget scenario
  • Use calculations to support a financial plan
  • Compare choices that affect saving and spending
  • Justify a final budget decision using evidence
