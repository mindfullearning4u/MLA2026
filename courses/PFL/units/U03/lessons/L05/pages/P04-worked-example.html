💡 Worked Example | Analyzing a Full Budget Scenario

Scenario: Rin has $150 for the week. Planned expenses are $35 for transportation, $25 for meals, and $20 for school supplies. Rin also wants to save $30 and is considering a $28 entertainment purchase.

Step 1: Add the planned budget categories before the entertainment choice.
$35 + $25 + $20 + $30 = $110

Step 2: Compare the remaining money.
$150 - $110 = $40 left before the entertainment choice

Step 3: Test the added purchase.
$40 - $28 = $12 left after the entertainment choice

Step 4: Evaluate the result.
The purchase fits mathematically, but it leaves very little flexibility. If Rin's goal is stronger financial security, delaying the purchase may be the better choice.

Best-supported conclusion: The final decision should be based on whether short-term entertainment is worth reducing the weekly financial cushion to $12.
