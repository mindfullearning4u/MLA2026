💡 Worked Example | Evaluating an Impulse Purchase

Scenario: Carlos has $60 left in his weekly budget. He plans to save $25 of it, but he wants to make an unplanned $30 purchase.

Step 1: Check the current plan.
If Carlos saves $25, only $35 is available for other spending.

Step 2: Compare the new choice.
If Carlos spends $30 on the unplanned item, only $5 remains after keeping the savings plan.

Step 3: Evaluate the consequence.
The purchase may fit the budget mathematically, but it sharply reduces flexibility for the rest of the week.

Best-supported conclusion: Carlos should compare whether the item is worth reducing the remaining budget so much, especially if the spending is not a priority.
