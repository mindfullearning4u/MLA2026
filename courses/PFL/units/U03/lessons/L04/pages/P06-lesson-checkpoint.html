🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Managing Spending and Avoiding Financial Pitfalls
This checkpoint verifies your ability to evaluate a spending decision and explain the consequences using evidence.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Use the numbers from the scenario correctly.
  • Show the effect of the spending decision on the budget.
  • Explain at least one financial risk.
  • State whether the decision is strong or weak.
  • Justify the conclusion clearly.
