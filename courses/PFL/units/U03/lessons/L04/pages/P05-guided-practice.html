🎯 Guided Practice

Scenario: Tia has $45 left in her weekly budget. She planned to save $20, but she is considering an unplanned $18 purchase.

Respond:

  • State how much money is left after saving $20.
  • State how much money would remain after the unplanned purchase.
  • Explain one risk of making the purchase.
  • Explain one reason the purchase might still be considered.
  • Justify whether the spending decision is strong or weak.
