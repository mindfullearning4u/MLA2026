Lesson 4: Managing Spending and Avoiding Financial Pitfalls

In this lesson, you will study how spending choices can support or weaken financial stability. Strong money management requires attention to habits, priorities, and the consequences of overspending.

You will compare spending options, evaluate impulse decisions, and explain how financial discipline supports long-term goals.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain how overspending can affect a budget
  • Identify common financial pitfalls
  • Compare short-term wants and long-term priorities
  • Justify a disciplined spending decision
