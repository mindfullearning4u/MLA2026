Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Managing Spending and Avoiding Financial Pitfalls

This checkpoint verifies your ability to analyze an unplanned spending decision and explain its budget impact.

Required evidence:
- Correct use of the scenario numbers
- Clear explanation of the budget result
- At least one financial risk identified
- One justified final conclusion

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly explain the consequences of the spending choice, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
