Lesson 2: Saving Strategies and Emergency Funds

In this lesson, you will examine why saving matters and how an emergency fund supports financial security. Saving decisions help prepare for both expected goals and unexpected costs.

You will compare saving options, calculate progress toward a target, and explain why emergency planning is part of strong money management.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain the purpose of saving
  • Describe how an emergency fund supports financial security
  • Calculate saving progress toward a target
  • Justify a saving strategy using financial reasoning
