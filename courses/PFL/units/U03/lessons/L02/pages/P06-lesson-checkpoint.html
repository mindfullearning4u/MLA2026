🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Saving Strategies and Emergency Funds
This checkpoint verifies your ability to calculate a savings plan and explain why it supports financial security.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • Show the savings goal.
  • Show the weekly or monthly saving amount.
  • Calculate the time needed to reach the goal.
  • Explain the purpose of an emergency fund.
  • Justify why the plan supports financial stability.
