💡 Worked Example | Building an Emergency Fund Plan

Scenario: Ava wants an emergency fund of $300. She can save $25 each week.

Step 1: Identify the target amount.
Emergency fund goal = $300

Step 2: Identify the weekly saving amount.
Weekly saving = $25

Step 3: Calculate the number of weeks needed.
$300 divided by $25 = 12 weeks

Best-supported conclusion: If Ava saves $25 each week, she can reach her emergency fund target in 12 weeks.
