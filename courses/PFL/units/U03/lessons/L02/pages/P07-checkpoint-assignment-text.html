Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Saving Strategies and Emergency Funds

This checkpoint verifies your ability to calculate a savings timeline and explain how saving supports financial security.

Required evidence:
- Savings goal identified clearly
- Saving amount identified clearly
- Correct calculation of the timeline
- Explanation of emergency fund purpose
- One justified conclusion about the strategy

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the response does not clearly calculate and justify the savings plan, the checkpoint is not complete.
