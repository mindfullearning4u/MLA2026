Lesson 3: Banking and Financial Accounts

In this lesson, you will compare common financial accounts and examine how banks and credit unions help people manage money. Different account choices affect access, safety, and saving decisions.

You will evaluate checking and savings accounts, compare account purposes, and explain which option fits a financial goal best.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain the purpose of checking and savings accounts
  • Compare financial account options
  • Identify how account choice supports money management
  • Justify which account fits a specific financial need
