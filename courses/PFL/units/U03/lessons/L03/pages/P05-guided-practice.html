🎯 Guided Practice

Scenario: Sam wants to keep money for three different purposes:

  • Money for weekly purchases
  • Money for an emergency fund
  • Money for a short-term savings goal

Respond:

  • Identify which purpose fits checking.
  • Identify which purposes fit savings.
  • Explain why easy access matters for one purpose.
  • Explain why limited spending access can help another purpose.
  • Justify the strongest account choice for each purpose.
