Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Consumer Decision-Making Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to evaluate purchases, identify financial risk, and explain a responsible consumer choice.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A student compares two phones, one with a lower price and one with better features and reliability.
- A shopper wants to buy a used bike but is unsure whether it will need expensive repairs later.
- A family compares two appliances and considers energy cost, quality, and purchase price.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The main consumer decision being made.
- An explanation of one financial risk in the scenario.
- A description of one strategy for comparing the purchase options.
- One sentence explaining which choice seems most financially responsible and why.

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the decision, risk, and reasoning are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
