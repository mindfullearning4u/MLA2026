Guided Practice

Practice comparing purchase options and identifying financial risk. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student compares two phones, one with a lower price and one with better features and reliability.
  • A shopper wants to buy a used bike but is unsure whether it will need expensive repairs later.
  • A family compares two appliances and considers energy cost, quality, and purchase price.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the main consumer decision being made.
  3. Explain one financial risk in the scenario.
  4. Describe one strategy for comparing the purchase options.
  5. Write one sentence explaining which choice seems most financially responsible and why.

⚠️ Reminder: Good consumer choices compare price, quality, usefulness, and long-term cost.
