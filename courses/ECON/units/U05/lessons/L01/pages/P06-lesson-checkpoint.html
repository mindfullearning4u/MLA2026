🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Budgeting Notebook Evidence
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify income, distinguish needs from wants, and explain how budgeting supports financial planning.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:

  • A student earns money babysitting and must decide how much to spend on snacks, school supplies, and saving.
  • A part-time worker wants to buy new shoes but also needs to pay for phone service and bus rides.
  • A teenager receives an allowance and wants to track spending on entertainment, clothing, and lunch.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • The income source in the scenario.
  • One need and one want from the scenario.
  • An explanation of how a budget would help the person manage money.
  • One sentence explaining why expense tracking would be useful.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if income, budgeting, and needs versus wants are not clearly identified, the checkpoint is not complete.

📘 Submit your notebook evidence in the assignment titled "Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Budgeting Notebook Evidence" that follows this lesson.
