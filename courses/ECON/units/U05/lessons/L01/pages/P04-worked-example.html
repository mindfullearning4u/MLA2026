Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how budgeting supports financial planning.

Scenario: A student earns $120 each month from a part-time job. The student needs to pay for school supplies and bus fare, but also wants to spend money on video games.

Income: The student earns $120.

Needs first: School supplies and bus fare should be included in the budget before entertainment spending.

Budget decision: The student can plan spending so necessary expenses are covered first and only the remaining money is used for wants.

Expense tracking: Writing down purchases helps the student stay within the budget and avoid running out of money early.

Why this works:

  • It shows how income limits spending choices.
  • It distinguishes needs from wants clearly.
  • It explains how budgeting supports better financial decisions.
