Guided Practice

Practice identifying income, needs, wants, and budgeting choices. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student earns money babysitting and must decide how much to spend on snacks, school supplies, and saving.
  • A part-time worker wants to buy new shoes but also needs to pay for phone service and bus rides.
  • A teenager receives an allowance and wants to track spending on entertainment, clothing, and lunch.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the income source in the scenario.
  3. List one need and one want from the scenario.
  4. Explain how a budget would help the person manage money.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why expense tracking would be useful.

⚠️ Reminder: A strong budget starts by comparing income with necessary expenses before spending on wants.
