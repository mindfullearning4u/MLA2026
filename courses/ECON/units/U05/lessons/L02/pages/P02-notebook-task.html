📘 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Saving: setting aside money for future use, often with low risk and easy access.
  • Investing: using money to try to earn a greater return over time, usually with more risk.
  • Interest: money earned on savings or paid on borrowed money.
  • Risk: the chance that money may not grow as expected or could lose value.
  • Return: the gain earned from saving or investing.
  • Key idea: saving and investing support long-term financial security, but they involve different levels of access, growth, and risk.
