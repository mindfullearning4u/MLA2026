Guided Practice

Practice identifying saving and investing choices and explaining how they support financial security. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student puts money into a savings account for emergency expenses that might happen soon.
  • A worker puts money into a long-term investment account for future financial growth.
  • A family splits money between short-term savings and a long-term investment goal.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify whether the main choice is saving, investing, or both.
  3. Explain how interest or return could help the money grow.
  4. Explain the level of risk in the scenario.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the choice supports financial security.

⚠️ Reminder: Saving usually provides easier access and lower risk, while investing usually offers greater possible growth with more risk.
