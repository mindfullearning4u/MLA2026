UNIT 05 | LESSON 5

Guided Practice

Apply all Unit 05 concepts to analyze a personal finance scenario.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student earns income from a job, needs to cover transportation costs, and wants to save for a future purchase.
  • A worker is deciding whether to save money for emergencies or invest money for a long-term goal.
  • A shopper is comparing two purchase options while also deciding whether using credit is financially responsible.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

Respond:

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Explain how budgeting applies to the scenario.
  3. Explain whether saving, investing, or borrowing is involved.
  4. Identify one financial risk in the scenario.
  5. Explain one responsible consumer or borrowing decision that could be made.
  6. Write one complete paragraph explaining the best overall financial plan.

⚠️Reminder: You must connect multiple Unit 05 concepts together. Do not explain only one part of the scenario.
