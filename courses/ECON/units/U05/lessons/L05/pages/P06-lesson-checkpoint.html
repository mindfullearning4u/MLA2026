🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Personal Finance Readiness
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply Unit 05 concepts in a complete personal finance analysis.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:

  • A student earns income from a job, needs to cover transportation costs, and wants to save for a future purchase.
  • A worker is deciding whether to save money for emergencies or invest money for a long-term goal.
  • A shopper is comparing two purchase options while also deciding whether using credit is financially responsible.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • An explanation of how budgeting applies to the scenario.
  • An explanation of whether saving, investing, or borrowing is involved.
  • Identification of one financial risk in the scenario.
  • An explanation of one responsible consumer or borrowing decision that could be made.
  • One complete paragraph explaining the best overall financial plan.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the analysis does not clearly connect multiple Unit 05 concepts, the checkpoint is not complete.
