Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how credit and debt affect financial responsibility.

Scenario: A student borrows money to buy a used laptop for school and agrees to repay the loan over time with interest.

Credit use: The student uses credit to make a purchase before having all the cash available.

Debt created: The student now owes the lender the amount borrowed plus interest.

Interest effect: Because of interest, the total amount repaid will be more than the laptop's purchase price.

Financial responsibility: The student must make payments on time and understand the total cost of borrowing.

Why this works:

  • It shows how borrowing creates debt.
  • It explains how interest changes the total cost.
  • It connects credit use to financial responsibility.
