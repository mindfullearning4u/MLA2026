UNIT 05 | LESSON 3

Guided Practice

Practice identifying how credit, loans, and interest affect financial decisions. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student uses a loan to pay for a computer needed for school.
  • A person borrows money for a purchase and learns the interest rate is higher than expected.
  • A borrower makes a plan to repay debt on time each month.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the type of credit or debt shown.
  3. Explain how interest affects the total cost.
  4. Describe one financial responsibility the borrower has.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why careful borrowing matters.

⚠️Reminder: Responsible borrowing means understanding both the amount borrowed and the full cost of repayment.
