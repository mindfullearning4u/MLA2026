Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Credit and Debt Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to explain how credit and debt work and why borrowing creates financial responsibility.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A student uses a loan to pay for a computer needed for school.
- A person borrows money for a purchase and learns the interest rate is higher than expected.
- A borrower makes a plan to repay debt on time each month.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The type of credit or debt shown in the scenario.
- An explanation of how interest affects the total cost.
- A description of one financial responsibility the borrower has.
- One sentence explaining why careful borrowing matters.

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if credit, debt, interest, and responsibility are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
