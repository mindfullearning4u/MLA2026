💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample scenario and identify the market structure.

Scenario: A city has only one company that provides electricity to all residents.

Market structure: Monopoly

Why? There is only one seller, and consumers have no alternative providers.

Pricing power: The company has significant control over pricing, although it may be regulated.

Consumer choice: Very limited, since no competitors exist.

✅Why this works:

  • It clearly identifies a single seller, which defines a monopoly.
  • It explains how limited competition affects pricing and choice.
  • It connects the concept to a real-world example.
