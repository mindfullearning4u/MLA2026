Unit 02 Lesson 4 - Market Structures

📘 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Market structure: the organization of a market based on the number of sellers and the level of competition.
  • Perfect competition: many sellers offering identical products with no control over price.
  • Monopoly: one seller dominates the market and controls price.
  • Oligopoly: a few large firms dominate the market and influence prices.
  • Monopolistic competition: many sellers offering similar but differentiated products.
  • Key idea: the structure of a market affects pricing power, competition, and consumer choice.
