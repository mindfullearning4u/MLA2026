Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Market Structures Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify market structures and explain their impact on competition and pricing.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- Many farmers sell identical apples in a market where no single farmer controls the price.
- Three large smartphone companies dominate the market and closely watch each other’s pricing decisions.
- A clothing store competes with many other stores by offering unique styles and branding.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The correct market structure.
- An explanation of how many sellers are in the market.
- An explanation of the level of competition.
- One sentence explaining how this structure affects price or consumer choice.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the market structure and explanation are unclear or incorrect, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
