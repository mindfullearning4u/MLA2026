Guided Practice

Practice identifying different market structures and explaining how they affect competition.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • Many farmers sell identical apples in a market where no single farmer controls the price.
  • Three large smartphone companies dominate the market and closely watch each other’s pricing decisions.
  • A clothing store competes with many other stores by offering unique styles and branding.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the market structure.
  3. Explain how many sellers are in the market.
  4. Explain the level of competition.
  5. Write one sentence explaining how this structure affects price or consumer choice.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on the number of sellers, product differences, and level of competition to identify the market structure correctly.
