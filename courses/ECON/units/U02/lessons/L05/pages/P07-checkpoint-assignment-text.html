Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Market Analysis Readiness

This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply all Unit 02 concepts in a complete market analysis.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A sudden trend increases demand for sneakers, but factories cannot immediately increase production.
- New technology lowers production costs, allowing companies to produce more laptops at the same price.
- A market with only one internet provider raises prices while consumer demand remains high.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- An explanation of what is happening to demand.
- An explanation of what is happening to supply.
- Identification of shortage, surplus, or equilibrium change.
- An explanation of how price is likely to change.
- Identification of the market structure (if applicable).
- One complete paragraph explaining the full market outcome.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the analysis does not clearly connect demand, supply, equilibrium, and market structure, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint prepares you for the Unit 02 Final Assessment and contributes to unit mastery.
