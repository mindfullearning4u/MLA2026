Guided Practice

Apply all Unit 02 concepts to analyze a complete market scenario.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A sudden trend increases demand for sneakers, but factories cannot immediately increase production.
  • New technology lowers production costs, allowing companies to produce more laptops at the same price.
  • A market with only one internet provider raises prices while consumer demand remains high.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Explain what is happening to demand.
  3. Explain what is happening to supply.
  4. Identify whether there is a shortage, surplus, or shift toward a new equilibrium.
  5. Explain how price is likely to change.
  6. Identify the market structure (if applicable).
  7. Write one complete paragraph explaining the full market outcome.

⚠️ Reminder: You must connect multiple concepts together. Do not explain each concept separately without linking them.
