Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how multiple economic concepts are used together.

Scenario: A popular new video game is released. Demand increases quickly, but production cannot keep up.

Demand: Increased because many consumers want the game.

Supply: Remains limited due to production constraints.

Market outcome: A shortage occurs at the current price.

Price effect: Prices are likely to rise as buyers compete for limited supply.

Market structure insight: If only a few companies produce the game (oligopoly), they may influence how quickly supply increases.

Conclusion: The market will move toward a new equilibrium with a higher price and eventually increased supply.

Why this works:

  • It connects demand, supply, and equilibrium in one explanation.
  • It identifies a shortage and explains the price adjustment.
  • It includes the role of market structure in shaping outcomes.
