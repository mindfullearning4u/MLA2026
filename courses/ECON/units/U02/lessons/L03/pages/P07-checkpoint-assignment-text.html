Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Market Equilibrium Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify equilibrium-related market outcomes and explain how supply or demand changes affect price.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- The price of school hoodies is set too high, and many hoodies remain unsold after several weeks.
- A sudden increase in demand for bottled water leaves store shelves nearly empty at the current price.
- A new farming technology helps farmers produce more strawberries, increasing supply in the market.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- Whether the scenario shows a surplus, a shortage, or a shift toward a new equilibrium.
- An explanation of what happened to supply or demand.
- An explanation of what is likely to happen to price if the market adjusts.
- One sentence explaining why the market outcome makes economic sense.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the equilibrium reasoning, supply or demand change, and price explanation are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
