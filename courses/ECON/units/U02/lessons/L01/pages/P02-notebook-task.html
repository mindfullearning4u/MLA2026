Unit 02 Lesson 1 - Demand and Consumer Behavior

📘 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Demand: the quantity of a good or service consumers are willing and able to buy at different prices.
  • Law of demand: as price increases, quantity demanded decreases. As price decreases, quantity demanded increases.
  • Movement along the demand curve: a change in quantity demanded caused only by a change in price.
  • Shift in demand: a change in demand caused by something other than price.
  • Demand determinants: income, preferences, prices of related goods, expectations, and number of buyers.
  • Key idea: price changes affect quantity demanded, but other factors can shift demand entirely.
