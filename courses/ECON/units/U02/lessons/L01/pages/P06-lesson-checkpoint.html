🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Demand Notebook Evidence
This checkpoint verifies your ability to explain the law of demand, identify a factor affecting demand, and classify the change correctly.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:

  • The price of concert tickets rises, and fewer students buy them.
  • A popular social media trend makes a certain brand of water bottle more desirable, and more students want one.
  • The number of people moving into a town increases, and more apartments are wanted in that area.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • Whether the example shows a movement along demand or a shift in demand.
  • The specific factor affecting demand.
  • An explanation of how consumer behavior changed.
  • One sentence explaining why the change makes economic sense.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the law of demand, factor, and classification are not clearly identified, the checkpoint is not complete.
