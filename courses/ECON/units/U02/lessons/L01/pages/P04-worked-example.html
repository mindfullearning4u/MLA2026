Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how demand and consumer behavior are connected.

Scenario: A school store sells smoothies. Last month, each smoothie cost $4. This month, the price increased to $6.

What happened? After the price increased, fewer students bought smoothies.

Law of demand: The higher price led to a lower quantity demanded.

Movement or shift? This is a movement along the demand curve because the change was caused by the smoothie price itself.

Possible demand shift example: If a health trend made smoothies much more popular, demand could increase even if the price stayed the same.

Why this works:

  • It shows the inverse relationship between price and quantity demanded.
  • It distinguishes price-driven movement from a shift caused by another factor.
  • It connects consumer behavior to a real school-based example.
