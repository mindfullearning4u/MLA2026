Guided Practice

Practice identifying the law of demand and factors that affect demand. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • The price of concert tickets rises, and fewer students buy them.
  • A popular social media trend makes a certain brand of water bottle more desirable, and more students want one.
  • The number of people moving into a town increases, and more apartments are wanted in that area.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. State whether the example shows a movement along demand or a shift in demand.
  3. Identify the specific factor affecting demand.
  4. Explain how consumer behavior changed in that scenario.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the change makes economic sense.

⚠️ Reminder: If the price of the product itself changes, it is movement along demand. If another factor changes, it is a shift in demand.
