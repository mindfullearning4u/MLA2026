Unit 02 Lesson 2 - Supply and Producer Behavior

📘 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Supply: the quantity of a good or service producers are willing and able to sell at different prices.
  • Law of supply: as price increases, quantity supplied increases. As price decreases, quantity supplied decreases.
  • Movement along the supply curve: a change in quantity supplied caused only by a change in price.
  • Shift in supply: a change in supply caused by something other than price.
  • Supply determinants: production costs, technology, expectations, and number of sellers.
  • Key idea: price changes affect quantity supplied, but other factors can shift supply entirely.
