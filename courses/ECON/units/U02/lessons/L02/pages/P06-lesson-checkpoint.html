🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Supply Notebook Evidence
This checkpoint verifies your ability to explain the law of supply, identify a factor affecting supply, and classify the change correctly.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:

  • The price of handmade bracelets rises, and a seller decides to make more bracelets each week.
  • A factory installs faster machines, and the company can produce more shoes at the same price.
  • The cost of flour rises sharply, and a bakery produces fewer loaves of bread.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • Whether the example shows a movement along supply or a shift in supply.
  • The specific factor affecting supply.
  • An explanation of how producer behavior changed.
  • One sentence explaining why the change makes economic sense.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the law of supply, factor, and classification are not clearly identified, the checkpoint is not complete.
