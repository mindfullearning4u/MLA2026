Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how supply and producer behavior are connected.

Scenario: A local bakery sells cupcakes. Last month, each cupcake sold for $2. This month, the price increased to $3.

What happened? After the price increased, the bakery decided to make more cupcakes each day.

Law of supply: The higher price led to a higher quantity supplied.

Movement or shift? This is a movement along the supply curve because the change was caused by the cupcake price itself.

Possible supply shift example: If a new mixing machine helped the bakery produce cupcakes faster, supply could increase even if the price stayed the same.

Why this works:

  • It shows the direct relationship between price and quantity supplied.
  • It distinguishes price-driven movement from a shift caused by another factor.
  • It connects producer behavior to a real business example.
