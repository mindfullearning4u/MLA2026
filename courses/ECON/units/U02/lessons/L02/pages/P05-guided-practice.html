Guided Practice

Practice identifying the law of supply and factors that affect supply. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • The price of handmade bracelets rises, and a seller decides to make more bracelets each week.
  • A factory installs faster machines, and the company can produce more shoes at the same price.
  • The cost of flour rises sharply, and a bakery produces fewer loaves of bread.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. State whether the example shows a movement along supply or a shift in supply.
  3. Identify the specific factor affecting supply.
  4. Explain how producer behavior changed in that scenario.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the change makes economic sense.

⚠️ Reminder: If the price of the product itself changes, it is movement along supply. If another factor changes, it is a shift in supply.
