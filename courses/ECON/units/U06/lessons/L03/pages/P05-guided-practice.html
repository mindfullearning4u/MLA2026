Guided Practice

Practice identifying global markets, globalization, and interdependence in economic examples.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A clothing brand designs products in one country, manufactures them in another, and sells them worldwide.
  • A food company depends on cocoa beans grown abroad and ships chocolate products to many countries.
  • A car company uses metal, computer chips, and tires from different countries to build vehicles.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify one example of a global market in the scenario.
  3. Explain how the scenario shows interdependence.
  4. Describe one way globalization makes the activity possible.
  5. Write one sentence explaining how a disruption in one country could affect the others.

Reminder: Interdependence means countries and businesses rely on one another, not that they operate separately.
