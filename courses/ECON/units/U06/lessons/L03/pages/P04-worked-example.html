Worked Example

Read the sample scenario and observe how global interdependence connects countries.

Scenario: A company in one country assembles phones using parts made in several other countries, then sells those phones around the world.

Global market: The phones are produced and sold through international economic connections.

Interdependence: The company depends on factories, materials, and transportation systems in multiple countries.

Globalization: Trade and communication allow the business to operate across borders.

Result: A disruption in one country can affect production and sales in many other places.

Why this works:

  • It shows how production can involve multiple countries.
  • It explains why countries and businesses depend on one another.
  • It connects global markets to everyday products.
