Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Economic system: the way a country organizes production, distribution, and use of goods and services.

  • Command economy: an economy in which the government makes many major production and resource decisions.
  • Mixed economy: an economy that combines elements of market and command systems.
  • Global comparison: countries may use different systems while still participating in world trade.
