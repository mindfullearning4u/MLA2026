Worked Example

Read the sample scenario and observe how economic systems can be compared across countries.

Scenario: Country A allows private businesses to make most production decisions, but the government regulates some industries and provides public services.

Economic system: Mixed economy

Why? Private choice is important, but the government also has a role in parts of the economy.

Global context: Country A can still trade with countries that use different economic systems.

Result: Comparing systems helps explain how countries make decisions while participating in international markets.

Why this works:

  • It identifies the main features of a mixed economy.
  • It shows how government and private decision-making can coexist.
  • It connects economic systems to global participation.
