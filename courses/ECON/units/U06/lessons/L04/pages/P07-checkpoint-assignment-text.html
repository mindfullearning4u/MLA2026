Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Economic Systems in a Global Context Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify economic systems and compare how countries organize economic decisions.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A country leaves most production decisions to private businesses, with limited government involvement.
- A country has a government that controls many major industries and decides what is produced.
- A country allows private business ownership but also regulates industries and provides many public services.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The economic system shown in the scenario.
- An explanation of who makes most major economic decisions.
- A description of one way that system could still participate in global trade.
- One sentence explaining why comparing systems across countries is useful.

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the economic system and explanation are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
