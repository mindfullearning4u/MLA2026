Guided Practice

Practice identifying economic systems and explaining how they compare across countries.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A country leaves most production decisions to private businesses, with limited government involvement.
  • A country has a government that controls many major industries and decides what is produced.
  • A country allows private business ownership but also regulates industries and provides many public services.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the economic system shown in the scenario.
  3. Explain who makes most major economic decisions.
  4. Describe one way that system could still participate in global trade.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why comparing systems across countries is useful.

Reminder: Market, command, and mixed economies differ mainly in how economic decisions are made.
