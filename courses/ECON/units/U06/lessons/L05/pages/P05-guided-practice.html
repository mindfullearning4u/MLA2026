Guided Practice

Apply all Unit 06 concepts to analyze a complete global trade scenario.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • Country A exports fruit and imports technology, while Country B exports technology and imports fruit.
  • Country A produces textiles at low opportunity cost, and Country B produces steel at low opportunity cost, so the countries trade.
  • Two countries with different economic systems trade energy, food, and manufactured goods with each other.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify what each country imports and exports.
  3. Explain how specialization or comparative advantage applies.
  4. Describe one way the countries are interdependent.
  5. Identify whether economic system differences may matter in the scenario.
  6. Write one complete paragraph explaining the overall trade relationship.

Reminder: A strong analysis connects trade basics, comparative advantage, interdependence, and economic systems instead of explaining only one part.
