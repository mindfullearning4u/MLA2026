Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Global Trade Readiness

This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply Unit 06 concepts in a complete analysis of international economic interaction.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- Country A exports fruit and imports technology, while Country B exports technology and imports fruit.
- Country A produces textiles at low opportunity cost, and Country B produces steel at low opportunity cost, so the countries trade.
- Two countries with different economic systems trade energy, food, and manufactured goods with each other.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- What each country imports and exports.
- An explanation of how specialization or comparative advantage applies.
- A description of one way the countries are interdependent.
- Identification of whether economic system differences may matter.
- One complete paragraph explaining the overall trade relationship.

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the analysis does not clearly connect multiple Unit 06 concepts, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint prepares you for the Unit 06 Final Assessment and contributes to unit mastery.
