Guided Practice

Practice identifying imports, exports, and specialization in simple trade examples.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A country sells corn to other countries and buys smartphones from abroad.
  • A country produces large amounts of oil and trades for machinery made elsewhere.
  • A country manufactures shoes and buys coffee beans from another country.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify one import in the scenario.
  3. Identify one export in the scenario.
  4. Explain what the country appears to specialize in.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why trade is useful in that scenario.

Reminder: Imports are bought from another country. Exports are sold to another country.
