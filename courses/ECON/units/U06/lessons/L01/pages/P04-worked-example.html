Worked Example

Read the sample scenario and observe how imports, exports, and specialization work together.

Scenario: Country A produces a large amount of wheat. Country B produces a large amount of electronics.

Specialization: Country A specializes in wheat production, and Country B specializes in electronics.

Exports: Country A exports wheat to Country B. Country B exports electronics to Country A.

Imports: Country A imports electronics, and Country B imports wheat.

Result: Each country receives goods it wants while focusing on production it does well.

Why this works:

  • It shows the difference between imports and exports clearly.
  • It explains specialization in a simple trade setting.
  • It connects trade to real exchange between countries.
