Guided Practice

Practice identifying trade-offs and opportunity cost. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • You have $30 and must choose between buying a pair of headphones, saving the money, or going out to eat with friends.
  • You have three hours on Saturday and must choose between homework, a sports practice, or extra sleep.
  • A small business must choose between buying new equipment, hiring part-time help, or spending money on advertising.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. State the choice you would make.
  3. List the trade-offs in that scenario.
  4. Identify the opportunity cost.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why your choice is reasonable.

⚠️ Reminder: A trade-off includes all alternatives given up. Opportunity cost is only the next best option not chosen.
