Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Opportunity Cost Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify trade-offs, determine opportunity cost, and justify a decision using economic reasoning.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- You have $30 and must choose between buying a pair of headphones, saving the money, or going out to eat with friends.
- You have three hours on Saturday and must choose between homework, a sports practice, or extra sleep.
- A small business must choose between buying new equipment, hiring part-time help, or spending money on advertising.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The choice you would make.
- The trade-offs in that scenario.
- The opportunity cost of your choice.
- One sentence explaining why your decision is reasonable.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the trade-offs and opportunity cost are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
