Unit 01 Lesson 2 - Opportunity Cost and Trade-Offs

📘 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Trade-off: all the alternatives you give up when you make a choice.
  • Opportunity cost: the value of the next best alternative you give up when making a choice.
  • Decision-making: the process of comparing options and choosing the one that best fits your goal.
  • Key idea: every decision has a cost, even when no money is involved.
  • Important reminder: opportunity cost is not every option you give up. It is the next best option you did not choose.
