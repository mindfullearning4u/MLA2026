Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Economics Notebook Evidence
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify scarcity, distinguish needs from wants, and explain how limited resources affect decision-making.

You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:
- You have two hours after school and must choose between studying, working, exercising, or resting.
- Your family has a limited monthly budget and must choose between several expenses.
- A business must decide whether to spend money on more workers, new equipment, or advertising.

Required evidence:
- The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
- One example of scarcity in that situation.
- One need and one want in that situation.
- One sentence explaining how economic thinking helps guide the decision.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the explanation of scarcity, needs, wants, and decision-making is unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
