Concept | What Is Economics?

Economics means using resources in a way that is logical, efficient, and deliberate. Every decision you make about time, money, energy, and materials reflects economic behavior and affects outcomes.

A strong economics student understands both scarcity and choice. Since resources are limited, people must decide what is most important and what they are willing to give up.

🧠 Think: How do limited time or limited money affect the choices you make each day?

⚠️ Common mistake: Believing that economics is only about money. Economics also includes time, effort, resources, priorities, and decision-making.
