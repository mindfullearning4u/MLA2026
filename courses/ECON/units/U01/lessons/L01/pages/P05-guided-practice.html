Guided Practice

Practice identifying economics concepts in daily life. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • You have two hours after school and must choose between studying, working, exercising, or resting.
  • Your family has a limited monthly budget and must choose between several expenses.
  • A business must decide whether to spend money on more workers, new equipment, or advertising.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify one example of scarcity in that situation.
  3. Identify one need and one want in that situation.
  4. Write one sentence explaining how economic thinking helps make the decision.

⚠️ Reminder: Economics requires careful choices, clear priorities, and awareness of how limited resources affect decisions.
