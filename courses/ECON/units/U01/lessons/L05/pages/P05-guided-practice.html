UNIT 01 | LESSON 5

Guided Practice

Apply all economic concepts from this unit to analyze a real-world decision.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Scenario:

  • A city has a limited budget and must decide whether to build a new park or repair public roads.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Explain the scarcity in this situation.
  2. Identify the opportunity cost of each choice.
  3. Describe at least one incentive for choosing the park and one for choosing the roads.
  4. Explain who is likely making the decision.
  5. Write one sentence explaining which option you would choose and why.

⚠️Reminder: Use multiple economic concepts together, not just one.
