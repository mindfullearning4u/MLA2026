🎯 Lesson Checkpoint | Economic Decision Scenario
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply multiple economic concepts to analyze a real-world decision.

Scenario (Required):

  • A city has a limited budget and must decide whether to build a new park or repair public roads.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • A clear explanation of scarcity in this situation.
  • The opportunity cost of each choice.
  • At least one incentive for building the park.
  • At least one incentive for repairing the roads.
  • Who is making the decision and why.
  • A final decision with justification using economic reasoning.

If your response does not include multiple economic concepts or does not clearly explain the decision, the checkpoint is not complete.
