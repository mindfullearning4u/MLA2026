Guided Practice

Practice identifying economic systems and how they operate. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A farming village produces goods the same way families have done for generations and passes jobs from parent to child.
  • A government decides how much steel, food, and fuel the country will produce and sets the prices for those goods.
  • A country allows private businesses to operate freely but also funds schools, roads, and safety inspections.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify which economic system is shown.
  3. Explain which details in the scenario support your answer.
  4. State who is making most economic decisions in that system.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why that system affects production and distribution.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on who makes decisions and how resources are organized. That is what identifies the economic system.
