Unit 01 Lesson 3 - Economic Systems

📘 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Economic system: the method a society uses to decide what to produce, how to produce it, and for whom to produce it.
  • Traditional economy: an economy based on customs, habits, and long-standing practices.
  • Command economy: an economy in which the government makes major economic decisions.
  • Market economy: an economy in which individuals and businesses make most economic decisions.
  • Mixed economy: an economy that combines elements of market and command systems.
  • Key idea: no economy answers economic questions in exactly the same way.
