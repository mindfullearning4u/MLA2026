Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Positive incentive: a reward that encourages a choice or behavior.
  • Negative incentive: a penalty, cost, or consequence that discourages a choice or behavior.
  • Decision-making: choosing between alternatives based on expected benefits and costs.
  • Key idea: people usually respond to incentives when making economic decisions.
  • Important reminder: incentives can influence behavior, but they do not guarantee the same response from every person.
