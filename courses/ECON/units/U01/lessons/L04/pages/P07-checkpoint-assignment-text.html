Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Incentives Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify an incentive, classify it correctly, and explain how it affects economic decision-making.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A store gives customers 20 percent off if they shop during a weekend sale.
- A school charges a fee for students who return textbooks late.
- A company offers employees a bonus for perfect attendance during one month.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The incentive in that scenario.
- Whether the incentive is positive or negative.
- An explanation of how the incentive is intended to influence behavior.
- One sentence explaining why the incentive may be effective.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the incentive and explanation are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
