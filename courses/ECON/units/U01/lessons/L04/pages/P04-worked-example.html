Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how incentives affect a decision.

Scenario: A coffee shop offers a free drink after a customer buys five drinks.

Positive incentive: The free drink encourages customers to return and keep buying from the same shop.

Decision affected: A customer may choose that shop over another because the reward increases the value of the purchase.

Economic reasoning: The business uses an incentive to influence consumer behavior and increase repeat sales.

Why this works:

  • It identifies the incentive clearly.
  • It shows how a reward can influence behavior.
  • It connects the incentive to a business decision and a consumer response.
