UNIT 01 | LESSON 4

Guided Practice

Practice identifying incentives and their effects on economic decisions. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A store gives customers 20 percent off if they shop during a weekend sale.
  • A school charges a fee for students who return textbooks late.
  • A company offers employees a bonus for perfect attendance during one month.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the incentive in that scenario.
  3. State whether it is a positive incentive or a negative incentive.
  4. Explain how the incentive is intended to influence behavior.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the incentive may be effective.

⚠️Reminder: Focus on how the reward or consequence changes decision-making.
