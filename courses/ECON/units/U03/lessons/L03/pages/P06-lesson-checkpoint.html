Lesson Checkpoint | Inflation Notebook Evidence
This checkpoint verifies your ability to explain inflation, describe purchasing power, and connect price changes to the cost of living.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:

  • The monthly rent for an apartment rises from $900 to $1,050 over two years.
  • A family notices that groceries now cost more, even though they are buying the same items as before.
  • A student can no longer buy as many school lunches with the same weekly lunch budget.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • An explanation of how inflation is shown in the scenario.
  • An explanation of how purchasing power is affected.
  • An explanation of how the scenario relates to the cost of living.
  • One sentence explaining why inflation matters in everyday life.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if inflation and purchasing power are not clearly explained, the checkpoint is not complete.

Submit your notebook evidence in the assignment titled "Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Inflation Notebook Evidence" that follows this lesson.
