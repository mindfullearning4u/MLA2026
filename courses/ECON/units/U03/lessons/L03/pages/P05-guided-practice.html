Guided Practice

Practice identifying inflation and its effect on purchasing power. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • The monthly rent for an apartment rises from $900 to $1,050 over two years.
  • A family notices that groceries now cost more, even though they are buying the same items as before.
  • A student can no longer buy as many school lunches with the same weekly lunch budget.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Explain how inflation is shown in the scenario.
  3. Explain how purchasing power is affected.
  4. Explain how the scenario relates to the cost of living.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why inflation matters in everyday life.

Reminder: Focus on how rising prices change what people can afford with the same amount of money.
