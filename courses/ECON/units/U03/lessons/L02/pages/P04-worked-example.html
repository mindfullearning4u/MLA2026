💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample scenario and observe how unemployment type is identified.

Scenario: A store closes several cashier positions after installing self-checkout machines. Some former cashiers are looking for work, but many available jobs now require technology skills they do not yet have.

Unemployment type: Structural unemployment

Why? The jobs changed because of technology, and the workers' current skills do not match the new positions.

Labor market connection: Employers are still hiring, but they are looking for different skills than before.

Workforce trend: Changes in technology can reshape what kinds of workers businesses need.

✅Why this works:

  • It identifies the specific cause of unemployment clearly.
  • It connects unemployment to changes in the labor market.
  • It shows how workforce trends can affect job opportunities.
