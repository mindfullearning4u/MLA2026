Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Unemployment Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify a type of unemployment and explain what it shows about labor markets and workforce trends.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A recent graduate is looking for a first full-time job after finishing school.
- A worker loses a job because new machines now complete most of the tasks.
- A restaurant lays off employees because customers are spending less during an economic slowdown.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The correct type of unemployment.
- An explanation of why that type fits the scenario.
- An explanation of what the scenario shows about the labor market.
- One sentence explaining how workforce trends or economic conditions affect the situation.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the unemployment type and explanation are not clearly identified, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
