Guided Practice

Practice identifying unemployment types and labor market changes. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A recent graduate is looking for a first full-time job after finishing school.
  • A worker loses a job because new machines now complete most of the tasks.
  • A restaurant lays off employees because customers are spending less during an economic slowdown.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the type of unemployment shown.
  3. Explain why that type of unemployment fits the scenario.
  4. Explain what the scenario shows about the labor market.
  5. Write one sentence explaining how workforce trends or economic conditions affect the situation.

Reminder: Focus on the reason the person is unemployed. The cause helps identify the correct type of unemployment.
