Lesson Checkpoint | Economic Indicators Readiness
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply Unit 03 indicators in a complete economic analysis.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:

  • GDP is rising, unemployment is falling, and consumer spending is increasing.
  • GDP is falling, unemployment is rising, and businesses are cutting production.
  • GDP begins rising again after a downturn, unemployment starts to improve, and spending slowly increases.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • An explanation of what the GDP trend suggests.
  • An explanation of what the unemployment trend suggests.
  • The business cycle phase shown most clearly by the scenario.
  • A statement of whether the economy appears to be growing, weakening, or recovering.
  • One complete paragraph explaining the full economic condition using multiple indicators.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the response does not clearly connect multiple indicators, the checkpoint is not complete.
