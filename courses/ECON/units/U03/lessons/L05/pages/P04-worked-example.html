Worked Example

Read the sample scenario and observe how multiple economic indicators are used together.

Scenario: Over the past year, GDP has increased, unemployment has fallen, and stores report stronger consumer spending. Inflation has risen slightly, but prices are still increasing at a moderate pace.

GDP: Rising GDP suggests the economy is producing more goods and services.

Unemployment: Falling unemployment suggests labor market conditions are improving.

Inflation: Moderate inflation suggests prices are rising, but not so quickly that purchasing power is collapsing.

Business cycle interpretation: These indicators suggest the economy is in an expansion.

Conclusion: The evidence points to a growing economy with stronger economic activity overall.

Why this works:

  • It uses multiple indicators instead of only one.
  • It explains what each indicator suggests.
  • It connects the evidence to a broader conclusion about the economy.
