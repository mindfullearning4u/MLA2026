Guided Practice

Apply all Unit 03 concepts to interpret an economic indicators scenario.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • GDP is rising, unemployment is falling, and consumer spending is increasing.
  • GDP is falling, unemployment is rising, and businesses are cutting production.
  • GDP begins rising again after a downturn, unemployment starts to improve, and spending slowly increases.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Explain what the GDP trend suggests.
  3. Explain what the unemployment trend suggests.
  4. Identify what phase of the business cycle the scenario most closely shows.
  5. State whether the economy appears to be growing, weakening, or recovering.
  6. Write one complete paragraph explaining the full economic condition using multiple indicators.

Reminder: You must connect multiple indicators together. Do not list them separately without explaining what they suggest as a group.
