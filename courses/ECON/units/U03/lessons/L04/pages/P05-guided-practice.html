Guided Practice

Practice identifying economic growth and business cycle phases. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • Businesses are hiring more workers, customers are spending more, and production is increasing.
  • Stores are selling less, factories are producing fewer goods, and overall spending has fallen.
  • Economic activity begins to improve after a long slowdown, and businesses slowly increase production again.

Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify which phase of the business cycle is shown.
  3. Explain which details in the scenario support your answer.
  4. Explain how economic activity is changing in that phase.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the phase matters to businesses or households.

Reminder: Focus on whether economic activity is rising, falling, or beginning to improve. That helps identify the correct business cycle phase.
