Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Business Cycle Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify a business cycle phase and explain how economic activity changes during that phase.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- Businesses are hiring more workers, customers are spending more, and production is increasing.
- Stores are selling less, factories are producing fewer goods, and overall spending has fallen.
- Economic activity begins to improve after a long slowdown, and businesses slowly increase production again.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The correct business cycle phase.
- An explanation of which details support your answer.
- An explanation of how economic activity is changing.
- One sentence explaining why the phase matters to businesses or households.

Accepted submission formats:
- Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
- PDF file
- Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the business cycle phase and explanation are not clearly identified, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
