Unit 03 Lesson 4 - Economic Growth and Business Cycles

Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Economic growth: an increase in the production of goods and services in an economy over time.
  • Business cycle: the pattern of expansion and contraction in economic activity over time.
  • Expansion: a period when the economy is growing and production, spending, and jobs are generally increasing.
  • Peak: the highest point of economic activity before a downturn begins.
  • Recession: a period of declining economic activity.
  • Trough: the lowest point of economic activity in the cycle.
  • Recovery: the period when the economy begins to improve after a recession.
  • Key idea: business cycles show that economic activity rises and falls over time rather than staying constant.
