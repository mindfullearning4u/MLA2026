Guided Practice

Practice identifying GDP and its components using real-world examples. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • Families spend more money on groceries and transportation during the year.
  • A company buys new computers and expands its office building.
  • The government increases spending on public schools and road repairs.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify which GDP component is shown most clearly.
  3. Explain why that activity counts as part of GDP.
  4. Explain how that activity reflects economic output.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why GDP matters to economists.

⚠️Reminder: Focus on what is being produced or purchased and how that activity contributes to total output in the economy.
