Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Gross Domestic Product (GDP): the total value of final goods and services produced within a country during a specific period of time.
  • GDP measures: the size and overall output of an economy.
  • Consumption: household spending on goods and services.
  • Investment: business spending on equipment, structures, and inventory.
  • Government spending: spending by government on public goods and services.
  • Net exports: exports minus imports.
  • Key idea: GDP helps economists compare economic activity over time and across countries.
