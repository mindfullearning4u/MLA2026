Guided Practice

Practice identifying types of taxes and explaining why governments collect revenue. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A worker sees money withheld from each paycheck before receiving take-home pay.
  • A family pays an added tax when buying furniture at a store.
  • A homeowner pays yearly taxes based on the value of a house.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the type of tax shown.
  3. Explain how that tax raises revenue for government.
  4. Explain one way that tax affects an individual or business decision.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why governments collect that tax.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on what is being taxed and how the tax affects the people or businesses involved.
