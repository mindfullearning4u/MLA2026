Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how taxes raise revenue and affect economic decisions.

Scenario: A student buys a pair of headphones priced at $30. At checkout, a sales tax is added, so the total cost is higher than the listed price.

Type of tax: Sales tax

Government revenue: The tax collected from the purchase helps raise money for government services.

Consumer impact: The student pays more than the original price because the tax increases the final cost.

Economic reasoning: Taxes raise revenue for government, but they also affect how much consumers pay and how businesses present prices.

Why this works:

  • It identifies a tax type clearly.
  • It shows how taxes raise revenue.
  • It connects taxation to everyday consumer decisions.
