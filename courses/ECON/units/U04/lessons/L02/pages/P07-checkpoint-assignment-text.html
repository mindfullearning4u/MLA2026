Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Taxes Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify a type of tax, explain how it raises revenue, and describe its economic impact.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A worker sees money withheld from each paycheck before receiving take-home pay.
- A family pays an added tax when buying furniture at a store.
- A homeowner pays yearly taxes based on the value of a house.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- The type of tax shown in the scenario.
- An explanation of how that tax raises revenue for government.
- An explanation of one way that tax affects an individual or business decision.
- One sentence explaining why governments collect that tax.

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the tax type and explanation are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
