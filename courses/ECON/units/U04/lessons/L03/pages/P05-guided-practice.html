Guided Practice

Practice identifying how fiscal policy can influence economic activity. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • The government increases spending on school construction during an economic slowdown.
  • The government raises taxes and cuts spending because leaders want to slow overall economic activity.
  • The government lowers taxes for households in order to encourage more spending.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify whether the policy is expansionary or contractionary.
  3. Explain what the government is doing with spending or taxes.
  4. Explain how the policy is intended to influence the economy.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why that policy makes economic sense in the scenario.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on whether government is trying to encourage activity or slow it down.
