Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how fiscal policy can influence the economy.

Scenario: The economy is slowing, and the government decides to fund major road repair projects and lower some taxes for households.

Policy type: Expansionary fiscal policy

Government action: The government increases spending and lowers taxes.

Why this matters: More government spending can create additional economic activity, and lower taxes leave more money with households.

Economic reasoning: The policy is meant to encourage spending and support the economy during a slowdown.

Why this works:

  • It identifies fiscal policy clearly.
  • It explains how spending and tax decisions work together.
  • It connects the policy to a specific economic goal.
