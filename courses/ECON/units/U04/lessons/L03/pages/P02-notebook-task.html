Unit 04 Lesson 3 - Fiscal Policy

📘 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Fiscal policy: government use of spending and taxation decisions to influence the economy.
  • Government spending: money the government uses to buy goods, services, or fund programs.
  • Expansionary fiscal policy: a policy that increases spending or lowers taxes to encourage economic activity.
  • Contractionary fiscal policy: a policy that reduces spending or raises taxes to slow economic activity.
  • Why fiscal policy matters: it can affect production, employment, and overall economic activity.
  • Key idea: governments can use spending and tax decisions to respond to economic conditions.
