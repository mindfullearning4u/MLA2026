UNIT 04 | LESSON 4

Guided Practice

Practice explaining the purpose of regulation and the tradeoffs it can create. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A government requires food companies to list ingredients and nutrition information on packages.
  • A city limits pollution from factories located near neighborhoods and schools.
  • A new safety rule requires toy companies to test products more carefully before selling them.

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

Respond:

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the main purpose of the regulation.
  3. Explain one public benefit created by the regulation.
  4. Explain one economic cost or tradeoff the regulation may create.
  5. Write one sentence explaining whether the regulation seems economically reasonable and why.

⚠️Reminder: Good analysis includes both the benefit of the rule and the tradeoff it may create.
