Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe how multiple Unit 04 concepts are used together.

Scenario: A city raises sales taxes to fund public bus service and also requires buses to meet new emissions rules.

Government role: The city is providing a public service and using regulation to reduce pollution.

Taxes and revenue: The higher sales tax raises money to support the bus system.

Regulation: The emissions rule is designed to protect the environment and public health.

Economic tradeoff: The policy may improve transportation and air quality, but higher taxes and stricter rules may increase costs for residents or service providers.

Conclusion: The policy combines government intervention, taxation, and regulation to address public needs, but it also creates tradeoffs that should be evaluated.

Why this works:

  • It connects multiple Unit 04 concepts in one scenario.
  • It identifies both policy benefits and economic tradeoffs.
  • It shows how government actions can work together.
