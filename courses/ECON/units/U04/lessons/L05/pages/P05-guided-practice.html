Guided Practice

Apply all Unit 04 concepts to analyze a policy scenario.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A state raises gasoline taxes to repair roads and bridges.
  • A city increases spending on water treatment and also requires factories to reduce pollution in nearby rivers.
  • A government lowers taxes for small businesses while funding new public transportation projects.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Explain what role government is playing in the scenario.
  3. Identify whether the policy involves taxes, fiscal policy, regulation, or more than one of these.
  4. Explain one intended public benefit of the policy.
  5. Explain one possible economic tradeoff or cost.
  6. Write one complete paragraph explaining the overall policy impact.

⚠️ Reminder: You must connect multiple Unit 04 concepts together. Do not describe only one part of the scenario.
