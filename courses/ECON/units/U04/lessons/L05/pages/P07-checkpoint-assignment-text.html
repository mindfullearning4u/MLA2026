Lesson 5 Checkpoint - Policy Impact Readiness

This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply Unit 04 concepts in a complete policy analysis.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A state raises gasoline taxes to repair roads and bridges.
- A city increases spending on water treatment and also requires factories to reduce pollution in nearby rivers.
- A government lowers taxes for small businesses while funding new public transportation projects.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- An explanation of what role government is playing in the scenario.
- Identification of whether the policy involves taxes, fiscal policy, regulation, or more than one of these.
- An explanation of one intended public benefit of the policy.
- An explanation of one possible economic tradeoff or cost.
- One complete paragraph explaining the overall policy impact.

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the analysis does not clearly connect multiple Unit 04 concepts, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint prepares you for the Unit 04 Final Assessment and contributes to unit mastery.
