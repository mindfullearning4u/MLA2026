Worked Example

💡 Read the sample scenario and observe why government may play a role in the economy.

Scenario: A city needs streetlights so roads are safer at night, but no private company wants to install lights on every street without guaranteed payment from each person who uses them.

Public good: Streetlights are a public good because many people can use them at the same time.

Market problem: A private business may not earn enough money because people can benefit from the lights even if they do not pay directly.

Government role: The city government can fund and provide the streetlights for the public.

Economic reasoning: Government intervention makes sense because the market may not provide enough of this public good on its own.

Why this works:

  • It identifies a public good clearly.
  • It shows why the market may fail to provide that good.
  • It explains why government intervention may be necessary.
