Guided Practice

Practice identifying why government may play a role in the economy. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📘 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A town pays for fire protection services that all residents can use when emergencies happen.
  • A factory dumps waste into a river, affecting people who live nearby.
  • A government requires companies to test food products for safety before selling them.

📘 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify whether the scenario shows a public good, a market failure, or regulation.
  3. Explain why the market may not solve the problem well on its own.
  4. State what role government is playing in the scenario.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why the government action makes economic sense.

⚠️ Reminder: Focus on why government is involved and what economic problem or public need is being addressed.
