Lesson 1 Checkpoint - Government Role Notebook Evidence

This checkpoint verifies your ability to explain why government sometimes intervenes in the economy and identify the economic reason for that action.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
- A town pays for fire protection services that all residents can use when emergencies happen.
- A factory dumps waste into a river, affecting people who live nearby.
- A government requires companies to test food products for safety before selling them.

Required evidence:
- The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
- Whether the scenario shows a public good, a market failure, or regulation.
- An explanation of why the market may not solve the problem well on its own.
- A description of the government's role in the scenario.
- One sentence explaining why the government action makes economic sense.

Accepted submission formats:
- jpg
- png
- pdf

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the government's role and economic reasoning are unclear, the checkpoint is not complete.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
