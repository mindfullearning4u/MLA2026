Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe healthy digital decision-making.

Scenario: A student stays up late scrolling on their phone every night and feels tired and unfocused during school.

Unhealthy habits:

  • Using a phone right before bed.
  • Ignoring sleep needs.
  • Allowing notifications to interrupt rest.

Healthier habits:

  • Setting a screen-free time before bed.
  • Charging the phone outside the bedroom.
  • Prioritizing sleep and focus.

Why this works:

  • It supports physical and mental health.
  • It improves focus and learning.
  • It demonstrates intentional technology use.
