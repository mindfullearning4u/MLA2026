🎯 Lesson Checkpoint – Digital Wellness, Balance & Healthy Tech Habits
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify unhealthy digital habits and propose healthier alternatives.

Important: You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:

  • Using a phone late at night.
  • Constantly checking notifications during study time.
  • Feeling stressed or distracted after extended screen use.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected.
  • One unhealthy digital habit.
  • An explanation of how it affects wellbeing or learning.
  • One healthier alternative habit.
  • One sentence explaining why digital balance matters.

If habits or alternatives are unclear or unrealistic, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
