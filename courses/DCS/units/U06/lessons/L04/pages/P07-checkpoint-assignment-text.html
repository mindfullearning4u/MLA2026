Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Digital Wellness, Balance & Healthy Tech Habits
This checkpoint verifies your ability to reflect on digital habits and propose healthier alternatives.

You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:
• Using a phone late at night.
• Constantly checking notifications during study time.
• Feeling stressed or distracted after extended screen use.

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected.
• One unhealthy digital habit.
• An explanation of how it affects wellbeing or learning.
• One healthier alternative habit.
• One sentence explaining why digital balance matters.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If habits or alternatives are unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
