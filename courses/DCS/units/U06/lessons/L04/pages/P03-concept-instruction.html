Unit 06 | Lesson 4

Concept Instruction

Technology provides powerful tools for learning, communication, and creativity. However, excessive or unbalanced use can impact sleep, focus, mental health, and relationships.

Digital wellness involves being aware of how technology affects mood, productivity, and wellbeing. Healthy habits include setting limits, taking breaks, and prioritizing offline responsibilities.

Responsible digital citizens recognize when technology use supports their goals - and when it becomes a distraction or stressor.

🧠Think: How can too much screen time affect learning or mental health?

⚠️Common mistake: Believing that constant connectivity is necessary or harmless.
