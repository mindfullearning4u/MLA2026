Guided Practice

Practice reflecting on digital habits and identifying healthier alternatives.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • Using a phone late at night.
  • Constantly checking notifications during study time.
  • Feeling stressed or distracted after extended screen use.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify one unhealthy digital habit.
  3. Explain how it affects wellbeing or learning.
  4. Describe one healthier alternative habit.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why digital balance matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Awareness is the first step toward healthier habits.
