Guided Practice

Practice applying multiple digital citizenship skills to realistic situations.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • Participating in a group chat with mixed content.
  • Sharing information in an online class forum.
  • Responding to a disagreement on social media.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify two digital citizenship skills involved.
  3. Explain how those skills apply to the situation.
  4. Describe one responsible action.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why applying multiple skills matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Real situations require layered thinking, not single-skill responses.
