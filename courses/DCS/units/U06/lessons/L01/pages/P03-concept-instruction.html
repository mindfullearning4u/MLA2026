Concept - Applying Digital Citizenship Skills

Real-world digital situations rarely involve just one skill. Online decisions often require safety awareness, ethical judgment, respectful communication, and responsibility at the same time.

For example, sharing content may involve privacy concerns, copyright rules, tone of communication, and long-term impact. Understanding how these skills interact leads to stronger decision-making.

Applying digital citizenship skills together helps individuals navigate complex online environments confidently and responsibly.

🧠 Think: Why is it risky to focus on only one digital skill at a time?

⚠️ Common mistake: Treating digital citizenship skills as separate instead of connected.
