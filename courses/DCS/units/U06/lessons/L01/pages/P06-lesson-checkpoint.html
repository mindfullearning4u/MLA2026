🎯 Lesson Checkpoint - Applying Digital Citizenship Skills
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply multiple digital citizenship skills to a real situation.

Important: You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:

  • Participating in a group chat with mixed content.
  • Sharing information in an online class forum.
  • Responding to a disagreement on social media.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected.
  • Two digital citizenship skills involved.
  • An explanation of how those skills apply.
  • One responsible action.
  • One sentence explaining why integrated skill use matters.

If the skills are unclear or not applied correctly, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
