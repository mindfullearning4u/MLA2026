Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe integrated skill use.

Scenario: A student is invited to join an online group chat where classmates share jokes and images. Some content may be inappropriate.

Poor application:

  • Joining without considering safety or consequences.
  • Sharing content to fit in.

Effective application:

  • Considering privacy and safety.
  • Choosing respectful communication.
  • Deciding whether participation aligns with ethical values.

Why this works:

  • It applies multiple digital skills at once.
  • It considers impact and responsibility.
  • It supports safe and ethical participation.
