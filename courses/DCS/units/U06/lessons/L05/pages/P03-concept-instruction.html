Concept - Creating a Personal Digital Citizenship Action Plan

A digital citizenship action plan helps turn knowledge into daily practice. Rather than reacting to problems, an action plan prepares you to make responsible choices before challenges arise.

An effective plan addresses safety, ethics, communication, collaboration, and digital wellness. It reflects personal values, realistic habits, and accountability.

Responsible digital citizens revisit and adjust their plans as technology and responsibilities change.

🧠 Think: Why is planning more effective than reacting when it comes to digital behavior?

⚠️ Common mistake: Believing that knowing the rules is the same as practicing responsibility.
