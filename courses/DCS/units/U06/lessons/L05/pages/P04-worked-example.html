Worked Example

👀 Read the example and observe how a personal plan is structured.

Example Plan (Excerpt):

  • Safety: I will not share personal information with people I do not know.
  • Ethics: I will submit only original work and follow rules for AI use.
  • Communication: I will communicate respectfully, even during disagreements.
  • Wellness: I will set screen-time limits during school nights.

Why this works:

  • It is specific and realistic.
  • It applies multiple digital citizenship skills.
  • It focuses on habits, not just rules.
