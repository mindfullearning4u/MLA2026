Guided Practice

Begin drafting your own digital citizenship action plan.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write one personal goal related to digital citizenship.
  2. List one safety habit you will follow.
  3. List one ethical or integrity-based commitment.
  4. List one communication or collaboration guideline you will use.
  5. List one digital wellness or balance habit.

⚠️ Reminder: Your plan should be realistic, personal, and actionable.
