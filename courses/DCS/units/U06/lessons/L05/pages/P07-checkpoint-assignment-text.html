Unit 06 Checkpoint - Personal Digital Citizenship Action Plan
This checkpoint verifies your ability to synthesize digital citizenship concepts into a personal action plan.

Required evidence:
• One clear digital citizenship goal.
• One safety habit you will follow.
• One ethical or integrity-based commitment.
• One communication or collaboration guideline.
• One digital wellness or balance habit.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion, clarity, and alignment with course concepts.

If the plan is incomplete, unrealistic, or does not reflect multiple digital citizenship skills, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to final course mastery and completion.
