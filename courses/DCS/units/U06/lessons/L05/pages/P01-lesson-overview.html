Lesson Overview

This lesson brings together everything you have learned about digital citizenship, safety, ethics, communication, responsibility, and wellness. You will create a personal action plan that demonstrates how you will apply these skills intentionally in your own digital life.

A strong digital citizen does not rely on rules alone. Instead, they develop habits, boundaries, and values that guide responsible behavior over time. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of mastery, reflection, and intentional planning.
