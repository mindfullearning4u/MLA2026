Concept – Evaluating Real-World Online Scenarios

Online situations are often complex and involve multiple factors at once. Evaluating a scenario means identifying risks, understanding context, and considering how actions affect others.

Responsible evaluation includes asking questions such as: What information is missing? Who could be affected? What are the short- and long-term consequences?

Strong digital citizens evaluate before they respond, share, or participate.

🧠 Think: Why can reacting quickly online lead to poor decisions?

⚠️ Common mistake: Responding emotionally without considering consequences.
