Guided Practice

Practice evaluating realistic online situations that require careful judgment.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • Receiving a message from an unknown account.
  • Seeing a post that shares unverified information.
  • Being added to an online group without consent.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify two concerns or risks.
  3. Explain why the situation requires evaluation.
  4. Describe one responsible response.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why evaluation matters online.

⚠️ Reminder: Evaluation protects safety, reputation, and integrity.
