🎯 Lesson Checkpoint – Evaluating Real-World Online Scenarios
This checkpoint verifies your ability to evaluate an online situation and justify a responsible response.

Important: You must choose ONE of the guided practice scenarios:

  • Receiving a message from an unknown account.
  • Seeing a post that shares unverified information.
  • Being added to an online group without consent.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected.
  • Two concerns or risks involved.
  • An explanation of why evaluation is necessary.
  • One responsible response.
  • One sentence explaining why careful evaluation matters.

If risks are unclear or the response does not show responsible judgment, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
