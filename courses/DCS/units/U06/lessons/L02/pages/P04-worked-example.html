Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe careful evaluation.

Scenario: A student receives a message from someone they do not know asking them to join a private online group.

Poor evaluation:

  • Joining immediately out of curiosity.
  • Sharing personal information.

Responsible evaluation:

  • Questioning who sent the message.
  • Considering privacy and safety risks.
  • Choosing not to engage or seeking adult guidance.

Why this works:

  • It considers safety and consequences.
  • It avoids unnecessary risk.
  • It demonstrates responsible judgment.
