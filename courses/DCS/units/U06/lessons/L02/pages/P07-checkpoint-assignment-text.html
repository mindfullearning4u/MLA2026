Lesson 2 Checkpoint – Evaluating Real-World Online Scenarios
This checkpoint verifies your ability to evaluate an online situation and justify a responsible response.

You must choose ONE of the guided practice scenarios:
• Receiving a message from an unknown account.
• Seeing a post that shares unverified information.
• Being added to an online group without consent.

Required evidence:
• The scenario you selected.
• Two concerns or risks involved.
• An explanation of why evaluation is necessary.
• One responsible response.
• One sentence explaining why careful evaluation matters.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If risks or reasoning are unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
