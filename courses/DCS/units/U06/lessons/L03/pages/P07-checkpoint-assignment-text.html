Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Digital Collaboration, Communication & Project Skills
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply collaboration and communication skills responsibly.

You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:
• A group member is not responding to messages.
• Confusion exists about who is responsible for tasks.
• A disagreement arises about project direction.

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected.
• One communication or collaboration challenge.
• An explanation of how the challenge affects the group.
• One respectful and responsible response.
• One sentence explaining why collaboration skills matter.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If communication challenges or responses are unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
