Guided Practice

Practice applying collaboration and communication skills to common online group situations.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A group member is not responding to messages.
  • Confusion exists about who is responsible for tasks.
  • A disagreement arises about project direction.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify one communication challenge.
  3. Explain why collaboration is affected.
  4. Describe one respectful and responsible response.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why communication matters in collaboration.

⚠️ Reminder: Clear communication strengthens teamwork and outcomes.
