Lesson 5: Unit Assessment: Cyber Safety Scenario

This lesson is the Unit 03 Assessment. Students apply everything learned about online privacy, account security, scams and phishing, cyberbullying, and safe communication.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Students will analyze a multi-part cyber safety scenario and answer questions that assess their ability to identify risks, protect personal information, and choose safe, responsible responses. This assessment includes multiple-choice, true/false, and scenario-based reasoning questions.
