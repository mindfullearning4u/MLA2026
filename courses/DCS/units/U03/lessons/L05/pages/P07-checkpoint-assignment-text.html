Lesson 5 Checkpoint – Cyber Safety Scenario

This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply Unit 03 concepts to a scenario before completing the assessment.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
• A student receives a message saying their device is infected and must “download a fix.”
• Someone threatens a classmate in a group chat and encourages others to join in.
• A friend sends a link that looks suspicious and says, “You have to see this!”

Required evidence:
• The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
• The main issue (privacy, security, phishing, malware, or cyberbullying).
• An explanation of why the situation is unsafe or harmful.
• A revised version of the situation showing the most responsible cyber safety response.
• One sentence explaining why cyber safety skills matter.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the safety explanation is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
