Guided Practice

Practice applying Unit 03 concepts to a cyber safety scenario. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student receives a message saying their device is infected and must “download a fix.”
  • Someone threatens a classmate in a group chat and encourages others to join in.
  • A friend sends a link that looks suspicious and says, “You have to see this!”

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the main issue (privacy, security, phishing, malware, or cyberbullying).
  3. Explain why the situation is unsafe or harmful.
  4. Describe the most responsible cyber safety response.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why cyber safety skills matter.

⚠️ Reminder: The assessment will require you to apply these same skills.
