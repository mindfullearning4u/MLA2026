Worked Example

👀 Review this example of a scenario-based assessment question.

Scenario: A student receives a DM saying, "Your account has been flagged. Click this link to verify your login or it will be deleted." The link looks suspicious and the message is not from an official account.

Question: What is the safest response?

Correct answer:

  • Do not click the link. Delete the message and report it as phishing.

Why this is correct:

  • It avoids giving information to scammers.
  • It prevents malware or account theft.
  • It models safe digital behavior.
