Concept – Unit Assessment Overview

This assessment measures your understanding of all concepts from Unit 03. You will apply your knowledge to a realistic cyber safety scenario involving privacy, security, scams, and safe communication.

The assessment includes:

  • Multiple‑choice questions about key vocabulary and safety practices.
  • True/false questions about online risks and safe behavior.
  • Scenario‑based reasoning where you choose the safest and most responsible response.

Your goal is to demonstrate mastery by identifying risks, protecting personal information, and choosing safe, ethical responses.

🧠 Think: What is the safest response when a message, link, or situation feels suspicious?

⚠️ Common mistake: Clicking or responding before thinking through the risks.
