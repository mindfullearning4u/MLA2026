Guided Practice

Practice identifying cyberbullying and choosing safe responses. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A group chat starts making fun of a student’s appearance.
  • Someone posts a private photo of a classmate without permission.
  • A student receives repeated messages telling them to “stop talking” or “no one likes you.”

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify why the situation is cyberbullying.
  3. Explain why responding emotionally could make things worse.
  4. Describe a safe, responsible response.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why data protection matters during online communication.

⚠️ Reminder: You never have to handle cyberbullying alone—safe responses protect you.
