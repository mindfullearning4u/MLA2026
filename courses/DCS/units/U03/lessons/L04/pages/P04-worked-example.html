💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample scenario and observe how safe communication protects you.

Scenario: A student receives repeated messages saying, "Everyone hates you. Just leave the group." The student feels upset and wants to respond angrily.

Why this is cyberbullying:

  • The messages are repeated and harmful.
  • The goal is to embarrass or isolate the student.
  • The behavior is intentional and targeted.

Safe response:

  • Do not reply.
  • Take screenshots of the messages.
  • Block the sender.
  • Report the behavior to the platform and a trusted adult.

✅Why this works:

  • It prevents escalation.
  • It protects the student's emotional safety.
  • It uses tools designed to stop harmful behavior.
