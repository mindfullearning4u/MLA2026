🎯 Lesson Checkpoint – Cyberbullying, Safe Communication & Data Protection
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify cyberbullying and choose safe responses.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:

  • A group chat starts making fun of a student’s appearance.
  • Someone posts a private photo of a classmate without permission.
  • A student receives repeated messages telling them to “stop talking” or “no one likes you.”

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • An explanation of why the situation is cyberbullying.
  • A description of why responding emotionally could escalate the situation.
  • A revised version of the situation showing a safe, responsible response.
  • One sentence explaining why data protection matters during communication.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the explanation is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
