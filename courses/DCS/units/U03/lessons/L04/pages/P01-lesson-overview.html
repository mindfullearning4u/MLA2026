Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: cyberbullying is harmful online behavior, and responsible digital citizens know how to respond safely and protect their personal data. Students learn how to recognize cyberbullying, use reporting tools, communicate safely, and avoid exposing personal information during online interactions.

A strong digital citizen stays safe by responding calmly, protecting their data, and seeking help when needed. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of learning and engagement.
