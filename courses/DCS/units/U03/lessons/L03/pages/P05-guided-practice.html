Guided Practice

Practice identifying phishing red flags and choosing safe responses. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • You receive a message saying you won a prize, but you never entered a contest.
  • A pop‑up claims your device is infected and tells you to “download this tool now.”
  • A friend sends a link that looks suspicious and says, “OMG is this you?”

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify the phishing or scam red flags.
  3. Explain why the situation is unsafe.
  4. Describe a safe response to the situation.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why you should avoid suspicious links.

⚠️ Reminder: If something feels off, it probably is.
