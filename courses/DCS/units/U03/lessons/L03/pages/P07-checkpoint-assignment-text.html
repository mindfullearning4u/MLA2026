Lesson 3 Checkpoint – Scams, Phishing, Malware & Safe Downloads

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify phishing red flags and choose safe responses.

You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:
• You receive a message saying you won a prize, but you never entered a contest.
• A pop‑up claims your device is infected and tells you to “download this tool now.”
• A friend sends a link that looks suspicious and says, “OMG is this you?”

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
• The phishing or scam red flags.
• An explanation of why the situation is unsafe.
• A revised version of the situation showing a safe response.
• One sentence explaining why you should avoid suspicious links.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the red‑flag explanation is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
