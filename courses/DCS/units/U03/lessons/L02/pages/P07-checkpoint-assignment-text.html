Lesson 2 Checkpoint – Digital Access, Inclusion & Participation

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify access barriers and explain how to make digital spaces more inclusive.

You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:
• An online study group only meets on video calls, and some members have slow internet.
• A gaming community uses small text and no captions, making it hard for some players to participate.
• A class discussion board assumes everyone has a laptop, even though several students only have phones.

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
• One access barrier that prevents full participation.
• An explanation of why the situation is not inclusive.
• A revised version of the situation that shows a more inclusive and accessible digital choice.
• One sentence explaining why digital inclusion matters.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the access barrier and inclusive revision are unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
