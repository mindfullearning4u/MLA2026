Concept – Passwords, Authentication & Account Security

Passwords are the first line of defense for your online accounts. A strong password is long, unique, and difficult for others to guess. Short or reused passwords make it easy for hackers to break into accounts.

Authentication is the process of proving you are the real account owner. The safest method is MFA (multi‑factor authentication), which requires two or more steps to log in—such as a password plus a code sent to your phone.

Using strong passwords and MFA protects your accounts from hacking, identity theft, and unauthorized access.

🧠 Think: If someone guessed your password, what could they access about you?

⚠️ Common mistake: Using the same password for multiple accounts or choosing passwords that are easy to guess.
