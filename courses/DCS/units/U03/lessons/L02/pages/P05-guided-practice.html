Guided Practice

Practice identifying weak passwords and choosing safer login methods. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A student uses “12345678” as their password because it’s easy to remember.
  • Someone refuses to turn on MFA because “it takes too long.”
  • A friend shares their password with someone they trust “just in case.”

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify the security risk.
  3. Explain why this choice is unsafe.
  4. Describe a safer account security choice.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why strong passwords matter.

⚠️ Reminder: If someone else knows your password, it is no longer secure.
