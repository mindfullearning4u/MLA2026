💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample scenario and observe how privacy settings protect personal information.

Scenario: A student downloads a new social media app. The default settings allow the app to track their location, access their contacts, and make their profile public.

Privacy risks:

  • Strangers can see their posts and profile.
  • The app knows their exact location.
  • Contacts may be shared without permission.

Safer privacy choices:

  • Switch the profile to private.
  • Turn off location sharing.
  • Deny access to contacts.
  • Review all privacy settings before using the app.

✅Why this works:

  • It limits who can see personal information.
  • It reduces the amount of data the app collects.
  • It protects the student's identity and safety.
