Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: online privacy means understanding how your data is collected, shared, and protected when you use digital tools. Students learn how websites and apps gather information, how privacy settings work, and how to make safe choices that protect personal data.

A strong digital citizen understands what information is being collected, how it may be used, and how to control their privacy settings. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of learning and engagement.
