Concept – Understanding Online Privacy

Every time you use a website, app, or device, information about you is collected. This includes your location, search history, clicks, likes, and even how long you look at certain posts.

Online privacy is your ability to control what information you share and who can access it. Companies use your data to personalize ads, recommend content, or improve their services.

Privacy settings allow you to limit what others can see, including your posts, location, and profile details. Reviewing these settings regularly helps protect your personal information.

Understanding privacy helps you make safer choices and avoid exposing information that could be misused.

🧠 Think: What information about you do apps and websites know that you don’t always think about?

⚠️ Common mistake: Assuming that “private account” means no one can access your information.
