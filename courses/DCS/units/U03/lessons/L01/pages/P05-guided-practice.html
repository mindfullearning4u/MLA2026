Guided Practice

Practice identifying privacy risks and choosing safer settings. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • An app automatically shares your location with all followers.
  • A website asks to save your login information on a shared device.
  • A game requests access to your microphone and camera for no clear reason.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify the privacy risk.
  3. Explain why this risk could be unsafe.
  4. Describe a safer privacy choice you could make.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why online privacy matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Apps often collect more information than you realize-always check the settings.
