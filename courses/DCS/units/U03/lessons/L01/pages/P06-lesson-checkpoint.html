🎯 Lesson Checkpoint – Understanding Online Privacy
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify privacy risks and choose safer settings.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:

  • An app automatically shares your location with all followers.
  • A website asks to save your login information on a shared device.
  • A game requests access to your microphone and camera for no clear reason.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • The privacy risk in the situation.
  • An explanation of why the risk is unsafe.
  • A revised version of the situation showing a safer privacy choice.
  • One sentence explaining why online privacy matters.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the privacy explanation is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
