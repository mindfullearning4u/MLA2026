Guided Practice

Practice identifying responsible and irresponsible digital citizenship. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A friend shares a screenshot of a private conversation without permission.
  • A classmate posts a comment that sounds rude, but you are not sure if they meant it that way.
  • You see someone spreading a rumor about another student online.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify one example of irresponsible digital citizenship in that situation.
  3. Rewrite the situation to show a responsible digital citizenship choice.
  4. Write one sentence explaining why responsible digital behavior matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Digital citizenship requires respectful communication, safe choices, and awareness of how your actions affect others.
