💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample scenario and observe how digital citizenship is applied.

Scenario: A student posts a joke online that another student finds hurtful. The original poster says, "It was just a joke. It doesn't matter because it's online."

Not responsible: Ignoring the impact of the post or blaming the person who felt hurt.

Responsible digital citizenship:

  • Acknowledge that online actions affect real people.
  • Edit or remove the post if it caused harm.
  • Communicate respectfully and avoid harmful language.
  • Consider how posts may be interpreted before sharing.

✅Why this works:

  • It recognizes that online behavior has real-world consequences.
  • It shows respect for others' feelings and experiences.
  • It demonstrates responsible decision-making in digital spaces.
