Concept - What Is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship means using technology in a way that is responsible, safe, respectful, and ethical. Every action you take online-posting, commenting, sharing, messaging-reflects your digital behavior and affects others.

A strong digital citizen understands both rights (such as privacy and access) and responsibilities (such as respectful communication and protecting personal information). These expectations apply in school, at home, and in every online community.

🧠 Think: How does your behavior online affect the people who see your posts, messages, or comments?

⚠️ Common mistake: Believing that online actions "don't matter" because they are not face-to-face. Digital behavior has real-world consequences.
