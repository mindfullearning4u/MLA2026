Lesson 4 Checkpoint - Managing Online Conflict & Responsible Decision-Making

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify a conflict trigger and choose a safe, ethical response.

You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:
• Someone replies "lol you're clueless" to your comment in a group chat.
• A classmate posts your message out of context to make you look bad.
• A friend becomes angry when you don't respond immediately and sends multiple upset messages.

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
• The conflict trigger in the situation.
• An explanation of why the situation could escalate.
• A revised version of the situation showing a safe, responsible response.
• One sentence explaining why responsible decision-making matters online.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the safe response is unclear or unsafe, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
