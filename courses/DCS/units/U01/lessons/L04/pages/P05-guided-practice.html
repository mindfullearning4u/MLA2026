Guided Practice

Read the scenario below and answer the questions.

Scenario:

Jordan posts an opinion in a group chat. Another student replies with: "That’s a dumb idea." Jordan feels upset and wants to respond immediately.

Questions:

  1. Why could this situation turn into a larger conflict?
  2. What mistake might Jordan make if they respond emotionally?
  3. What is one responsible way Jordan could respond to reduce conflict?
Reminder: Use complete sentences and apply conflict management strategies from the lesson.
