Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: responsible digital citizens manage online conflict by making thoughtful decisions, de-escalating situations, and choosing safe and ethical responses. Students learn how conflicts can develop online and how to respond in ways that reduce harm and maintain respect.

A strong digital citizen does not react impulsively. Instead, they pause, evaluate the situation, and choose actions that are safe, respectful, and responsible. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of learning and engagement.
