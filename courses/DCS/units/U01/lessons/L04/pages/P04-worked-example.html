💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample scenario and observe how responsible decision-making is applied.

Scenario: A student posts a comment on your project saying, "This is terrible. Do you even try?"

Conflict triggers:

  • The comment is rude and insulting.
  • It invites an emotional reaction.

Not responsible:

  • Responding with anger or insults.
  • Posting publicly about the person to get others involved.

Responsible digital citizenship:

  • Pause before responding.
  • Decide whether replying is necessary or helpful.
  • Use reporting tools if the comment violates platform rules.
  • Disengage if the person is trying to provoke conflict.

✅Why this works:

  • It prevents escalation.
  • It protects your emotional safety.
  • It uses platform tools appropriately.
