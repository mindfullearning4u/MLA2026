Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: digital access and inclusion ensure that all people can participate fully and safely in online spaces. Students learn how factors such as income, location, disability, and available technology affect a person’s ability to access digital tools and opportunities.

A strong digital citizen understands the digital divide, recognizes access barriers, and supports inclusive participation for people with diverse digital experiences. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of learning and engagement.
