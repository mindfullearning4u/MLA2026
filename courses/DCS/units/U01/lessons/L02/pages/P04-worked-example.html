Worked Example

Scenario: A teacher assigns an online project that requires internet access at home.

Problem: One student does not have internet at home and cannot complete the assignment.

Responsible digital citizenship:

  • The teacher provides alternative options (printed materials or school access).
  • Students avoid judging or criticizing the classmate.
  • Support is offered to ensure everyone can participate.

Why this works:

  • Recognizes unequal access
  • Promotes fairness and inclusion
  • Supports equal learning opportunities
