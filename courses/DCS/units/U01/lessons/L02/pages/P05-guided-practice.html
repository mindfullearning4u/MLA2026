Guided Practice

Read the scenario below carefully and answer the questions.

Scenario:

A student named Luis shares a tablet with three siblings at home. He can only use it for a short time each evening. Because of this, he cannot always complete online assignments or participate in live class discussions.

Questions:

  1. What challenge is Luis experiencing related to digital access?
  2. How might this situation affect his learning and participation?
  3. What is one possible solution that could help Luis?
Reminder: Use complete sentences and refer to concepts from the lesson such as digital access, digital divide, or inclusion.
