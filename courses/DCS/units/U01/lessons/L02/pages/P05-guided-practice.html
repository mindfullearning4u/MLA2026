Guided Practice

Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook. Incomplete work must be revised before proceeding.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A student cannot join a video call due to weak internet.
  • A group project requires a device, but one student does not have one.
  • A classmate finishes work faster because they have better technology.

📓 Notebook Directions:

  1. Write the situation.
  2. Explain the access problem.
  3. Describe a fair and responsible solution.
  4. Explain why fairness in access matters.
