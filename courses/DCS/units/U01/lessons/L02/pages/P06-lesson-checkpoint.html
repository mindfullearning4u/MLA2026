Lesson Checkpoint

Answer the questions below. This checkpoint will be graded by your Teacher of Record (TOR).

  1. Define digital access and explain why it is important in today’s world.
  2. What is the digital divide? Provide one clear example.
  3. Describe how limited access to technology can affect a person’s education or opportunities.
  4. Explain one way individuals, schools, or communities can support digital inclusion.
Important: Write your answers clearly and completely. Support your responses with ideas from the lesson.
