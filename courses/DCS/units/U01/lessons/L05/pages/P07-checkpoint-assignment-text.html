Lesson 5 Checkpoint – Unit Assessment Readiness

This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply Unit 01 concepts to a scenario before completing the assessment.

You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:
• A student is excluded from an online group because they only have a phone.
• A classmate misreads your message and becomes upset.
• Someone posts a rude comment about your work in a shared space.

Required evidence:
• The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
• The main issue (access, communication, conflict, or decision-making).
• An explanation of why the situation is unsafe, unfair, or disrespectful.
• A revised version of the situation showing the most responsible response.
• One sentence explaining why responsible choices matter in digital spaces.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the responsible response is unclear or unsafe, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
