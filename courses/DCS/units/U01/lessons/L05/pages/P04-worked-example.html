Worked Example

👀 Review this example of a scenario-based assessment question.

Scenario: A group project chat moves quickly, and one student with slow internet keeps missing messages. Another student posts, "Just keep up or don't bother."

Question: What is the most responsible response?

Correct answer:

  • "Let's slow down and make sure everyone can participate. Not everyone has the same internet speed."

Why this is correct:

  • It recognizes an access barrier.
  • It supports inclusive participation.
  • It de-escalates the situation.
