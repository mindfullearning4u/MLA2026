Lesson 3 Checkpoint – Respectful Online Communication & Digital Etiquette

This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify unclear or disrespectful tone and revise it to be respectful and clear.

You must choose ONE of the three situations:
• “Why are you taking so long? Hurry up.”
• “Whatever. Do what you want.”
• “You clearly didn’t read the instructions.”

Required evidence:
• Selected situation
• Explanation of tone issue
• Revised respectful message
• Explanation of why tone matters

Accepted formats:
• Photo (jpg/png)
• PDF
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review your submission.

If incomplete or unclear, revision is required.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
