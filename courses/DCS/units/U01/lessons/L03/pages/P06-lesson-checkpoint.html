Lesson Checkpoint

Answer the questions below. This checkpoint will be graded by your Teacher of Record (TOR).

  1. Define digital etiquette and explain why it is important in online communication.
  2. What is tone in digital communication, and why can it be misunderstood?
  3. Provide one example of a message that could be misinterpreted and explain why.
  4. Rewrite your example to make it more respectful and clear.
Important: Write complete and thoughtful responses. Support your answers using ideas from the lesson.
