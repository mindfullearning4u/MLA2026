🎯 Lesson Checkpoint – Respectful Online Communication & Digital Etiquette
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify unclear or disrespectful tone and revise it to be respectful and clear.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:

  • “Why are you taking so long? Hurry up.”
  • “Whatever. Do what you want.”
  • “You clearly didn’t read the instructions.”

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • An explanation of what makes the tone unclear or disrespectful.
  • A revised version of the message that is respectful, clear, and appropriate.
  • One sentence explaining why tone matters in online communication.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the tone revision is unclear or still disrespectful, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
