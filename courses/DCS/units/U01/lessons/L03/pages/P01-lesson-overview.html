Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: respectful online communication requires clear tone, thoughtful wording, and awareness of how messages may be interpreted. Students learn how misunderstandings happen in text-based communication and how to apply digital etiquette across different platforms.

A strong digital citizen communicates respectfully, avoids harmful or unclear messages, and adapts their tone to the platform and audience. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of learning and engagement.
