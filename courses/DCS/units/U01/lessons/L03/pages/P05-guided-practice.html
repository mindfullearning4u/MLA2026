UNIT 01 | LESSON 3

Guided Practice

Practice identifying unclear or disrespectful tone and revising it to be respectful and clear. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📒Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • "Why are you taking so long? Hurry up."
  • "Whatever. Do what you want."
  • "You clearly didn't read the instructions."

📝Notebook Directions (Required):

Respond:

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify what makes the tone unclear, rude, or disrespectful.
  3. Rewrite the message to be respectful, clear, and appropriate.
  4. Write one sentence explaining why tone matters in online communication.

⚠️Reminder: Respectful communication builds trust and prevents unnecessary conflict.
