Guided Practice

Practice verifying information using fact-checking strategies.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A post claims a major event is happening but provides no sources.
  • An article shares statistics without explaining where they came from.
  • A video makes a strong claim but links only to personal blogs.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify one reason the information should be verified.
  3. Describe one credible source you would check.
  4. Explain how you would confirm whether the claim is accurate.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why verification matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Responsible sharing requires verification, not assumptions.
