Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe how verification is applied.

Scenario: A post claims that schools will be closed nationwide next week. The post is widely shared but does not link to official announcements.

Unverified response:

  • Believing the claim because many people shared it.
  • Sharing the post without checking sources.

Verified response:

  • Checking official school district or government websites.
  • Looking for confirmation from multiple trusted news outlets.
  • Not sharing the information until it is confirmed.

Why this works:

  • It relies on credible, authoritative sources.
  • It prevents unnecessary confusion or panic.
  • It supports responsible digital behavior.
