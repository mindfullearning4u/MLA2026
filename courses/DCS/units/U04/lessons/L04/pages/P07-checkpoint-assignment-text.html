Lesson 4 Checkpoint – Fact-Checking & Verification Tools
This checkpoint verifies your ability to evaluate credibility and confirm information.

You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:
• A post claims a major event is happening but provides no sources.
• An article shares statistics without explaining where they came from.
• A video makes a strong claim but links only to personal blogs.

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected.
• One reason the information should be verified.
• One credible source you would check.
• An explanation of how you would confirm accuracy.
• One sentence explaining why verification matters.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the verification process is unclear or does not demonstrate credible source evaluation, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
