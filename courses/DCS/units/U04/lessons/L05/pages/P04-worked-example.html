💡Worked Example

💡Read the scenario and observe a complete media analysis.

Scenario: A trending post claims that a new law will immediately ban a popular social media platform. The post uses urgent language, encourages users to share quickly, and does not link to official sources.

Media analysis:

  • The language is emotional and designed to create urgency.
  • No credible sources or official announcements are provided.
  • The message encourages sharing before verification.

Responsible response:

  • Pause before reacting or sharing.
  • Check official government or news sources.
  • Compare coverage across multiple reliable outlets.

✅Why this works:

  • It evaluates both content and intent.
  • It avoids emotional manipulation.
  • It supports accurate and responsible information sharing.
