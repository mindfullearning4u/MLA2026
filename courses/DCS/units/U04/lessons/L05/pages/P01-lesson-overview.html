Lesson Overview

This lesson is the Unit 01 Assessment. Students apply everything learned about digital citizenship, including access, inclusion, communication, conflict management, and responsible decision-making.

Students will analyze a multi-part scenario and answer questions that assess their understanding of safe, ethical, and respectful digital behavior. This assessment includes multiple-choice, true/false, and scenario-based reasoning questions.

Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your assessment results as evidence of mastery for Unit 01.
