Lesson Overview

This lesson brings together everything you have learned about media literacy and misinformation. You will analyze a realistic online media scenario and use critical thinking to evaluate credibility, bias, and accuracy.

A strong digital citizen does not react quickly or emotionally to media. Instead, they slow down, ask questions, verify information, and justify their conclusions with clear reasoning. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of mastery and thoughtful analysis.
