Guided Practice

Practice conducting a deeper media analysis using one of the scenarios below.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A viral post claims a health tip works instantly but cites no medical sources.
  • An article uses emotionally charged language to support one side of a controversial issue.
  • A video presents statistics without explaining where the data came from.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify one sign that the information should be questioned.
  3. Explain whether bias or misinformation may be present.
  4. Describe one verification step you would take.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why careful media analysis matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Strong media analysis relies on evidence, not assumptions or emotions.
