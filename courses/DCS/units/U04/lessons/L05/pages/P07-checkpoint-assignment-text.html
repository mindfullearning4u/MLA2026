Unit 04 Checkpoint – Media Analysis Scenario
This checkpoint verifies your ability to evaluate media messages and justify your reasoning.

You must choose ONE of the guided practice scenarios:
• A viral post claims a health tip works instantly but cites no medical sources.
• An article uses emotionally charged language to support one side of a controversial issue.
• A video presents statistics without explaining where the data came from.

Required evidence:
• The scenario you selected.
• One reason the information should be questioned.
• An explanation of whether bias or misinformation may be present.
• One verification step you would take before believing or sharing the content.
• One sentence explaining why media literacy is important.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the analysis is incomplete, unclear, or unsupported by reasoning, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
