Concept - News Sources, Bias & Loaded Language

News and media sources make decisions about what to include, what to exclude, and how to frame information. These choices can introduce bias.

Loaded language uses emotionally charged words to influence opinions rather than present facts neutrally. Recognizing tone and word choice helps readers analyze messages more critically.

🧠 Think: How can two articles about the same event feel very different?

⚠️ Common mistake: Assuming professional formatting means neutral reporting.
