Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe how media literacy is applied.

Scenario: A social media post claims that a new phone app can instantly improve grades. The post includes flashy graphics and hundreds of comments but no sources or evidence.

Not media-literate response:

  • Believing the claim because many people shared it.
  • Downloading the app without checking credibility.
  • Ignoring the lack of evidence or sources.

Media-literate response:

  • Questioning who created the post and why.
  • Looking for reliable sources or reviews.
  • Recognizing emotional appeal and marketing tactics.

Why this works:

  • It evaluates the message instead of accepting it automatically.
  • It considers credibility and purpose.
  • It reduces the risk of misinformation.
