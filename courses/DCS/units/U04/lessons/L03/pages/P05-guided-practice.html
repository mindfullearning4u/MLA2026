Guided Practice

Practice identifying misinformation, disinformation, and algorithm influence.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A viral post shares an alarming claim but later turns out to be incorrect.
  • A misleading video is created to intentionally influence opinions.
  • A user repeatedly sees the same false claim recommended by their feed.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify whether it is misinformation or disinformation.
  3. Explain how algorithms contribute to its spread.
  4. Describe one action a responsible digital citizen should take.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why this matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Algorithms promote engagement, not accuracy.
