Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: false information spreads online through both human behavior and algorithm-driven systems. Students learn the difference between misinformation and disinformation and how algorithms influence what people see online.

A strong digital citizen understands that repeated or popular content is not always accurate. Instead, they recognize how algorithms, emotional reactions, and sharing behavior contribute to the spread of false information.
