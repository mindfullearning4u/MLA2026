Concept - Smart Posting & Profile Management

Smart posting means thinking before you share. Every post, comment, photo, or like sends a message about who you are and becomes part of your digital footprint.

Smart posting includes:

  • avoiding risky or inappropriate content
  • checking photos for personal information in the background
  • thinking about how others may interpret your words
  • choosing posts that reflect your values and goals

Profile management means keeping your online profiles updated, safe, and appropriate. This includes reviewing privacy settings, removing outdated or risky posts, and making sure your profile represents who you want to be.

🧠 Think: If someone looked at your profile for the first time, what would they learn about you?

⚠️ Common mistake: Believing that "private accounts" guarantee safety or that deleted posts disappear forever.
