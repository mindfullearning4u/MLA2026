Guided Practice

Practice identifying risky posts and revising them to be safer and more responsible. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A student posts a picture at a party where others are doing unsafe things.
  • Someone shares a sarcastic comment that could easily be misinterpreted as rude.
  • A friend posts a screenshot of a private conversation without permission.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify what makes the post risky.
  3. Explain how the post could affect the person’s reputation.
  4. Rewrite the post to show a safer, smarter choice.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why smart posting matters.

⚠️ Reminder: If a post could embarrass, expose, or harm someone, it is not a smart post.
