🎯 Lesson Checkpoint - Smart Posting & Profile Management
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify risky posts and revise them to be safer.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:

  • A student posts a picture at a party where others are doing unsafe things.
  • Someone shares a sarcastic comment that could easily be misinterpreted as rude.
  • A friend posts a screenshot of a private conversation without permission.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • An explanation of what makes the post risky.
  • A description of how the post could affect reputation.
  • A revised version of the post showing a safer, smarter choice.
  • One sentence explaining why smart posting matters.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the safety explanation is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
