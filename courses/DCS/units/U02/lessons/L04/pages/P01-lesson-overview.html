Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: responsible digital citizens manage online conflict safely, calmly, and ethically. Students learn how conflict starts, how to de-escalate tense situations, when to disengage, and how to use reporting tools appropriately.

A strong digital citizen recognizes conflict triggers, avoids escalating harmful interactions, and makes decisions that protect themselves and others. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of learning and engagement.
