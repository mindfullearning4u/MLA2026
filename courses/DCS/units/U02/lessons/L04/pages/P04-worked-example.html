Worked Example

👀 Read the sample scenario and observe how smart posting protects reputation.

Scenario: A student posts a joke making fun of a teacher. They think it’s harmless because “only friends will see it.” A friend screenshots it and shares it in another group chat.

Why this is risky:

  • It shows disrespect toward a teacher.
  • It can spread beyond the intended audience.
  • It could affect the student’s reputation at school.

Smarter posting choice:

  • Avoid posting jokes that target specific people.
  • Share humor that does not insult or embarrass others.
  • Think about how the post would look if shared publicly.

Why this works:

  • It prevents misunderstandings and conflict.
  • It protects the student’s reputation.
  • It models responsible online behavior.
