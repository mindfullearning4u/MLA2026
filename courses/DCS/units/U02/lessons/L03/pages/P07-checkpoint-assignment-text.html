Lesson 3 Checkpoint - Digital Footprints & Reputation
This checkpoint verifies your ability to explain how digital footprints affect reputation.

You must choose ONE of the three situations from the guided practice:
• A student posts angry rants every time they lose a game.
• Someone frequently shares risky or inappropriate jokes publicly.
• A student deletes posts often, but screenshots of past arguments still circulate.

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected (must match one of the three options).
• A description of the digital footprint being created.
• An explanation of how this footprint could affect reputation.
• One suggested change that would improve their digital footprint.
• One sentence explaining why digital footprints matter.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If the situation is not one of the three provided options, or if the footprint explanation is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
