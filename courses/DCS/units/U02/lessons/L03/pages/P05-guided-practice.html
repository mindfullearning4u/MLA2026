Guided Practice

Practice analyzing how digital footprints affect reputation. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A student posts angry rants every time they lose a game.
  • Someone frequently shares risky or inappropriate jokes publicly.
  • A student deletes posts often, but screenshots of past arguments still circulate.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Describe the digital footprint this person is creating.
  3. Explain how this footprint could affect their reputation.
  4. Describe one change that would improve their digital footprint.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why digital footprints matter.

⚠️ Reminder: Your digital footprint follows you-even when you think it won't.
