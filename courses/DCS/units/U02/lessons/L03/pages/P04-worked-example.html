Worked Example

👀 Read the sample scenario and observe how digital footprints affect reputation.

Scenario: A student applies for a summer job. The employer searches their name and finds several public posts where the student used rude language and argued aggressively with others.

How this affects reputation:

  • The employer may see the student as unprofessional.
  • The posts suggest poor communication skills.
  • The student appears unreliable or disrespectful.

Better digital footprint choices:

  • Posting achievements, hobbies, or positive interactions.
  • Keeping arguments private or offline.
  • Using respectful language in public spaces.

Why this works:

  • It shows how online behavior affects real-world opportunities.
  • It demonstrates the importance of long-term thinking.
  • It models how to build a positive reputation online.
