Concept - Digital Footprints & Reputation

Every online action-posts, comments, likes, searches, messages-creates a digital footprint. Even if you delete something, screenshots, archives, or copies may still exist.

Your digital footprint shapes your online reputation. This reputation can influence opportunities such as:

  • school activities
  • jobs or internships
  • scholarships
  • relationships and trust

People often judge your character based on what they find online. A positive footprint shows responsibility, kindness, and maturity. A negative footprint can create long-term consequences.

🧠 Think: What would someone assume about you if they only saw your last 10 posts?

⚠️ Common mistake: Believing that "only friends" will ever see your posts or that deleted content disappears forever.
