Guided Practice

Practice analyzing how online identity is formed. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A profile mostly shows funny memes, but many of them insult certain groups or people.
  • A student rarely posts, but when they do, it is always about being "bored" or "hating everything."
  • Someone comments supportive messages on friends' posts but argues aggressively with strangers.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Describe what kind of online identity this person appears to have based on their behavior.
  3. Explain how others might perceive this person.
  4. Describe one change that would move their online identity in a more positive direction.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why it matters how your online identity is perceived.

⚠️ Reminder: People can only judge your online identity based on what they see, not what you meant.
