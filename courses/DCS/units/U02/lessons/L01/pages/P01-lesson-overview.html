Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: your online identity is how you appear to others based on what you do, say, and share online. Students learn how posts, comments, usernames, photos, and interactions shape how others see them in digital spaces.

A strong digital citizen is aware of how their online identity is formed and makes choices that reflect who they truly want to be. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of learning and engagement.
