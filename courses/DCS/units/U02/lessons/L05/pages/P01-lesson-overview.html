Lesson Overview

This lesson is the Unit 02 Assessment. Students apply everything learned about online identity, personal information, digital footprints, reputation, and smart posting.

Students will analyze a multi‑part scenario and answer questions that assess their understanding of safe, responsible, and reputation‑protecting digital behavior. This assessment includes multiple‑choice, true/false, and scenario‑based reasoning questions.

Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your assessment results as evidence of mastery for Unit 02.
