Concept – Unit Assessment Overview

This assessment measures your understanding of all concepts from Unit 02. You will apply your knowledge to a realistic digital scenario involving identity, privacy, reputation, and posting choices.

The assessment includes:

  • Multiple‑choice questions about key vocabulary and concepts.
  • True/false questions about safe and responsible posting.
  • Scenario‑based reasoning where you choose the safest and most responsible response.

Your goal is to demonstrate mastery by identifying safe, ethical, and reputation‑protecting choices in digital environments.

🧠 Think: How would a responsible digital citizen respond in a situation where a post could harm someone’s reputation or reveal personal information?

⚠️ Common mistake: Choosing the “funny” or impulsive response instead of the safest and most responsible one.
