Worked Example

👀 Review this example of a scenario‑based assessment question.

Scenario: A student posts a “funny” video of their friend tripping in the hallway. The friend asks them to delete it, but the video has already been shared by others.

Question: What is the most responsible response?

Correct answer:

  • Delete the video immediately, apologize to the friend, and ask others to remove their copies.

Why this is correct:

  • It respects the friend’s privacy and dignity.
  • It reduces further harm to the friend’s reputation.
  • It models responsible posting and accountability.
