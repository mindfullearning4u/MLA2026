Guided Practice

Practice applying Unit 01 concepts to a scenario. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student is excluded from an online group because they only have a phone.
  • A classmate misreads your message and becomes upset.
  • Someone posts a rude comment about your work in a shared space.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the main issue (access, communication, conflict, or decision-making).
  3. Explain why the situation is unsafe, unfair, or disrespectful.
  4. Describe the most responsible digital citizenship response.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why responsible choices matter in digital spaces.

⚠️ Reminder: The assessment will require you to apply these same skills.
