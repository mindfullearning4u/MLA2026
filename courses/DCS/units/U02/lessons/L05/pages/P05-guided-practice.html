Guided Practice

Practice applying Unit 02 concepts to a scenario. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • A student posts a joke about a classmate that spreads quickly and embarrasses them.
  • Someone shares a photo that accidentally reveals personal information in the background.
  • A student’s old negative posts resurface and affect their reputation.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the main issue (identity, privacy, footprint, reputation, or posting choice).
  3. Explain why the situation is unsafe, unkind, or harmful.
  4. Describe the most responsible digital citizenship response.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why responsible posting matters.

⚠️ Reminder: The assessment will require you to apply these same skills.
