🎯 Lesson Checkpoint – Unit Assessment Readiness
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply Unit 02 concepts to a scenario before completing the assessment.

Important: You must choose ONE of the three scenarios from the guided practice:

  • A student posts a joke about a classmate that spreads quickly and embarrasses them.
  • Someone shares a photo that accidentally reveals personal information in the background.
  • A student’s old negative posts resurface and affect their reputation.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected (must match one of the three options).
  • The main issue (identity, privacy, footprint, reputation, or posting choice).
  • An explanation of why the situation is unsafe, unkind, or harmful.
  • A revised version of the situation showing the most responsible response.
  • One sentence explaining why responsible posting matters.

If the scenario is not one of the three provided options, or if the responsible response is unclear or unsafe, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
