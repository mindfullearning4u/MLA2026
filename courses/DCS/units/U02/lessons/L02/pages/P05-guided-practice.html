Guided Practice

Practice identifying whether a digital space is inclusive and accessible. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • An online study group only meets on video calls, and some members have slow internet.
  • A gaming community uses small text and no captions, making it hard for some players to participate.
  • A class discussion board assumes everyone has a laptop, even though several students only have phones.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify one access barrier in the situation.
  3. Explain why the situation is not fully inclusive.
  4. Rewrite the situation to show a more inclusive and accessible digital choice.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why digital inclusion matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Inclusive digital spaces consider different devices, internet speeds, abilities, and digital experiences.
