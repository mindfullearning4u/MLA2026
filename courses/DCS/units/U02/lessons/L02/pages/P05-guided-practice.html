Guided Practice

Practice identifying personal vs. public information and making safe posting choices. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • A student posts a photo of their school ID badge.
  • Someone shares a screenshot of a group chat that includes full names and phone numbers.
  • A friend posts a selfie in front of their house with the street sign visible.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify what personal information is being shared.
  3. Explain why sharing this information is unsafe.
  4. Rewrite the situation to show a safer posting choice.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why oversharing is risky.

⚠️ Reminder: Even small details can reveal more than you think.
