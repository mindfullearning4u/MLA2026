💡Worked Example

💡Read the sample scenario and observe how personal information can be exposed.

Scenario: A student posts a picture of their new driver's license on social media with the caption "Finally got it!"

Personal information exposed:

  • Full name
  • Address
  • Date of birth
  • ID number

Why this is unsafe:

  • Strangers could use this information for identity theft.
  • The photo could be saved or shared without permission.
  • Deleting the post does not remove copies others may have taken.

Safer choice:

  • Share a photo of the car keys or a celebration selfie instead.
  • Avoid posting documents, IDs, or anything with personal details.

✅Why this works:

  • It protects sensitive information.
  • It still allows the student to celebrate safely.
  • It models responsible posting behavior.
