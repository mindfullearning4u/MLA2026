Unit 02 Lesson 2 - Personal vs. Public Information

📓 Write these required notes in your notebook:

  • Personal information: details that identify you or can be used to find, contact, or impersonate you.
  • Public information: information that is safe to share widely and does not put you at risk.
  • Oversharing: posting too much personal information online.
  • Privacy choices: decisions you make about what to share, where to share it, and who can see it.
  • Key idea: once information is posted online, you lose control over where it goes.
