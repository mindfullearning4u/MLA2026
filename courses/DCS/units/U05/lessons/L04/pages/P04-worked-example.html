Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe responsible decision-making.

Scenario: A student uses an AI tool to generate a full assignment and submits it as their own work.

Unethical and risky behavior:

  • Submitting AI-generated work without disclosure.
  • Violating academic integrity policies.
  • Ignoring responsibility for the final submission.

Ethical and responsible behavior:

  • Using AI as a study or brainstorming tool.
  • Creating original work based on understanding.
  • Following school policies regarding AI use.

Why this works:

  • It respects academic and legal expectations.
  • It maintains honesty and accountability.
  • It models responsible technology use.
