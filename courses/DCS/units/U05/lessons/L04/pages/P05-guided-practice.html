Guided Practice

Practice identifying conflict triggers and choosing safe, ethical responses. Read the situations below and complete the required notebook work.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • Someone replies “lol you’re clueless” to your comment in a group chat.
  • A classmate posts your message out of context to make you look bad.
  • A friend becomes angry when you don’t respond immediately and sends multiple upset messages.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify the conflict trigger.
  3. Explain why the situation could escalate.
  4. Describe a safe, responsible response (de-escalate, disengage, or report).
  5. Write one sentence explaining why responsible decision-making matters online.

⚠️ Reminder: Not every message deserves a response—sometimes the safest choice is to disengage.
