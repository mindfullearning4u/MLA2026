Guided Practice

Practice evaluating digital actions involving laws, consequences, and AI use.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • Using AI to complete an assignment without permission.
  • Sharing private digital information publicly.
  • Ignoring platform rules when using digital tools.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify the digital law or rule involved.
  3. Explain the possible consequences of the action.
  4. Describe one ethical alternative behavior.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why responsible technology use matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Digital freedom comes with responsibility and accountability.
