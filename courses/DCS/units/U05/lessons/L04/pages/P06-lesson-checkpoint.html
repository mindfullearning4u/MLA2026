🎯 Lesson Checkpoint - Digital Laws, Consequences & Ethical Use of AI
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply legal awareness and ethical judgment to digital situations.

Important: You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:

  • Using AI to complete an assignment without permission.
  • Sharing private digital information publicly.
  • Ignoring platform rules when using digital tools.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected.
  • The digital law, rule, or policy involved.
  • An explanation of the possible consequences.
  • One ethical alternative action.
  • One sentence explaining why ethical technology use matters.

If the law, consequence, or ethical alternative is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
