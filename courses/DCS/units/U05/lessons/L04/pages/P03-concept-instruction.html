Concept - Digital Laws, Consequences & Ethical Use of AI

Digital laws exist to protect individuals, creators, and communities in online spaces. These laws address issues such as privacy, copyright, harassment, and misuse of technology.

Violating digital laws can result in serious consequences, including academic penalties, account restrictions, or legal action. Understanding these consequences helps individuals make responsible choices.

Artificial intelligence tools can support learning and productivity, but they must be used ethically. Ethical AI use includes transparency, originality, and following rules set by schools, organizations, or platforms.

🧠 Think: Why does powerful technology require stronger ethical responsibility?

⚠️ Common mistake: Assuming that using AI removes personal responsibility for the work produced.
