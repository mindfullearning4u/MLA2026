Lesson Overview

This lesson teaches one concept: digital actions are governed by laws, consequences, and ethical expectations, including the responsible use of artificial intelligence. Students learn how digital laws apply to online behavior and how ethical judgment is required when using AI tools.

A strong digital citizen understands that technology does not remove responsibility. Choices made online - including how AI is used - carry legal, academic, and ethical consequences. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of responsible understanding and reasoning.
