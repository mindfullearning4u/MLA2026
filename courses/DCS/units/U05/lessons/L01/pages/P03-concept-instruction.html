Concept – Digital Ethics & Responsible Behavior

Digital ethics involve making choices that are fair, respectful, and responsible when using technology. These choices affect not only the individual but also other users and digital communities.

Responsible behavior includes thinking before posting, respecting others’ rights, and following rules and expectations in digital spaces. Ethical decision-making helps prevent harm and promotes trust online.

Understanding digital ethics prepares individuals to navigate complex situations where rules may not always be clear.

🧠 Think: How can one online decision affect many people?

⚠️ Common mistake: Believing that actions online do not have real-world consequences.
