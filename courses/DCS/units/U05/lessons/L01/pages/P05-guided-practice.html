Guided Practice

Practice applying digital ethics to real-world online situations.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • Sharing someone else’s content without permission.
  • Posting a comment that could embarrass another person.
  • Using digital tools in ways that break stated rules.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify the ethical issue involved.
  3. Explain why the situation requires responsible decision-making.
  4. Describe one ethical choice that could be made.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why ethical behavior matters online.

⚠️ Reminder: Ethical choices online protect individuals and communities.
