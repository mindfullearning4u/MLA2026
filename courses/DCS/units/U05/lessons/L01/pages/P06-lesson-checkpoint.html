🎯 Lesson Checkpoint - Digital Ethics & Responsible Behavior
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply ethical reasoning to digital situations.

Important: You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:

  • Sharing someone else's content without permission.
  • Posting a comment that could embarrass another person.
  • Using digital tools in ways that break stated rules.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected.
  • The ethical issue involved.
  • An explanation of why the situation requires responsible behavior.
  • One ethical decision that could be made.
  • One sentence explaining why digital ethics matter.

If the ethical issue or reasoning is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
