Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe ethical decision-making.

Scenario: A student finds a private message meant for someone else. The message contains personal information.

Unethical response:

  • Sharing the message with others.
  • Posting the information publicly.

Ethical response:

  • Respecting privacy.
  • Not sharing the information.
  • Informing the sender if appropriate.

Why this works:

  • It respects privacy and trust.
  • It considers potential harm.
  • It demonstrates responsible digital behavior.
