Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe ethical reasoning in action.

Scenario: A student is behind on an assignment and considers using AI to generate the entire project without permission.

Unethical reasoning:

  • It saves time and reduces stress.
  • The work will probably not be detected.

Ethical reasoning:

  • The work would not represent the student's learning.
  • It violates academic integrity rules.
  • The student is responsible for the submission, regardless of the tool used.

Why this works:

  • It evaluates consequences, not convenience.
  • It prioritizes integrity over shortcuts.
  • It demonstrates responsible digital judgment.
