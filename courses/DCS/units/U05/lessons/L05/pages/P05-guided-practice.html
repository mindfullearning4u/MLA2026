Guided Practice

Practice evaluating complex digital ethics situations that require judgment and responsibility.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE scenario:

  • Using AI-generated content for an assignment without permission.
  • Sharing copyrighted material in a group project.
  • Posting private or sensitive information about another person.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the scenario you selected.
  2. Identify the ethical issue involved.
  3. Explain the potential consequences of the action.
  4. Describe one responsible and ethical alternative.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why ethical digital behavior matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Ethical decisions online reflect character and responsibility.
