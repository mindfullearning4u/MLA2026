🎯 Unit 05 Checkpoint - Digital Ethics Scenario
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply ethical judgment to a complex digital situation.

Important: You must choose ONE of the guided practice scenarios:

  • Using AI-generated content for an assignment without permission.
  • Sharing copyrighted material in a group project.
  • Posting private or sensitive information about another person.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The scenario you selected.
  • The ethical issue involved.
  • An explanation of potential consequences.
  • One responsible and ethical alternative action.
  • One sentence explaining why digital ethics matter.

If ethical reasoning is unclear, consequences are not considered, or the alternative is not responsible, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
