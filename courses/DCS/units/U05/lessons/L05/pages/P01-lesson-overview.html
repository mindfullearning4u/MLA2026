Lesson Overview

This lesson brings together everything you have learned about digital ethics, responsibility, laws, and ethical technology use. You will evaluate a realistic digital scenario that requires careful judgment, ethical reasoning, and responsible decision-making.

A strong digital citizen does not rely on impulse or convenience. Instead, they consider consequences, follow ethical standards, and make choices that protect themselves and others. Your Teacher of Record (TOR) will review your notebook as evidence of mastery and thoughtful reasoning.
