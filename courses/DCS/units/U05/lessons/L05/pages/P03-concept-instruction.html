Concept – Unit Assessment Overview

This assessment measures your understanding of all concepts from Unit 01. You will apply your knowledge to a realistic digital scenario that includes issues related to access, communication, conflict, and responsible decision-making.

The assessment includes:

  • Multiple-choice questions about key vocabulary and concepts.
  • True/false questions about safe and ethical digital behavior.
  • Scenario-based reasoning where you choose the safest and most responsible response.

Your goal is to demonstrate mastery by identifying safe, respectful, and ethical choices in digital environments.

🧠 Think: How would a responsible digital citizen respond in a situation where someone is excluded, misunderstood, or trying to start conflict?

⚠️ Common mistake: Choosing the “fast” or emotional response instead of the safest and most ethical one.
