Concept - Ethical Judgment & Digital Responsibility

Ethical digital decision-making often involves situations where rules, convenience, and pressure conflict. In these moments, individuals must evaluate consequences, responsibilities, and ethical expectations.

Responsible digital citizens consider how their actions affect others, follow rules and laws, and choose integrity over shortcuts. This applies to content use, sharing information, and the use of digital tools such as artificial intelligence.

Strong ethical judgment protects trust, learning, and community safety in digital spaces.

🧠 Think: Why is it sometimes harder to make ethical choices online than offline?

⚠️ Common mistake: Justifying unethical choices because "everyone does it" or because the risk feels low.
