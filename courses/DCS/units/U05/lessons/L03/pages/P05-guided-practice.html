Guided Practice

Practice identifying plagiarism and applying academic integrity principles.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • Copying text from a website into an assignment.
  • Paraphrasing a source without credit.
  • Sharing completed work with a friend to submit.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify whether plagiarism or ethical sharing is involved.
  3. Explain why the situation violates or supports academic integrity.
  4. Describe one ethical alternative.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why integrity matters.

⚠️ Reminder: Integrity reflects who you are, not just what you submit.
