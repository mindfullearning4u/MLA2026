Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe ethical academic behavior.

Scenario: A student finds a paragraph online that perfectly explains their topic and copies it into their assignment.

Unethical approach:

  • Copying the paragraph word-for-word.
  • Submitting it as original work.

Ethical approach:

  • Writing the idea in their own words.
  • Citing the original source if required.
  • Using the source to support—not replace—their own thinking.

Why this works:

  • It demonstrates honesty.
  • It respects intellectual effort.
  • It supports authentic learning.
