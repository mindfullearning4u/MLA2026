🎯 Lesson Checkpoint – Plagiarism, Academic Integrity & Ethical Sharing
This checkpoint verifies your ability to identify plagiarism and apply ethical sharing practices.

Important: You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:

  • Copying text from a website into an assignment.
  • Paraphrasing a source without credit.
  • Sharing completed work with a friend to submit.

Checkpoint Submission Requirements (Required):

  • The situation you selected.
  • Whether plagiarism or ethical sharing is involved.
  • An explanation of how academic integrity is affected.
  • One ethical alternative action.
  • One sentence explaining why academic integrity matters.

If plagiarism is misunderstood or the ethical alternative is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.
