Concept – Copyright, Fair Use & Creative Rights

Copyright protects original works such as writing, music, images, videos, and artwork. The creator automatically owns the rights to their work unless they give permission for others to use it.

Fair use allows limited use of copyrighted material in specific situations, such as education or commentary. However, fair use does not mean unlimited copying or reposting.

Giving proper attribution respects the creator’s effort and helps maintain trust and integrity in digital spaces.

🧠 Think: Why do creators deserve control over how their work is used?

⚠️ Common mistake: Believing that anything found online is free to use.
