Lesson 2 Checkpoint - Copyright, Fair Use & Creative Rights
This checkpoint verifies your ability to apply copyright and fair use principles responsibly.

You must choose ONE of the guided practice situations:
• Using an online image in a school project.
• Sharing a song clip in a video.
• Reposting someone's artwork on social media.

Required evidence:
• The situation you selected.
• The copyrighted material involved.
• An explanation of whether fair use applies.
• One responsible action that should be taken.
• One sentence explaining why respecting creative rights matters.

Accepted submission formats:
• Clear photo(s) of handwritten notebook pages
• PDF file
• Typed document

Your Teacher of Record will review this submission for completion and accuracy.

If fair use is misunderstood or the responsible action is unclear, the checkpoint is not met and must be revised.

This checkpoint contributes to unit mastery and progression.
