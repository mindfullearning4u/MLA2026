Guided Practice

Practice applying copyright and fair use principles to common digital situations.

📓 Notebook Accountability:
Your Teacher of Record will check your notebook for completion of this guided practice. Incomplete or missing work will require revision before you may proceed.

Choose ONE situation:

  • Using an online image in a school project.
  • Sharing a song clip in a video.
  • Reposting someone’s artwork on social media.

📓 Notebook Directions (Required):

  1. Write the situation you selected.
  2. Identify the copyrighted material involved.
  3. Explain whether fair use may apply.
  4. Describe one responsible action the user should take.
  5. Write one sentence explaining why creative rights matter.

⚠️ Reminder: Ethical use of content respects both law and creativity.
