Worked Example

👀 Read the scenario and observe responsible use of creative content.

Scenario: A student wants to include an image they found online in a school presentation.

Improper use:

  • Copying the image without permission or credit.
  • Assuming school use automatically makes it acceptable.

Responsible use:

  • Checking whether the image is free to use.
  • Confirming fair use applies.
  • Giving proper attribution to the creator.

Why this works:

  • It respects legal protections.
  • It honors creative effort.
  • It models ethical digital behavior.
